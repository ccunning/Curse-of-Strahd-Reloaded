# Act I: Into the Mists
<div class="subtitle"><em>For 2nd to 3rd-level characters.</em></div>
In this act, the players are lured into the haunted [[Factions/Death House|Death House]], which mystically transports them into the land of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]. As the players gain their bearings, they travel to [[Places/Village of Barovia|the village of Barovia]], where they meet burgomaster [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark Kolyanovich]] and his sister, [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]], and learn about the mysterious Strahd von Zarovich, a vampire that has recently awoken to plague the Barovian valley. 

The players are asked to escort [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] to the nearby town of Vallaki for safety; if the players agree, Ireena also asks them to assist in the burial of her late father, [[NPCs/Burgomaster Kolyan Indirovich|Burgomaster Kolyan Indirovich]]. While at the local church, the players meet [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]], a **[[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]]** at war with his bloodthirsty nature, and have an opportunity to reconcile him with his father, the priest [[NPCs/Father Donavich|Donavich]].

Upon receiving a warning and invitation sent in a dream from the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] seer [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]], the players depart the village of Barovia for a [[Places/Vistani Encampment|Vistani encampment]] at nearby [[Places/Tser Pool Encampment|Tser Pool]]. Following a brief encounter with Strahd himself, the players receive a mystical [[Items/Tarokka reading|Tarokka reading]] from [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]], who foretells the location of three mystical artifacts—and a companion—who will help them defeat Strahd.

As the players travel to Madam Eva's camp and the nearby town of Vallaki, they encounter a number of characters with ties to possible future adventures, as well as some adversarial encounters that will test their tactics, coordination, and combat skill.

Upon their arrival in Vallaki, the players can find shelter at the [[Places/Blue Water Inn|Blue Water Inn]], and—if she is with them—help Ireena seek shelter at St. Andral’s Church. The act ends when the players take their first long rest in Vallaki.

# Act II: The Shadowed Town
<div class="subtitle"><em>For characters of 4th level and higher.</em></div>
In this act, the players are left to explore the shadowed town of Vallaki—a Barovian settlement that has fallen to tension and conflict in the wake of Strahd's awakening.

As the players seek to complete lingering obligations—escorting Ireena to [[Places/St. Andral's Church|St. Andral's Church]], purchasing and delivering a toy for the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] child [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]], and uncovering the *[[Items/The Tome of Strahd|Tome of Strahd]]*—they meet new allies and enemies, including [[NPCs/Father Lucian Petrovich|Father Lucian]], the priest of St. Andral's Church; [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]], Baron Vallakovich's brutal enforcer; and [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]], the Baron's Strahd-loyalist rival.

At St. Andral's Church, the players will be tasked with recovering the stolen bones of St. Andral in order to protect the church's holy protection. At the nearby [[Places/Vistani Encampment|Vistani encampment]], the players will have an opportunity to prove themselves heroes by finding and rescuing [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]] following her mysterious disappearance—and receive a mysterious prophecy if they do. And at [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]], the ancestral home of [[Factions/House Wachter|House Wachter]], the players will be given a choice: slay Izek Strazni and allow Lady Wachter to seize power—or do nothing, and allow the Baron's mad reign to continue.

Meanwhile, one of the players begins to experience haunting apparitions, leading them to discover the tragic fate of Stella Wachter's soul—and an opportunity to restore her broken mind. Simultaneously, a nocturnal killer haunts Vallaki's streets, secretly working to drive the disguised vampire hunter Dr. Rudolph van Richten out of hiding.

Through it all, the players will have an opportunity to seek out clues leading to the hidden location of the *Tome of Strahd*, which—depending on the Tarokka reading they received—may be hidden in the [[Places/Baron's Mansion|Baron's mansion]] or in [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]]. And as the date of the Festival of the Blazing Sun approaches, Izek Strazni and Ireena Kolyana grow closer to a fateful, yet tragic encounter.

Act II ends on the morning after the players' first full moon in Barovia, which takes place on the night of the sixth full day after they first arrive in Vallaki. However, the players can still complete unresolved arcs from this act even after Act III begins—assuming, of course, that those arcs are still possible to complete.

# Act III: The Broken Land
<div class="subtitle"><em>For characters of 5th level and higher.</em></div>

In this act, the players receive a plea from [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin Martikov]] to investigate [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] winery—and an invitation from Strahd to dine at Castle Ravenloft.

If the players agree to [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin]]'s request in [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]], they are joined by [[NPCs/Muriel Vinshaw|Muriel Vinshaw]]—the secret identity of the disguised **wereraven** that they rescued near Tser Pool. As the players work to free the winery from the scourge of druids and blights that have invaded it, [[NPCs/Muriel Vinshaw|Muriel]] relays an excerpt of Strahd's foretelling to [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian Martikov]], an elder **wereraven** and the leader of [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].

Upon freeing the winery from the druids' clutches, [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian]] asks them to journey to [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] to the south, where the druids are plotting a dark ritual that threatens the vineyard's destruction. Once the players have recovered the winery's stolen gemstone and returned it to Davian [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]], they can learn the true nature of [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]] and gain Muriel's knowledge of Strahd's cryptic fortunetelling.

Should the players accept Strahd's invitation to dine at Ravenloft, they will have one week to explore the valley before the occasion begins. As they do, they might recruit the monster hunter [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda d'Avenir]] from Krezk in [[Arc K - The Fallen Abbey]], investigate the lost *[[Items/Holy Symbol of Ravenloft|Holy Symbol of Ravenloft]]* in [[Arc L - The Den of Wolves]], or visit the haunted mansion of [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] in [[Arc M - The Dragon's Manor]].

In Krezk, the players will learn that the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia]] has fallen into madness, and that the key to cleansing his divine spirit lies amongst the crypts of Castle Ravenloft. In the [[Places/Werewolf Den|Werewolf Den]], the players will receive an offer from [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]], a **werewolf** priestess of [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]]: To receive the *Holy Symbol*, they must first rescue her mate from the castle [[Places/Dungeons|dungeons]]. At Argynvostholt, the players receive a solemn missive from the spirit of the silver dragon [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]: return his stolen skull from the desecrated grounds of the castle cellar and restore it its rightful tomb. 

On the night of [[Arc O - Dinner with the Devil]], the players are escorted by Strahd's [[Items/Black Carriage|black carriage]] to the castle, where they will meet Strahd's servants, brides, and consorts. After dining with Strahd and touring the castle, the players will learn that Strahd will be away from the castle the following night, providing the players free reign to infiltrate the keep. Not long thereafter, however, a bolt of lightning disables the drawbridge, forcing them to remain as Strahd's guests until morning—and survive an old and vengeful spirit in the process.

Should the players survive, they are free to return the following night for [[Arc P - Ravenloft Heist]], where they must battle Strahd's brides and servants to fulfill their assorted quests. Should they escape, they can then return to purify the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]], defeat the werewolf alpha [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril Stoyanovich]], and light the radiant [[Places/Beacon of Argynvostholt|beacon of Argynvostholt]].

Act III ends when the players recruit the dusk elf [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir]] to journey to [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]]. When the act ends, the **[[NPCs/Tree Blight|tree blight]]** [[NPCs/Wintersplinter|Wintersplinter]] destroys [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] winery unless the players stopped the druids' ritual in [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]].

# Act IV: Secrets of the Ancient
<div class="subtitle"><em>For characters of 8th level and higher.</em></div>

Throughout this act, and until the players light the *[[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]]*, Strahd—having now assumed his [[Strahd von Zarovich#The Tyrant|Tyrant persona]] following the players' heist of Castle Ravenloft—appears nightly to torment them in [[Arc R - Trials of the Mountain]], seeking to test their ambition, cunning, power, and capacity for cruelty.

Meanwhile, the players journey with the dusk elf [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir Velikov]] to the Amber Temple, traveling along [[Places/Tsolenka Pass|Tsolenka Pass]] as they ascend [[Places/Mount Ghakis|Mount Ghakis]] in [[Arc S - A Sword of Sunlight]]. As they do, they can befriend the [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] of [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]], including [[NPCs/Elder Burebis|Elder Burebis]], an ancient **[[NPCs/Storm Giant|storm giant]]** and devotee of the Ladies Three. From Burebis, the players can learn of [[Places/The Three Fanes|the three Fanes]] of Barovia, and of the profane knowledge that Strahd took from the Amber Temple to desecrate them.

After overcoming the trials of Mount Ghakis's slopes, the **giant goat** [[NPCs/Sangzor|Sangzor]], multiple fiends corrupted by the Amber Temple, and the **[[NPCs/Roc|roc]]** of Mount Ghakis itself, the players can finally arrive at their destination. Within the Amber Temple, the players can obtain the broken hilt of the *Sunsword*, as well as the arcane secrets that Strahd used to corrupt the Barovian Fanes—and which can, in turn, be used to restore them.

Upon restoring the memory of the **[[NPCs/Lich|lich]]** [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]] and sharing with him the schematics found in the *Tome of Strahd*, the players can also learn Strahd's ultimate plan: to channel the power of the Fanes into the indestructible [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|Heart of Sorrow]] within Castle Ravenloft, which Strahd will use to tear open the Mists and escape. The players will also learn that Strahd plans to consume the souls of all Barovia in order to power his audacious scheme, and that he cannot ever truly be killed unless severed from the Fanes' power.

When the players have recovered the broken hilt of the *Sunsword*, they learn that Strahd is hunting Dr. Rudolph van Richten for use in his fell ritual, and must aid him and the Vistana [[NPCs/Arturi Radanavich|Arturi Radanavich]] to lift both men's curses in [[Arc T - The Three Fanes]]. When dawn comes, however, the players learn that the swamp witch [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] has kidnapped the Martikovs of the Wizard of Wines winery, forcing them to travel to [[Places/Berez|Berez]] to defeat her. 

The players must use the three enchanted gems of the Wizard of Wines winery and three ancient artifacts retrieved from [[Items/Gulthias tree|the Gulthias Tree]] of Yester Hill to reconsecrate the [[Items/Three Fanes|three Fanes]], even as Strahd musters his forces to defeat them. Act IV ends when the players reconsecrate all [[Items/Three Fanes|three Fanes]].
# Act V: The Curse of Strahd
<div class="subtitle"><em>For characters of 9th level and higher.</em></div>
In this act, soon after the last Fane is restored, Strahd's masterstroke unfolds. By releasing the stored energy bound within [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|the Heart of Sorrow]], Strahd plunges Barovia into an eternal night—and turns the valley itself into desecrated ground. Meanwhile, [[Places/Whispering Wall|the Whispering Wall]]—resonating with the energies of the Heart of Sorrow—relocates to the surface of [[Places/Lake Zarovich|Lake Zarovich]], where its deadly tendrils threaten to extinguish all life in Vallaki in [[Arc U - Dreams of Dawn]].

Even once [[Places/Whispering Wall|the Whispering Wall]] is quelled, however, a greater danger emerges in [[Arc U - Dreams of Dawn]]. As an undead apocalypse threatens to overwhelm the Barovian people, the players must journey to Castle Ravenloft to face Strahd at the Heart of Sorrow. As the final battle unfolds, the Heart of Sorrow shows visions of Strahd's undead siege from across Barovia—and of the heroic resistance shown by the allies and friends that the players have made across their journey:

***Village of Barovia.*** If the players escorted Ireena to Vallaki, reconciled Doru with [[NPCs/Father Donavich|Father Donavich]], befriended Dr. Rudolph van Richten in Vallaki, and inspired Ireena to reject Sergei's invitation, the village of Barovia is saved by the leadership and prowess of [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark Kolyanovich]], Doru and Donavich, Dr. Van Richten and the **ghost** [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus van Richten]], and the spirit of Sergei von Zarovich.

***Town of Vallaki.*** If the players restored the soul of Stella Wachter, aided Lady Fiona Wachter in defeating Izek Strazni, located the missing [[NPCs/Vistana Arabelle|Vistana Arabelle]], recovered the stolen bones of St. Andral, and rescued the [[Places/Blue Water Inn|Blue Water Inn]] from Baba Lysaga, the town of Vallaki is saved by the power and foresight of Lady Fiona Wachter and her cult; the young **mage** [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor Vallakovich]]; the Vistani [[NPCs/Luvash|Luvash]], [[NPCs/Arrigal|Arrigal]], and Arabelle; the [[Factions/Dusk Elves|dusk elves]]; the priest [[NPCs/Father Lucian Petrovich|Father Lucian Petrovich]]; and the **wereravens** Urwin [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]] and [[NPCs/Danika Dorakova|Danika Dorakova]].

***[[Places/Village of Krezk|Village of Krezk]].*** If the players rescued [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya Krezkov]] from the Werewolf Den, rescued [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil Toranescu]] from the dungeons of Castle Ravenloft, and restored the Abbot's divine grace, the [[Places/Village of Krezk|village of Krezk]] is saved by the might and savagery of the redeemed Abbot, the Belview **mongrelfolk**, the Krezkov **[[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]]**, and Emil's new **werewolf** pack.

***Wizard of Wines.*** If the players saved the Wizard of Wines from the druids and [[NPCs/Wintersplinter|Wintersplinter]], defeated Baba Lysaga, and relit the beacon of Argynvostholt, the winery is saved by the combined efforts of the Keepers of the Feather and the spirits of the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]].

***Settlement of [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]].*** If the players aided the [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] within the Amber Temple and reconsecrated the [[Places/Fanes of Barovia|Fanes of Barovia]], the settlement of Soldav is saved by the sudden arrival of the Mountain Folk warrior Helwa, the chieftain [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] and his warband of spirits, the bestial spirits of the [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]], and the **[[NPCs/Roc|roc]]** of Mount Ghakis itself.
