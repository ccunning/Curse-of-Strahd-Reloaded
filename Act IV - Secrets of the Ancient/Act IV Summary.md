In this act, which begins once the players have lit the [[Places/Beacon of Argynvostholt|beacon of Argynvostholt]] in [[Arc Q - A Shining Beacon]], the players must journey to the peak of Mt. Ghakis in order to find the long-lost *[[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]]* and uncover the secrets of the [[Places/Fanes of Barovia|Fanes of Barovia]]. 

Upon recruiting the dusk elf [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir Velikov]] and learning of his interest in [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]], the players set off along [[Places/Tsolenka Pass|Tsolenka Pass]] in [[Arc R - Trials of the Mountain]], battling monsters, vengeful foes, and the mountain itself in order to reach their destinations. As they do, they are haunted each night by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd von Zarovich]], who seeks to torment them with cruel, painful games. 

The players' only respite lies in the hidden [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] village of [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]]. There, the ancient **[[NPCs/Storm Giant|storm giant]]** Burebis shares that only the Ladies Three know how the Fanes may be rescued from [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s corruption—but that the secret to contacting their spirits lies in the depths of [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] itself.

In [[Arc S - A Sword of Sunlight]], the players delve into the darkness of the Amber Temple, seeking to resist its shadows and corruption long enough to obtain the hidden hilt of the *[[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]]*, which [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina Velikovna]] sealed long ago within the amber sarcophagus of [[NPCs/The Vampyr|the Vampyr]]. Upon retrieving the hilt and defeating [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina]]'s wrathful spirit, the players must empower the broken sword by conjuring the spirit of Sergei von Zarovich from the [[Places/Mists|mists]].

After learning how to contact the spirits of the Ladies Three from the ghost [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]], the players return to [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]] in [[Arc T - The Three Fanes]]. There, the spirits of the [[NPCs/Weaver|Weaver]] and [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]] can share that [[Places/The Three Fanes|the three Fanes]] may be reconsecrated by obtaining the three enchanted gems of [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] winery and reigniting the magic that lies within. 

This act ends when the players descend Mt. Ghakis and return to the valley below.
