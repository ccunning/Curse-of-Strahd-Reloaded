# Stone Crest

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- It emits a low hum and smells of pine needles and earthy soil, growing stronger as it points towards [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Recognized by [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] in [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]] or [[NPCs/Elder Burebis|Elder Burebis]] in [[Arc R - Trials of the Mountain]].
- Once belonged to a [[Factions/First Folk|First Folk]] spiritual leader, dividing the people into Forest and [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]].
- Emits scent of pine needles and earthy soil upon sensing [[Factions/Death House|Death House]], growing stronger near it.
- A flat, round stone engraved with a three-pointed star; glimmers emerald on new moon.
- Depicts symbols of the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]], [[NPCs/Weaver|Weaver]], and [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]]—the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]].
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- In [[Arc R - Trials of the Mountain]], it hums and releases scent when approaching [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]].
- Can lead to Shrine of the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]] within [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]]’s territory and is connected to the Fanes.
