# Sunrise Medallion

> **item** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Once belonged to Sergei von Zarovich, brother of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and acolyte of the Church of the Morninglord.
- Glitters like gold under sunrise; evokes reverence and peace on touch by Good-aligned creatures.
- Used in conjunction during the narrative involving the retrieval of the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] hilt.
- It shines with a warm golden glow and radiates light in the direction of [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- One is a medallion glowing with golden light, the other a [[Items/Broken Blade|broken blade]] humming.
- In [[Arc D - St. Andral's Feast]], it glows in direction of church’s doors.
- They guide the wielder towards powerful relics within [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Magical artifacts that respond to each other when near a [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]].
- A bronze disc engraved with the image of a rising sun.
- Shines golden toward [[Factions/Death House|Death House]] upon sensing it.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- In [[Arc D - St. Andral's Feast]], [[NPCs/Father Donavich|Father Donavich]] or Lucian recognize and warmly greet player upon seeing the medallion.
