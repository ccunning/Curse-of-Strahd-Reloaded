# Vasilka

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- She has knowledge of her younger brother (the [[Items/Flesh Golem|flesh golem]] in the east wing) but fears the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]’s disapproval if she interacts with him.
- She learns about romance and etiquette with help from players in exchange for teaching them about mortals.
- Wants to make the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] happy, befriend her [[Items/Flesh Golem|flesh golem]] 'brother', and learn about people and the world.
- She is described as polite, withdrawn, shy, yet curious and endlessly fascinated by people and stories.
- She made a flower necklace for the golem as an act of compassion, which leads to her punishment.
- Polite, withdrawn, and well-mannered to the world but shy and curious with those she trusts.
- Possesses a gentle, innocent nature that makes her endearing but anxious around the Abbot.
- The character '[[Items/Vasilka|Vasilka]]' plays an important role in the Abbot’s plan to take control.
- The Abbot's creation and the 'younger sister' of the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]]'s mindless flesh golem.
- [[Items/Vasilka|Vasilka]] is a flesh golem created by the Abbot as a potential bride for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- It’s a physical embodiment of the threat to both [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] and the Krezkovs.
- Its nature is tied to themes of love, death, and divine punishment.
- Cowers when attacked and does not fight even to defend herself.
- It is a creation of the Abbot made with a human heart.
- He needs [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala]]'s heart to complete its power.

## DM Notes

- Vasilka’s punishment involves watching her flower necklace be burned in a fire by the golem due to its aversion to fire.
