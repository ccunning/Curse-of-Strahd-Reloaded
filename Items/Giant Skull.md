## Key Facts

- Connected to her magical powers and the power of the [[Places/Mists|Mists]].
- Crafted by [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] after defeating her rival.
- Used to travel and perform tasks in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
