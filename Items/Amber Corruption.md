# Amber Corruption

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Can be a major plot device that ties into other mechanics involving player character transformation and temptation.
- Affects players who use the dark gifts offered by amber sarcophagi in various stages of [[Items/Amber Corruption|amber corruption]].
- Players can stop the corruption using *lesser restoration* or [[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]]'s healing touch.
- Each stage of corruption brings new physical and thematic changes.
- Dark artifacts that carry power but also bring corruption.

## DM Notes

- The impact of [[Items/Amber Corruption|amber corruption]] serves as a driving theme for player choices and moral consequences throughout the setting.
