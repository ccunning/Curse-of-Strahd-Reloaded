# Sigil of the Sun

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Later became known as the *[[Items/Holy Symbol of Ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]]* through manipulation told by [[NPCs/Ismark Antonovich|Ismark Antonovich]].
- A platinum amulet shaped like the sun that is meant to empower those fighting against undead.
- Was vital in early conflicts against vampiric power and helped shape beliefs within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Passed through a succession of high priests to Sergei before becoming part of his legacy.
- Carved with symbols of light and divine power for use in preparing for a future enemy.
- Had power to resist [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s creatures before losing it in a battle at [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
- It was sought out by [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark]] for its potential in overcoming supernatural threats.
- Gained new meaning when [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] received it following the death of Sergei.
- A holy relic created by Kir, the Most High Priest after [[NPCs/Saint Andral|Saint Andral]].
- Ancient artifact discovered by [[NPCs/Livius Martikov|Livius Martikov]] and used by [[NPCs/Lugdana|Lugdana]].
- Connected to themes of faith, resistance, and divine intervention.
- Used as a symbol of religious authority during times of conflict.
- Strahd, although not possessing it, recognizes its description.
