# Khazan's Staff

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Its magic can surge through the wielder's body and cause damage if not controlled properly.
- Can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff that grants a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
- While wielding it, the user gains benefits of wearing a robe of the archmagi.
- A legendary staff requiring attunement by a sorcerer, warlock, or wizard.
- The staff is hidden in Crypt 15 inside the North [[Places/Catacombs|Catacombs]].

## DM Notes

- The arcane surge effects are detailed, potentially threatening to the user.
- The staff requires use of the magical runes inside Crypt 15.
