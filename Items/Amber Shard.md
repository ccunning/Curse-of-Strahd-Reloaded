# Amber Shard

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- A magical object around Elisabeth Durst's spectral neck, used to command the flesh mound using a DC 13 Charisma (Intimidation) check.
- If used before escaping, it may play an important role in unlocking the mystery of the house.
- Later transformed him into having a demonic limb capable of fire conjuration.
- Used to establish key transformations of character and narrative shifts.
- It glows amber light and tugs on its cord, pointing toward [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Elisabeth Durst's [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] is a key item found inside the house.
- It crumbles into dust once the players escape from [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Offered power to protect his sister but changed him profoundly.
- Found and taken by [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]] after his arm was bitten off.
- A magical relic with deep spiritual or infernal connections.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.
- Connected to Walter in its effects.

## DM Notes

- The shard’s disappearance might indicate that the true mystery of the house or the family's past is tied to more significant artifacts.
- Its power and implications will be crucial in shaping future transformations and decisions among characters.
