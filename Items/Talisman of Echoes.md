# Talisman of Echoes

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Owned by Dr. Rudolph van Richten and given to players after helping resolve [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]]'s situation.
- A magical wondrous item capable of casting gentle repose or speak with dead.
- Used in conversation with [[NPCs/Yan|Yan]] to obtain proof about [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]]’s kidnapping.
- Essential tool for revealing truths behind events tied to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Has 4 charges that recover daily.

## DM Notes

- May be used later in other storylines involving undead revelations or character backstories.
