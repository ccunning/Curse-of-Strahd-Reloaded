# Holy Symbol of Ravenkind

> **item** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- Grants powers including Dawn's Embrace (reducing damage at 0 HP), Light of Hope (curing conditions), and Sun’s Blessing (extra radiant damage to allies).
- May trigger recognition or reactions from certain enemies such as [[NPCs/Anastrasya Karelova|Anastrasya]] who knew the original wearer.
- Is a holy symbol of [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]], guarded by a curse that causes damage unless retrieved with permission.
- A powerful magical object connected with [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]] and their fight against darkness.
- An item worn by players, recognized and commented on by [[NPCs/Anastrasya Karelova|Anastrasya]] as having a special significance.
- Serves as an indicator of faith or heritage and may carry symbolic meaning beyond its physical form.
- An artifact taken from [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] by [[NPCs/Livius Martikov|Livius Martikov]], also known as [[Items/Sigil of the Sun|the Sigil of the Sun]].
- Connected to older events involving [[NPCs/Lugdana|Lugdana]], a former paladin who sought out Anastrasya.
- Used as a sign of faith by good-hearted individuals in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] to defend against evil.
- A legendary wondrous item found in the shrine that contains a statue of [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]].
- Carries importance among those familiar with Barovian history or traditions.
- Has properties including Dawn's Embrace, Light of Hope, and Sun’s Blessing.
- A legendary wondrous item requiring attunement by a good-aligned creature.
- Revealed through [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark]]'s tale as origin of faith among the people.
- Associated not only with religion but also with legacy and memory.
- Can be taken only with Oath of the New Moon sworn by the party.
- Placed in the shrine of Mother Night in the [[Places/Werewolf Den|werewolf den]].
- Lost in battle with [[NPCs/Chernovog|Chernovog]] and became a lost relic.
- Known after being renamed from *[[Items/Sigil of the Sun|Sigil of the Sun]]*.
- Has 5 charges and regains 1d4 + 1 each dawn.

## DM Notes

- Has potential ties to past history and possibly prophetic elements depending on its bearer's role.
- Its absence may point toward future restoration or revelation of lost power within the land.
- May unlock deeper lore or provide information for future quests.
- Arc V: The [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]].
