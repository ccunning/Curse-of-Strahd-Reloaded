# Dragon's Scale

> **item** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- If handled with reverence and respect, it can give advantage on Charisma checks made toward [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Godfrey]] unless he turns hostile.
- Recognized by [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Godfrey]] as one of [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]’s own scales and holds significance for heroes seeking his blessing.
- It appears to be related to the inheritance and legacy associated with the Dragon's Manor and its events.
- It can also be used to confirm the legitimacy of a player's ties to the dragon or the mission ahead.
- Originally belonged to [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]], an adult silver dragon who founded the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]].
- A piece of Argynvost's scale, found in the inheritance of players during [[Items/Character Creation|Character Creation]].
- Burns cold while approaching manifestations of Argynvost’s spirit in [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]].
- This item is mentioned in the context of [[Items/Character Creation|character creation]] during inheritance.
- Not tied to any particular artifact but represents connection to Argynvost.
- It burns icy cold, with one end glowing bright silver towards [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Provides a calming sense of safety and comfort for Good-aligned creatures.
- Glows with silver light in the direction of [[Factions/Death House|Death House]] when sensed.
- A rare magical item referenced when discussing the Dragon's Manor.
- A large silver scale that feels cold to the touch.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- The significance of this item relates to potential legacy or family history for players in their journey through the manor.
- Can play a key role in earning trust and respect from key characters like Godfrey; may provide clues when examined.
- Upon helping retrieve Argynvost’s skull, his spirit greets player as heir to the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]].
- In [[Arc M - The Dragon's Manor]], it guides the approach to Argynvost’s [[NPCs/Shadow|shadow]] in [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]].
