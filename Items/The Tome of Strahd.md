# The Tome of Strahd

> **item** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- Its presence enables characters to access information that can lead them to understanding about the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] and events concerning [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Contains historical accounts about Sergei's death, [[NPCs/Tatyana Federovna|Tatyana]]'s fate, and the creation of the land of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Locked by an arcane lock spell at 5th level, requiring pass phrase 'I come in Dostron's memory' to open.
- Is kept within [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]] after being stolen by [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]] upon his mother’s murder.
- A powerful artifact containing the inner thoughts and secrets of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd von Zarovich]].
- A powerful enchanted tome with knowledge and prophetic visions related to Strahd.
- Has diagrams and symbols hidden by a cipher, deciphered by the [[NPCs/Lich|lich]] [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]].
- Placed in the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] under the gallows speaker [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]].
- Expanded to link with the [[Places/Fanes of Barovia|Fanes of Barovia]] and [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- A magical tome stolen from [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] by [[NPCs/Livius Martikov|Livius Martikov]].
- Contains secret information about Strahd's ultimate plan.
- Connected to the history and power of Strahd in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Contains information that helps plan the campaign.
- Mystical artifact to be recovered in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].

## DM Notes

- The cipher will be crucial information in later storylines, especially involving [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]].
- Its content will reveal more secrets about Strahd's history in the campaign.
- Key item with clues leading to more significant arcs like Act IV.
- Arc H - The Lost Soul.
