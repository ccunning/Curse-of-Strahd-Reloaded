# Wolf's Tooth

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Recognized by [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] at [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]] or [[NPCs/Elder Burebis|Elder Burebis]] in [[Arc R - Trials of the Mountain]].
- Once belonged to a werewolf serving the [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]], who was later cursed with lycanthropy.
- Lengthens and sharpens on nights of the full moon; calms its wearer’s dreams.
- It lengthens, glows silver moonlight, and pulls its cord toward [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Glows with silver moonlight upon sensing [[Factions/Death House|Death House]] and pulls toward it.
- A wolf’s tooth coated in clear resin and mounted on a leather cord.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- In [[Arc T - The Three Fanes]], the tooth’s glow leads to the [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane]].
