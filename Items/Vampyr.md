# Vampyr

## Key Facts

- Set the beginning for later relationships or influences involving supernatural transformation.
- Described as a transformative power with consequences and responsibilities.
- Dark gift mentioned in early accounts of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s transformation process.

- Reflects the theme of corruption and its long-lasting ramifications.
