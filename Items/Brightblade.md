# Brightblade

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- It glowed with the light of the sun and is associated with a history where it might be connected to the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] prophecy.
- Its whereabouts are uncertain after being passed down or possibly destroyed.
- A crystal blade created from [[NPCs/Prince Erevan Löwenhart|Prince Erevan Löwenhart]]'s shattered moonblade.
- Gifted by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] to Sergei, who became a priest and later married [[NPCs/Tatyana Federovna|Tatyana]].
- Sealed Prince Löwenhart’s spirit within it to empower its magic.
- Forged by King Barov with assistance from [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]].
- A legendary enchanted sword wielded by King Barov II.
- Eventually destroyed or lost during [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s journey.

## DM Notes

- The mention of [[Items/Brightblade|Brightblade]] connects the current struggle to the ancient power of the Barovian king, suggesting it may play an important part in defeating Strahd.
