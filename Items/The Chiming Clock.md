# The Chiming Clock

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- A magical clock that chimes on each hour and inflicts necrotic damage and reduces maximum hit points.
- It remains functional even if damaged or destroyed, reappearing in its original place after removal.
- It is a recurring element within the nightmare realm that affects player characters.
