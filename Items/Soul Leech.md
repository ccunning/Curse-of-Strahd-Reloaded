# Soul Leech

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Players are instructed to deliver it to Rahadin’s office at [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] and uncork it, allowing the leech to attach itself to his desk so that it can entrap his soul upon his death.
- A Tiny fiend resembling a leech that [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] imprisoned in a glass vial, used as part of her bargaining scheme with players seeking to borrow the [[Items/Heartstone|heartstone]].
- The leech is intended to transform into a soul larva, which [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] hopes to use for nefarious purposes after Rahadin dies.
- Represents an important point in the narrative for fulfilling Morgantha’s conditions and avoiding consequences.
- Its appearance reflects its role as a tool of manipulation within [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s dark machinations.
- A key element used later in story progression if players choose to take on Morgantha’s task.
- Players must complete the task in fourteen days or risk having their dreams devoured.
- An item that Morgantha requested players deliver to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s chamberlain Rahadin.
- Linked to larger plans involving Strahd's court and possibly hidden dark magics.
- Used as a form of bargaining after acquiring [[Items/Heartstone|heartstone]] from the players.

## DM Notes

- The [[Items/Soul Leech|soul leech]] is instrumental to Morgantha's plan involving Rahadin, pointing toward larger consequences tied to Strahd's own escape strategies and possible betrayal.
