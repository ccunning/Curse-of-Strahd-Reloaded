# The Oath Celestial

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- It appears to be mentioned in the same area as other items, which may be associated with a historical conflict involving [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] or the Order.
- A book containing a prayer for the departed, which also outlines one of the oaths of the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]].
- Contains a passage that refers to the eternal nature of life and death and the promise made towards the end of days.
- Found in an old satchel within the Spiders' Ballroom alongside holy water and other items.
- An important holy text that contains the oath sworn by members of the Silver Dragon Order.
- Can be read aloud at the beacon where [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]’s spirit resides to invoke his guidance.
- Its presence or absence in different areas can affect gameplay and storytelling.
- Its contents are consistent with other Order-related oaths.

## DM Notes

- Required as part of the ritual to awaken [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]; may be tied into deeper lore or future quests.
- This text is integral to understanding the oath of the Order and the history at [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]].
- It plays a key role in summoning Argynvost's spirit through reading out the oath aloud.
