# Tarokka Cards

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Consist of two decks—one for the layout and one for additional readings—revealing specific cards related to destiny, help, weapons, enemies, and more.
- Used by [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]] to provide prophetic guidance to the players during a [[Items/Tarokka reading|Tarokka reading]] at [[Places/River Ivlis Crossroads|River Ivlis Crossroads]].
- Each card possesses unique meanings from Appendix E, which guide interpretation of answers provided by spirits.
- They provide visual and thematic guidance in interpreting spiritual revelations regarding the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]’s fall.
- They are consulted by [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]] as a medium for [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]] to speak with the living in the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]].
- Each card reveals cryptic images and meanings with connections to events or characters in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Only ten questions can be asked during one seance before it needs restocking for three days.
- These cards influence the actions and decisions of the party based on their revelations.
- Used during seances to contact spirits and gain insight into mysteries.

## DM Notes

- Use of [[Items/Tarokka Cards|Tarokka cards]] provides key knowledge about the artifact (the [[Items/Icon of Dawn's Grace|Icon of Dawn's Grace]]) and leads into major plot points involving [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]]'s crypts and other Arcs.
- Cards play critical roles in later chapters where they might reveal hidden information or paths toward [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s defeat.
- May have additional importance in arcs dealing with prophecies, past sins, or historical lore related to the land.
