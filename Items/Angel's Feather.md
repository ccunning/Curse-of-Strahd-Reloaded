# Angel's Feather

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Guides players toward the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] in [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|the Abbey of Saint Markovia]] when first arriving at the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|abbey]].
- Swept away by an unseen wind upon sensing [[Factions/Death House|Death House]] and lands on the house's threshold.
- It is swept away by an unseen wind, glowing with golden light, toward [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Once belonged to [[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]], a deva serving the god Lathander, the Morninglord.
- A large, snow-white feather that glimmers like gold under sunlight.
- [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] recognizes it as his own but dismisses its importance.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- Can be found in [[Arc I - The Walls of Krezk]] or [[Arc K - The Fallen Abbey]].
