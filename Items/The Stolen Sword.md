# The Stolen Sword

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- A location beyond the canal of blood within [[Places/The Vampyr’s Sarcophagus|the Vampyr’s sarcophagus]], where the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] hilt lies.
- Its connection to key characters or events is implied by its mention in multiple arcs.
- It is described as a demiplane with a reflective chamber and golden light beneath.
- A transition point between the regular [[Places/Amber Vault|Amber Vault]] and activation of the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]].
- Key area where the final confrontation with [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina]] occurs.
- A powerful sword referenced in the narrative.

## DM Notes

- [[Items/The Stolen Sword|The stolen sword]] is likely an important artefact in various parts of the campaign, possibly tied to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s story.
