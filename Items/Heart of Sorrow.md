# Heart of Sorrow

> **item** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- Constructed from the remains of a shattered magical artifact, symbolizing [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s early victims.
- An enormous crystal heart in the center of the tower that serves as the core of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s ritual.
- Protected by a wall of force augmented with private sanctum, preventing entry or escape.
- Connected to all Fanes, crucial for bringing the valley along with Strahd upon escape.
- Became a key component in both Strahd’s ascension and later plans for escape.
- Used as a monument to the early victims and the loss of purity in the castle.
- An artifact that came from the slaughter of castle guards during early times.
- Linked to many historical tales and personal struggles across generations.
- Associated with his transformation and the bond he forged with [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- A red crystal heart that hovers in the center of the North Tower peak.
- Associated with dark history, betrayal, and suffering throughout time.
- Used as a source of power or influence within key narrative threads.
- The sliver of darkness within it represents [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]]'s vestige.
- Connected to the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] and used by Strahd for his escape plan.
- Contains a vestige extracted by Strahd from [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Key artifact used to power the ritual for escaping the [[Places/Mists|Mists]].
- A heart-shaped crystal that floats above [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Acts as both a conduit and an anchor during the ritual.
- Used by Strahd to channel the power of the Fanes.
- Serves to bind or inspire supernatural entities.
- Artifact within [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].

- Its use suggests possible connections to major figures or events that will come into play later in the storyline.
- Will be vital in the climactic confrontation, possibly needing to be destroyed or controlled by the players.
