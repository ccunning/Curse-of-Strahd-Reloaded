# Silverthorn Collar

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Has a keyhole that can open it with a Strength or Dexterity check, or with dispel magic.
- Causes piercing damage to any lycanthrope who attempts transformation or lock picking.
- Prevents regeneration when worn and is immune to dispel magic and knock spells.
- Has a small keyhole, accessible only with the key held by [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril Stoyanovich]].
- A magical item worn by those in the [[Places/Werewolf Den|werewolf den]] to prevent transformation.
- A magical item worn by lycanthropes to prevent transformations.
- Can be opened only through brute force or using proper key.
