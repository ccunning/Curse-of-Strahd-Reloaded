# Spirit Mirror

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Allows [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]] to visually present Stella to players using only gesture and lipreading techniques.
- A wondrous item found in [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor Vallakovich]]'s workroom that reflects locations in [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]].
- Originally obtained through magical means involving [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus]] and possibly Dr. Rudolph van Richten.
- A magical mirror capable of reflecting visions from the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] to the Material Plane.
- Used by Victor and Stella to communicate during Stella’s absence due to soul separation.
- Crucial tool for keeping Stella's presence known while she remains trapped beyond sight.
- Plays a key role in the early stages of helping Stella return to her body.
- Has relevance in other story arcs, notably in lifting Van Richten's curse.
- Can be used daily once per day for either shrinking or growing in size.
- Given to the players as a reward for restoring Stella Wachter’s soul.

## DM Notes

- Revealed as a tool for viewing Ethereal Plane locations, which could be useful later in campaign.
- Connected to future events around Van Richten's curse, as detailed in Arc T.
