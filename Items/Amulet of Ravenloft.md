# Amulet of Ravenloft

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- When activated, it grants the wielder lair actions and allows passage through solid walls.
- It triggers animation of multiple skeletons and other creatures from various crypts.
- A legendary wondrous item requiring attunement by a creature chosen by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- It can cause all doorways in K84 [[Places/Catacombs|Catacombs]] to open.
- Can only be used once every thirty days.

## DM Notes

- The amulet causes the activation of many traps and creature animations in combat.
- Rahadin uses it as an escape mechanism when reduced to 50 HP or fewer.
