# Icon of Dawn's Grace

> **item** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- A holy relic created by [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]] and possessed by [[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]] (the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]), which symbolizes the heart of divinity.
- It played a role in the formation of [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]]'s prophetic visions as the artifact itself held divine spark.
- Contains information that can be shared with characters about Saint Markovia's [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|abbey]] and her final days.
- An enchanted icon depicting a small statuette, part of the depiction in the stained-glass windows.
- Revealed to be located inside Saint Markovia's crypt during visions received after the seance.
- An eight-inch-tall statuette that grants protection from evil and good to nearby creatures.
- It is revealed that this object is related to Saint Markovia being anointed by [[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]].
- Found in Arc P - Ravenloft Heist, specifically the [[Places/South Crypts|South Crypts]] under the third row.
- The icon recognizes the appearance of the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] if he is identified in his guises.
- Its primary function is to cleanse the Abbot’s corrupted soul and thus redeem him.
- A sentient magic item inhabited by a shard of the Abbot’s divine consciousness.
- Is attuned to a deva named Ithuriel, who blessed it to Markovia centuries ago.
- It is kept in Saint Markovia's crypt located in the [[Places/Castle Crypts|Castle Crypts]].
- It is one of the objects to be recovered during the heist.
- The [[Items/Icon of Dawn's Grace|Icon of Dawn's Grace]] is a powerful magical artifact.
- Its glow symbolizes a part of the Abbot's divinity.
- Artifact located in St. Markovia's crypt.
- Taken during Act P – Ravenloft Heist.

## DM Notes

- The icon serves as a significant magical item in the storyline, particularly linked to understanding the prophecy and the true enemy that they must face.
- This artifact will play an important part in redeeming the Abbot and completing key story arcs, particularly Arc Q & P.
- It can be utilized to restore corruption on an individual (the Abbot) and has a narrative role in ending the curse.
- The icon connects to the restoration of the Abbot’s divinity which involves the Morninglord.
