# Crumpled Page

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Its ink glows amber light, reshapes itself into a map showing the player location and pointing to [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- In [[Arc S - A Sword of Sunlight]], it reshapes into a map of [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]]’s chambers.
- Glows amber when sensing [[Factions/Death House|Death House]] and reshapes itself into a map to the house.
- Taken from the [[Places/Library|library]] of [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]], a [[NPCs/Lich|lich]] dwelling in [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- An old yellowed page torn from an arcane book filled with cryptic runes.
- Speaks of a path to immortality and untold power.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- If shown to Exethanter afterward, he grows warmer and friendlier towards the player.
