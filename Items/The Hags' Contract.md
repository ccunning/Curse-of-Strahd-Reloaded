# The Hags' Contract

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- A leather book made from human skin, written in Abyssal script and signed with the true names of the three hags: [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha Stormreaver]], [[NPCs/Belladonna Sunbane|Belladonna Sunbane]], and [[NPCs/Offalia Wormwiggle|Offalia Wormwiggle]].
- Can’t be destroyed by normal attacks, only damaged by the hags’ own claw attacks; destroying it dispels the coven into individual night hags.
- Located inside the millstone of [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]], unlocked and retrieved using an iron key or a successful Dexterity (Thieves' Tools) check.
- Holds both the names of the hags necessary to defeat them permanently, and the treasure of the room as specified in Chapter 6: O3. Bedroom.
- Essential for completing the raid on [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]] and obtaining key identifiers for defeating the hags once in battle.

## DM Notes

- This item’s existence implies knowledge of magical contracts or binding arrangements within [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s domain, which may be connected to larger arcane forces affecting the campaign.
