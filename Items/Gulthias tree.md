# Gulthias tree

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Nurtured by the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] within the winery, and gives birth to blights.
- Is an accomplice to the druids' will and grows stronger with cursed blood.
- Plays an active role in the ritual being performed by [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk druids]].
- Used in the winery to create blights and control environments.
- Connected to the overall theme of corruption in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Has a grove that contains the [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane]] shrine.
- A vampiric tree growing atop [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
- A dark, corrupted tree atop [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
- A cursed tree growing atop Yester Hill.

## DM Notes

- Gulthias is tied to the blight creatures that spawn from it, and thus influences the battle at Yester Hill.
