# Keeper's Whistle

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Useful for scouting, delivering messages, obtaining items, distracting enemies, or spying.
- It can summon [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]] to scout for enemies or deliver messages.
- Used as part of [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian]]’s gratitude for players’ efforts in retrieving the gem.
- The [[Items/Keeper's Whistle|Keeper's whistle]] is obtained at the end of Arc J - The Stolen Gem.
- Belongs to family lineage of the [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|Keepers]] and passes down generations.
- A magic item in the form of an obsidian whistle shaped like a raven.
- Players can use it to request assistance while plotting their heist.
- Can summon a wereraven agent within five miles once per day.

## DM Notes

- Frequent use may increase involvement with wereraven factions or missions.
- Agents summoned could introduce new NPCs or challenges for the party.
