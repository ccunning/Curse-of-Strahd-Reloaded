# Heartstone

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- An artifact allowing the wielder to borrow and use spells of a limited number of levels, with restrictions like returning it within three days or suffering permanent dream loss if betrayed.
- [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] lends heartstones to players for short periods during negotiations, providing them access to magical power tied closely to her domain.
- Heartstones are described as items that can be used in conjuring and casting powerful magical spells beyond a typical character’s capability.
- Must be returned within three days, otherwise dream loss occurs; also serves as collateral when players engage in risky deals with [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]].
- Used for gaining access to spells during the conflict with the hags but holds significant narrative weight in relation to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s plans.
- Used under duress by players when negotiating with Morgantha, resulting in them taking magical dreams as collateral.
- The [[Items/Heartstone|heartstone]] causes physical disturbances such as ripples in the floor and mist roiling around.
- Can be used to perform the *etherealness* ritual when placed at the center of a pentagram.
- Described in detail in the book *[[Items/Ethereal Entities|Ethereal Entities]]* under the section about night hags.
- A necessary component in the etherealness and restoration rituals to bring Stella back.
- Appears as an obsidian object with a shimmering, spectral luminescence when activated.
- Can be found in the possession of Morgantha and forms part of her coven’s ritual.
- Essential for moving players into the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] where Stella's soul resides.
- Magical artifact used by night hags to escape or enter the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]].
- Required component for using a specific spell that mimics etherealness.

## DM Notes

- Heartstones are central in the lore of Ravenloft and indicate an underappreciated source of arcane power available through negotiation or coercion.
- They could play into future scenarios involving magical conflicts, especially considering how Morgantha uses them as leverage in her dealings.
