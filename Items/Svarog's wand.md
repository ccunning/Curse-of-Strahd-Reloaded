# Svarog's wand

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Possessed and later stolen or lost, returned to the players in this arc.
- It was used in connection with rituals involving the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]] goddesses.
- A wand wielded by those who connected [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]] to the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]].
- Its current whereabouts are tied to events in [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].

## DM Notes

- Recovery of the wand could be connected to restoring spiritual power or access to [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]].
- Its role in connecting [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] to old traditions may be important in future lore.
