# Broken Blade

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- It emits a high-pitched hum and spins like a compass needle when oriented towards [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Rahadin helped King Barov conquer [[NPCs/Prince Erevan Löwenhart|Erevan]]’s kingdom, breaking the blade and executing [[NPCs/Prince Erevan Löwenhart|Erevan]].
- When in proximity to Rahadin, it trembles violently, spinning like a compass toward him.
- Once belonged to Erevan, dusk elf prince who refused to pay tribute to King Barov.
- Emitting high-pitched hum near [[Factions/Death House|Death House]], spins toward it like a compass needle.
- The shattered tip of an elven longsword engraved with a lion rampant sigil.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- In [[Arc E - The Missing Vistana]], [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir]] recognizes its history and greets the player as family.
- In combat with Rahadin, he eventually recalls blade’s origin after being reminded of it.
