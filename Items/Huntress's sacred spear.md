# Huntress's sacred spear

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Can be recovered in the tomb of [[NPCs/King Dostron|King Dostron]], who stole it after [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]]’s defeat.
- A holy weapon belonging to the goddess [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]] and guarded at the [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane]].
- Reconsecrating this spear at [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]] may allow redemption of past misdeeds.
- Stolen by [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]], who later used it to unify tribes under his rule.
- It was taken from its holy site by Kavan as a means of power.

## DM Notes

- The spear's recovery and reconsecration is part of the redemption arc for Kavan and players.
- It could become a symbol of divine authority or power over corruption.
