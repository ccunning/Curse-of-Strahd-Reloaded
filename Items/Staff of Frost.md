# Staff of Frost

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Can dispel any cantrip or 1st-level spell cast on it but not instantaneous spells or those affecting specific creatures.
- Whispers into players' minds if picked up at Vilnius’s request—trying to trick them into stealing it.
- Has five charges, uses two charges to cast *fabricate* with an ice-based creation at 1 minute.
- Is cursed—when removed from [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]], it returns to its original position.
- A staff shaped like a jagged icicle found in the [[Places/Shrine of Temptation|shrine of temptation]].

## DM Notes

- It may play a role in controlling access or manipulating characters through dark gifts or corruption.
