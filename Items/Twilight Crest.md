# Twilight Crest

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Can be used to cast aid during daytime, spirit guardians during nighttime, or enervation at any time.
- Believed to be a relic of a dead god who once dwelt in [[Places/Whispering Wall|the Whispering Wall]].
- A wondrous item given to players after defeating the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]].
- Has 3 charges and regenerates 1 charge at dawn.
