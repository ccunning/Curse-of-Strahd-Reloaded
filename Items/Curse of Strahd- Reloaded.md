# Curse of Strahd: Reloaded

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Designed to make the campaign more accessible, cohesive, and enjoyable by addressing issues in the original module.
- Intended to be used as a standalone resource with no need to reference the original module except where specified.
- Does not incorporate all elements from the original, only those referenced or overwritten by this guide.
- The core campaign that players are expected to engage with during [[Items/Session Zero|Session Zero]] and beyond.
- A comprehensive guide for Dungeon Masters to running the [[Items/Curse of Strahd|Curse of Strahd]] campaign.
- The main campaign setting, a gothic horror adventure in the world of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- A complete replacement guide for the original Curse of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] module.
