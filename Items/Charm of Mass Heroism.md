# Charm of Mass Heroism

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- A golden medallion adorned with interlocking rings, capable of providing multiple creatures within 30 feet with the benefit of a *potion of heroism* as an action.
- Found in the stone compartment inside the millstone of [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]] after retrieving the iron key used to unlock it.
- Useful for enhancing group capabilities in combat or challenging scenarios requiring heightened bravery or strength.
- Revealed when the players obtain [[Items/The Hags' Contract|the hags' contract]], signifying it as a reward for overcoming the hags.
- Can only be activated once and disappears from wielder’s person after usage.

## DM Notes

- Such an item reflects the theme of empowerment within [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s domain—the magic that allows the bold to stand against [[Factions/Dark Powers|dark powers]], although here used for tactical gain rather than absolute good.
