# Electrum Coin

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Players can learn of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s treasure hoard in [[Arc E - The Missing Vistana]] or [[Arc M - The Dragon's Manor]].
- Was taken from [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s treasuries by a thief before [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] was lost to the [[Places/Mists|mists]].
- The visage changes from aristocratic sneer to monstrous snarl in certain light.
- An old, chipped coin stamped with Strahd von Zarovich's profiled visage.
- It lands on its edge, rolls across the stones towards [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Rolls toward [[Factions/Death House|Death House]]’s steps upon sensing its presence.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- When approaching Strahd's [[Places/Treasury|treasury]], it rolls as far as the entrance, mirroring its behavior at Death House.
