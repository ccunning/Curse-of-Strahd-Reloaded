# Crystal of Alarm

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Can alert the entire [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]] if shattered by players during confrontation.
- Is magically connected to [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril Stoyanovich]], who knows when it is broken.
- Made of fragile yellow-gold crystalline glass shaped like a wolf fang.
- A magical item worn by [[NPCs/Bianca Stoyanovich|Bianca]] that creates an alarm when shattered.
- [[NPCs/Bianca Stoyanovich|Bianca]] breaks it to summon [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]]'s return as a last resort.
