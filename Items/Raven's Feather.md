# Raven's Feather

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Recognized by [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]] in [[Act I - Into the Mists/Arc C - Into the Valley|Arc C - Into the Valley]] and members of [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- It drifts away on the wind, casting a dancing [[NPCs/Shadow|shadow]] as it moves toward [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Swept away by an unseen wind upon sensing [[Factions/Death House|Death House]], landing at house's threshold.
- Belonged to a [[NPCs/Roc|roc]] serving the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]], who disguised itself as a raven.
- Exudes a scent of cold rain and damp earth before storms.
- A small, black raven feather with soft, downy barbs.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- If shown to [[NPCs/Roc|Roc]] after consecrating theMountain Fane, the roc places its beak against the player's forehead as sign of gratitude.
- In [[Arc T - The Three Fanes]], it trembles and emits scent of rain upon approaching [[Places/The Mountain Fane|the Mountain Fane]].
