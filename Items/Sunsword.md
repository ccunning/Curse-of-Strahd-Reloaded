# Sunsword

> **item** · 6 source files

## Key Facts

- The prophecy mentions it as possibly being the only weapon capable of destroying [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] after his transformation into a vampire.
- A sentient magic weapon that cannot be attuned until submerged in the blessed pool near the [[Places/Shrine of the White Sun|Shrine of the White Sun]].
- Its presence ties into the tale of King Barov II and how a [[Items/Brightblade|Brightblade]] (a type of sun-glowing sword) might relate.
- Its full power is activated when it is infused with another suitable soul, turning into a radiant divine blade.
- Essential for players to combat [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] in his final form, weakening his sunlight vulnerability.
- A powerful weapon made from a platinum hilt enchanted with unbreakable metal and a sapphire.
- Contains the soul of [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina Velikovna]], who has been bound within for nearly four centuries.
- Its radiant blade is withheld to create narrative opportunities and climactic combat.
- Can be obtained early in the game, which is one of the campaign's design problems.
- Its hilt is hidden in a secret demiplane and within the [[Places/Prison|prison]] of [[NPCs/The Vampyr|the Vampyr]].
- A magical weapon that has the potential to defeat Strahd if used properly.
- [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina Velikovna]] hid the hilt with plans to use it as her phylactery.
- A powerful mystical sword with effects on sunlight and [[Factions/Dark Powers|dark powers]].
- A prophetic item connected to the destiny of overcoming Strahd.
- Can be used as a weapon after proper infusion and activation.
- A powerful artifact capable of killing Strahd von Zarovich.
- Used in the climax to weaken his control over the valley.
- Placed in the [[Items/Vampyr|Vampyr]]'s sarcophagus in [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- The key artifact sought by players in the temple.
- Ancient sword whose hilt is to be recovered.
- Important for defeating Strahd in Act V.

## DM Notes

- It connects with the idea of returning the skull to [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]'s crypt, where it will play a significant role in events following Arc P - Ravenloft Heist.
- The activation of the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] will have significant consequences affecting Strahd’s rule.
- The hilt might be linked to Patrina's spirit and require confronting her to retrieve.
- Its full power may be necessary to defeat his phases and eventually destroy him.
- Finding the [[Items/Sunsword|sunsword]] can determine the success or failure of defeating Strahd.
- Finding this powerful artifact could be essential to defeating Strahd.
- Arc V: The Sunsword.
