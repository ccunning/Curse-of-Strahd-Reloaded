# Teleportation Circle

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Could potentially serve as a base mechanism if reworked or corrected with new knowledge gained from other magical sources.
- Revealed to be the original cause of Stella’s soul being severed and serves as motivation for further action.
- Failed during a test involving human sacrifice, resulting in Stella losing her soul to the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]].
- A magical construct developed by [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]] and Stella in preparation for escape from [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s realm.
- The focus of [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]]’s research as he attempts to restore Stella through alternate means.
- Used by Victor Vallakovich and Stella Wachter to attempt escape from [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s rule.
- Resulted in unintended consequences for both parties involved in its creation.
- Played a part in causing the spiritual destruction of a loved one.
- Demonstrated potential misuse of powerful magical technologies.
- Symbolized early attempts at freedom and escape from darkness.

## DM Notes

- It implies there are more uses for such items and that they may be central to upcoming adventures or escapes.
