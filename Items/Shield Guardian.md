# Shield Guardian

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- The [[Items/Shield Guardian|shield guardian]] was a construct bound to Vilnius's life force over centuries.
- It acts as a protector and ally in combat alongside Vilnius in the [[Places/Lecture Hall|Lecture Hall]].
- Its control amulet only functions for Vilnius.

## DM Notes

- The [[Items/Shield Guardian|shield guardian]]’s bond with Vilnius reflects their shared fate within [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]]'s curse.
