# Bloodthorn

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Spear is associated with [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] and was central to his rise to power over [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]].
- Imbued with the ability to steal strength from enemies and nourish its wielder.
- Later passed down through [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]]'s legacy and became a symbol of corruption.
- A spear stolen from the [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane]] by Kavan and blessed with dark power.
- Named after Kavan’s title as Blood-Drinker.
