# Wanderer's Scarf

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Caravan leader gave it as a farewell gift; the player received it from a family member.
- It is carried aloft by the wind, shifting in patterns as it swirls toward [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Once belonged to a Vistana who left the caravan to marry someone they loved.
- Carried away by a warm, playful breeze toward [[Factions/Death House|Death House]]’s threshold.
- When bright light passes through it, its patterns shimmer and twist.
- A multicolored scarf woven with complex patterns, soft and warm.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- In [[Arc K - The Fallen Abbey]], it lands at [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]]'s feet when encountered, and she greets the player as family.
