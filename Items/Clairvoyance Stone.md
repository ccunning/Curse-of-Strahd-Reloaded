# Clairvoyance Stone

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- [[Items/Clairvoyance Stone|Clairvoyance Stone]] is a magical item that grants the effect of clairvoyance spell.
- It allows use of clairvoyance spell three times before crumbling to dust.
- Players receive it from [[NPCs/Stanimir|Stanimir]] if they had previously helped [[NPCs/Varushka|Varushka]].
