# Amber Shards

> **item** · 6 source files

## Key Facts

- If a player possesses an [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]], it may cause them to be viewed suspiciously or even attacked by key NPCs until proven otherwise.
- Objects tied to the corruptive [[Places/Amber Temple|Amber Temple]] that can influence player character's choices, especially in regard to Charisma checks.
- These items are tied to specific individuals and locations, with one belonging to the Hell Hound Whisperer Seriach.
- The corruption progresses through stages and players must convince [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Godfrey]] they don't seek the [[Items/Amber Shards|amber shards]]' gifts.
- It is not clear if any are present in this arc directly but the implications of their presence are significant.
- In [[Arc R - Trials of the Mountain]], [[NPCs/Elder Burebis|Elder Burebis]] recognizes it as evil taken from [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- They have significance in the game's lore related to powerful entities and their influence on [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Accessing [[Items/Amber Shards|amber shards]] will likely lead to deeper narrative exploration in other arcs of the campaign.
- Amber Shards are relics found inside a pouch recovered from [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]] after his assassination.
- In [[Arc A - Escape From Death House]], it is recognized by Elisabeth Durst and [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]].
- A compendium included in the guide listing all amber shards appearing in the adventure.
- Its corruption affects decision-making and can influence how NPCs respond to players.
- Each stage of the [[Items/Amber Corruption|amber corruption]] includes specific physical and behavioral changes.
- The Amber Shards are a system of corruption affecting creatures who take dark gifts.
- A small chunk of amber resin mounted on a chain-link cord with complex arcane runes.
- They appear as part of the lore and are mentioned in the description of prophecies.
- Formerly part of a dark vestige's amber sarcophagus in the Amber Temple in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- There are several sarcophagi throughout the temple where these gifts originate.
- Items connected to the Amber Temple and associated with the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] prophecy.
- Glows with amber light when sensing [[Factions/Death House|Death House]] and tugs its cord toward it.
- A category of magical items introduced in an appendix.
- One of the items placed inside the treasure room.

## DM Notes

- The mention of amber shards introduces the idea of potent magical artifacts that may play a role in future arcs like Arc D - [[Items/The Tome of Strahd|The Tome of Strahd]] or Arc S - A Sword of Sunlight.
- The Amber Shards may be central to further plot developments related to curses, transformations, and [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]].
- If player character is reduced to 0 hit points with it, it offers pact with a vestige of the Amber Temple.
- May return as part of later campaigns or arcs; serves as a tool for testing character integrity.
- These shards may hold importance in locating a powerful artifact or completing specific quests.
- Guides toward an amber sarcophagus in the Amber Temple upon entering.
