# Gargoyles

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Statues that animate once activated from the tower roof or when climbers approach [[Places/Scaffolding|scaffolding]].
- Each has different resistances and vulnerabilities based on element.
- Can lose flight speed by suffering certain elemental damage types.
- They attack player characters upon animation unless prevented.
- The gargoyle statues are disguised as normal statuary.

## DM Notes

- May be connected to larger themes of deception and the supernatural within the world.
