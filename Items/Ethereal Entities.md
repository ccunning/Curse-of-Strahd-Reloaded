# Ethereal Entities

> **item** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Contains information on specific creatures including night hags and rituals that use heartstones to access the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]].
- Described as containing bestiaries divided into categories like Etherborn, Phantomfolk, and Veil-Walkers.
- Used as a key resource that helps [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]] and players understand methods for entering ethereal realms.
- A book written by [[NPCs/Mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]] describing creatures within the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] and their behaviors.
- Influential in developing the ritual needed to perform an etherealness spell or bind hags.
