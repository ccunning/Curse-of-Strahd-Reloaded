# Tattered Banner

> **item** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Elicits revulsion from [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Sir Godfrey Gwilym]], Sir [[NPCs/Vladimir Horngaard|Vladimir Horngaard]], [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir Velikov]], or others.
- Crimson light glimmers from castle’s keep whenever lawful-aligned creature holds it.
- It sounds like war-drums and blows in an unseen wind towards [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Once flown by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd von Zarovich]] in the days before he became a vampire.
- Flaps with wind upon sensing [[Factions/Death House|Death House]], pulling toward house direction.
- An old, ragged red banner bearing raven and castle heraldry.
- One of the [[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian relics]] that players carry.

## DM Notes

- Recognized as the heraldry of [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] by Dr. Rudolph van Richten and [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]].
- In [[Arc I - The Walls of Krezk]], it flaps near haunted battlefield north of [[Places/Berez|Berez]].
