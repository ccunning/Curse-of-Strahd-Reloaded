# Huntress

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Bestowed lycanthropy upon followers; her werewolf servants were called wolfir.
- One of the four sisters in the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]], representing summer and hunting.
- Governs war, flames of passion, and hunting; her symbol is the wolf.
- Part of the Ladies Three alongside the [[NPCs/Weaver|Weaver]] and [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]].
- Had a sacred spear taken by [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] from the [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane]].
