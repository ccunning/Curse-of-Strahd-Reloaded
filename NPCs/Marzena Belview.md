# Marzena Belview

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- She escaped from her post in the courtyard during the players' visit and tries to find her patriarch.
- She is eventually bound to a shed in the courtyard by the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] due to her mental instability.
- Marzena seeks the 'perfection' that her ancestors were promised but never achieved.
- Her escape occurs shortly after [[Items/Vasilka|Vasilka]] gives the golem the flower necklace.
- Marzena is a bat-winged Belview and grand-niece of [[NPCs/Cyrus Belview|Cyrus]].
