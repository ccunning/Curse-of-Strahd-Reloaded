# Volenta

> **NPC** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- Her abilities include regeneration, spider climb, sunlight hypersensitivity, and attacks like Hail of Daggers and Bite.
- [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]] uses alchemical weapons and magic in combat, such as Tanglefoot, Thunderstone, and Alchemist's Firebomb.
- [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]] is a sadistic vampire bride of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] who seeks to impress him through innovation and suffering.
- [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]] holds keys to all the cells in the [[Places/Dungeons|dungeons]] and also has a key to [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil]]'s [[Items/Silverthorn Collar|silverthorn collar]].
- She has two main forms: First Form (undead humanoid, chaotic evil) and Second Form ([[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]]).
- Known for her volatility and cruel treatment of others, especially the tongueless servant [[NPCs/Anton Konstantinovich|Anton]].
- Tends to be irritable when approached by guests, often showing anger at minor inconveniences.
- A chaotic evil undead alchemical savant, seated on the left throne in [[Places/Torture Chamber|the Torture Chamber]].
- Connected to Volenta's personal suffering; she tortured [[NPCs/Anton Konstantinovich|Anton]] and his wife Dezdrelda.
- The 'Frenzied Spawn' form is a variant that she transforms into when reduced to 0 HP.
- May appear dead if players previously killed her in Arc D - [[Places/St. Andral's Feast|St. Andral's Feast]].
- Volenta is a vampire who guards [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil Toranescu]] in the castle's black cells.
- She and her zombies are found in the [[Places/Dungeons|dungeons]] far below the castle.
- She plays with a human skull belonging to [[NPCs/Dezdrelda Konstaninova|Dezdrelda Konstaninova]].
- Used for keeping order and executing his plans in some locations.
- Acts as a low-level boss encounter in the [[Places/Coffin Maker's Shop|Coffin Maker's Shop]].
- Represents one of the figures who must fulfill specific roles.
- A [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]] and mastermind behind the theft of the bones.
- Intended to be recurring antagonist.
- One of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s vampiric brides.
- Among Strahd's vampiric brides.

## DM Notes

- She joins zombies in attacking player characters in combat if players encounter her after Arc D - [[Places/St. Andral's Feast|St. Andral's Feast]].
- Given her violent nature, she could be either a combatant or victim depending on player actions and choices.
- Her treatment of Anton could reveal further lore related to Strahd’s methods of subjugation.
- She uses alchemist's firebombs, thunderstones, froststars, and caustic splash magic attacks.
- Volenta is an antagonist who has been relishing the opportunity for a rematch.
