# Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- He was involved in [[Factions/The Belviews|the Belviews]]' transformation and created [[Items/Vasilka|Vasilka]] as a potential bride for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] to help lift [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]’s curse.
- Has phases: first phase as a monk, second phase as a massive creature with a lion head, ram body, bat wings, and crocodile tail.
- The [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] is an angel named [[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]] sent to honor [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]]'s memory, and is the master of [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|the Abbey of Saint Markovia]].
- He is currently focused on perfecting his creatures through experiments, but also seeks to rid the land of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s curse.
- Played a role in establishing early threats to the sanctity and security of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]’s religious institutions.
- Planned a final bride for Strahd, aiming to create a monstrous entity in pursuit of supernatural goals.
- His identity and true nature are unclear, raising suspicion he could be Strahd or one of his servants.
- Led investigations into human remains for magical experiments including the creation of flesh golems.
- The [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] operates from [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|the Abbey of Saint Markovia]] and is connected to the Morninglord.
- He may not be aware of all truths about his history or the corruption within the Abbey.
- He orchestrated the death of [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala]] and her resurrection in exchange for [[NPCs/Anna Krezkova|Anna]]’s heart.
- He is the creator of a [[Items/Flesh Golem|flesh golem]] [[Items/Vasilka|Vasilka]] and the resurrector of [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala Krezkova]].
- He is a mysterious entity with celestial powers that can assume different forms.
- He believes that filling the void in Strahd's soul with love can heal the land.
- He uses deceptions and manipulation to control families like the Krezkovs.
- He has been the abbott for 117 years, ten months and twenty-six days.
- Transforms into a powerful celestial being ([[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]]) during combat.
- The Abbot is an angel named Ithuriel, sent to honor [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]].
- A religious leader of Krezk who felt Strahd’s influence returning.
- Serves as an example of how even sacred spaces become corrupted.
- Can be redeemed through the use of the *[[Items/Icon of Dawn's Grace|Icon of Dawn's Grace]]*.
- He possesses healing touch that cures lycanthropy without fee.
- His restored form is a white-winged youth known as Ithuriel.
- A monk with a plan to seize [[NPCs/Anna Krezkova|Baroness Anna Krezkova]]'s heart.
- He appears as a handsome young monk or an eagle.

## DM Notes

- His use of dark techniques suggests future events may involve divine and supernatural corruption, particularly linked to the church.
- The Abbot can be redeemed through defeating him and using the [[Items/Icon of Dawn's Grace|Icon of Dawn's Grace]] to cleanse his corruption.
- His madness and corruption are tied to [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]] and the shard of divinity within the Icon.
- Players might uncover more about the Abbey’s hidden history and its connection to lycanthropy.
- The Abbot transforms into a celestial being/star to defend his mission or express wrath.
- The Abbot may be revealed as a greater figure in Strahd's conflict with the Morninglord.
- His connection with [[Places/Amber Temple|the amber temple]] is revealed.
