# Eliza

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Serves as a potential future antagonist or ally depending on how the party handles their interaction with her.
- Has been following the party and tries to track down details about [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s intentions through surveillance.
- Attempts to gather intelligence from outsiders but was foiled during [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]]'s [[Items/Tarokka reading|Tarokka reading]].
- Follows players after they leave [[Places/Tser Pool Encampment|Tser Pool encampment]] and is eventually caught by [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]].
- A Vistana woman acting as a spy for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]], using the stats of a **spy**.

## DM Notes

- If pursued, she might lead to more information on her connection to [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Could be used for intrigue by Strahd or other factions during subsequent events.
