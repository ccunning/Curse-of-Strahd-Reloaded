# Jakarion

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Has features like *shapechanger* and *invisibility*, making it a tricky and powerful ally for Vilnius.
- [[NPCs/Jakarion|Jakarion]] is a quasit familiar that takes the form of a toad and hides on Vilnius's shoulder.
- It has the shapechanger feature, allowing it to fly away from combat if danger arises.
- Is bound with Vilnius, so long as either lives, neither gains true death or peace.
- Its presence affects how Vilnius fights—he often uses it as a tactical measure.
- Vilnius's quasit familiar.

## DM Notes

- [[NPCs/Jakarion|Jakarion]] acts as one of the guardians of Vilnius’s spirit and a symbol of the ongoing conflict tied to him.
- Jakarion might be a clue in future dealings with Vilnius or [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]]’s history.
