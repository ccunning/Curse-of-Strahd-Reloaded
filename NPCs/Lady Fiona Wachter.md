# Lady Fiona Wachter

> **NPC** · 11 source files

## Key Facts

- Initially skeptical of [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]]'s claims about Stella’s soul condition but can be convinced to help if properly persuaded or by previous actions like killing [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]].
- Possesses spellcasting ability including raising binding circles with arcane power and providing magical assistance such as *mage armor* and sending rituals.
- A cult leader assisting players in combating or escaping the windmill, capable of calling upon four [[Factions/Cult Fanatics|cult fanatics]] to raise a binding circle for combat.
- Works in conjunction with [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor Vallakovich]] to help the players during the Battle with the Hags in order to safely obtain [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]]'s contract.
- Established a protective circle around [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]] to prevent ethereal creatures from entering, particularly the gallows speaker [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]].
- Can provide a [[Items/Charm of Mass Heroism|charm of mass heroism]] upon completing certain conditions, but is also known for employing deception or negotiation strategies.
- Is present in the dream world in her future form (the elderly) along with others like Nikolai, [[NPCs/Danika Dorakova|Danika]], Gadof, [[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]], Victor, and [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark]].
- Her stance is controversial among those attending the War Council; some believe they must act quickly while others advocate preparation.
- Eventually involved with the cult of [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] to overthrow and influence [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]'s destiny via a prophecy linking her with [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]].
- She was trained by the swamp witch [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] (referred to as 'Lysa') and follows the worship of [[Factions/Ezra|Ezra]], goddess of the [[Places/Mists|Mists]].
- [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]] is a widow of Nikolai Wachter I and mother of Nikolai Wachter II, [[NPCs/Karl Wachter|Karl Wachter]], and Stella Wachter.
- Has had a friendship with Victor Vallakovich until it ended after their failure to defend her from [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s emissary.
- Her family history includes tragedy—her parents and elder brother died in a mysterious accident during her youth.
- A young woman whose soul was severed from her body by a gallows speaker during a failed teleportation experiment.
- Received divine guidance from what she believed to be the god [[Factions/Ezra|Ezra]] (who is revealed to be a mask of [[Factions/Dark Powers|Dark Powers]]).
- Is present in both the current timeline and dreamed futures of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], where she is mentioned as a key figure.
- She suffers in a vegetative state since her illness developed two months ago after meeting Victor Vallakovich.
- She suffered due to the use of powerful artifacts under duress—connecting her to deeper narrative conflicts.
- Taught about magic and [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]], eventually choosing to return and assume leadership over her property.
- Has been able to communicate with Victor and [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus]] through a [[Items/Spirit Mirror|spirit mirror]] since her soul was separated.
- Serves as a powerful ally if approached correctly and aids in creating binding rituals to trap night hags.
- Her fate was tied directly into events involving both FionaWachter's and the Valeckich’s personal turmoil.
- Offers to assist the players in removing [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]] from power, potentially taking the position of Burgomaster.
- Involves herself with the current events concerning players and their interaction with local authority.
- Reveals three secrets when pressured by the party, related to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s escape plan and secret shrines.
- Floats down to players after the etherealness ritual and is shown to be translucent with fading legs.
- The Burgomistress of [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]], who has become a traitor to [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] after aiding the players.
- Is Lady Wachter's daughter, best friend of Victor Vallakovich, and a friend of [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus van Richten]].
- Disables the protective circle around [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]] to let creatures pass through the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]].
- She leads the initiative to confront Strahd in an alliance with other Vallakian nobles and leaders.
- His recognition of players and personal history with the family contributes to background details.
- Stella's condition is directly linked to events involving Izek Strazni according to Lady Wachter.
- Has lost her tangibility and slowly fades into the Deep Ethereal, requiring a restoration ritual.
- Her death contributed to the emotional stakes around [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Fiona Wachter]] and the rise of darker themes.
- Her illness involves soul loss, which will be explored more thoroughly in Arc H - The Lost Soul.
- A sixteen-year-old specter who was previously captured in the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] by [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]].
- Adopted by the Wachters, whose estate she later took control of after returning from hiding.
- Engage in conversation about festivals, baron's rules, and strange lights over the mansion.
