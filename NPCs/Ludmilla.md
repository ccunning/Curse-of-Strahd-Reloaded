# Ludmilla

> **NPC** · 6 source files

## Key Facts

- Prefers to lead enemies up <span class='citation'>K48. Offstair (p. 70)</span> toward <span class='citation'>K57. Tower Roof (p. 72)</span> for battle.
- [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]] can use magic like Hypnotic Pattern, Dimension Door, and Shroud of Shadows, as well as illusions and teleportation.
- She has three forms: First Form (undead humanoid, neutral evil), Elementalist, and Mistfiend, each with enhanced abilities.
- She is an ambitious necromancer dedicated to gaining knowledge and power, with a detached analytical personality.
- Can become aggressive if provoked, particularly in front of others like [[NPCs/Anton Konstantinovich|Anton]] or in connection with past events.
- She appears to be hiding in the [[Places/Main Foyer|main foyer]], waiting for players who may have caused disturbances earlier.
- Is present in the Element Room fighting alongside other witches unless prevented by player actions.
- Leader of the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk druids]] at [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] and has dealings with [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- A witch who helped find [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]]’s body and then orchestrated the plan against the Martikovs.
- Responsible for researching and vivisecting a wereraven to learn its secret properties.
- Works under [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s commands and aids in furthering his influence over the [[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]].
- She is known for provoking players into attacking her with various taunts and insults.
- [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]] is [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s second-eldest vampiric bride and the matron of [[NPCs/Barovian witches|Barovian witches]].
- She shows no remorse when spilling wine on [[NPCs/Anton Konstantinovich|Anton]]—unlike [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]], she uses lightning.
- Informs the players about a lightning strike damaging the south gate tower's runes.
- Plays an instrumental role in various plotlines due to her proximity to key events.
- A character mentioned in connection with provocations, not detailed further here.
- Possibly linked with [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]] due to shared antagonistic traits in Strahd’s court.
- Appears to be competent in handling castle maintenance and repairs during storms.
- Knew about the source of the wereraven curse and directed attacks on the winery.
- Helped [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]] gain the loyalty of Strahd’s **dire [[NPCs/Wolves|wolves]]** through her influence.
- She also imprisoned [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil Toranescu]] and holds the key to [[Items/Silverthorn Collar|the silverthorn collar]].
- Seems to have been involved in bringing a [[NPCs/Tree Blight|tree blight]] called [[NPCs/Wintersplinter|Wintersplinter]].
- Known for ambitious pursuit of magic knowledge and impressing Strahd.
- Connected to the events leading up to the current crisis in the pack.
- Ludmilla Vilisevic is a vampire bride of Strahd and an elementalist.
- Is described as having a '[[Places/The Shroud of Mist|shroud of mist]]' that she uses in combat.
- Brokered an agreement with druids of [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] to aid her plot.
- Is likely not aligned with players unless manipulated or coerced.
- Sentry who works with [[NPCs/Diavola|Diavola]] to address mechanical failures.
- Icy and disdainful in her interactions with the party.
- The mistress of the coven residing in the South Tower.
- A vampiric bride of Strahd, one of his second-eldest.
- The executor of [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]] [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]]'s fate by vivisection.
- Assigned to discipline [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- Was involved in [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]]’s transformation process.
- A vampiric bride and associate of Strahd.
- A vampire bride of Strahd von Zarovich.
- A [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]] or servant of Strahd.
- One of Strahd's vampiric brides.
