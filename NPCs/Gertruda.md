# Gertruda

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]] is a young woman with an idealistic and determined personality, often seeing the silver lining in dark situations.
- Her disappearance is the motivation for [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]] and [[NPCs/Father Donavich|Donavich]]’s journey to [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] and then to [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- A key figure whose fate is intertwined with events unfolding around players in the castle court.
- She is connected to a lure created by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] for the players to follow toward her bedchamber.
- Her primary motivation is to confirm [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]]'s safety and return home to [[Places/Village of Barovia|the village of Barovia]].
- Plays a significant role in connecting characters via romantic ties and potential rescues.
- Found in the King's Bedchamber, guarded by [[NPCs/Anastrasya Karelova|Anastrasya]] who wants her returned to chambers.
- Currently prisoner in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]], according to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s hold on her and others.
- Accidentally lost her slipper on the stairs as ordered by the charmed instructions.
- A woman whose slipper is found on a stairway, suggesting she was heading upward.
- [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]] expresses sincere gratitude to the player characters for rescuing her.
- A villager from [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] who was spirited away while traveling to [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
- Has been charmed by Strahd and manipulated into seeking help from [[NPCs/Sasha Ivliskova|Sasha]].
- Used as bait to lure players into Castle Ravenloft while Strahd is away.
- Character imprisoned in Castle Ravenloft during Act P – Ravenloft Heist.
- Gertruda is Doru's betrothed and is being held prisoner in the castle.
- She feels curiosity, thoughtfulness, excitement, and worry most often.
- Held as a prisoner in the castle and believed to be in danger.
- She is kept under lock and key by vampire [[NPCs/Anastrasya Karelova|Anastrasya Karelova]].
- Was tricked into coming to Castle Ravenloft by Strahd himself.
- She is one of the characters to be rescued during the heist.
- She is the daughter of [[NPCs/Mad Mary|Mad Mary]] and betrothed to Doru.
- Charmed by Strahd since her arrival in the castle.
- Placed in the king’s suites on the third floor.
- Daughter of [[NPCs/Mad Mary|Mad Mary]], betrothed of Doru.
- Betrothed to Doru, a [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]].
- Rescued by the players.

## DM Notes

- May provide insight into how Strahd treats those he cares for versus those he punishes.
- Her role ties into future plots involving Strahd’s strategy against the players.
- Her slipper is part of an ambush setup by Strahd meant to draw out players.
- Her status as prisoner implies she might be rescued or negotiated for.
- She represents a key figure to be saved or manipulated by the players.
