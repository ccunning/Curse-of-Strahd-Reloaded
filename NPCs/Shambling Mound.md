# Shambling Mound

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- It can be commanded with Elisabeth's [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] by speaking the name 'Walter', if Charisma (Intimidation) success is made.
- In its first form, it uses its 'heavy sleeper' feature to reduce multiattack power if unconscious within past minute.
- The primary enemy in the [[Places/Ritual Chamber|Ritual Chamber]], described as a swollen, bloated mound of bones, flesh, and gore.
- It contains Walter's spirit and remains, awakening when attacked or when tendrils are damaged.
- Upon reaching 0 HP and expelling creatures, it transforms into [[NPCs/Walter, the Graveborn|Walter, the Graveborn]].
- Possesses resistance, immunities, and new movement capabilities.
- A monster form assumed by Lorghoth during combat.
