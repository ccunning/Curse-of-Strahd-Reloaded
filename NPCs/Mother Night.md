# Mother Night

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- The entity to whom the shrine in the den is dedicated, and whose hoard includes artifacts like the *[[Items/Holy Symbol of Ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]]*.
- Cursed the hoard so that anything taken from it would kill its taker unless permission was granted.
- Players will encounter more references to [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]] throughout their journey through [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] sees her influence on [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]], who was captured by a werewolf serving [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]].
- Cult followers of Mother Night are linked to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s influence and supernatural activities.
- She is a divine figure for the "Ladies Three"—a cult or divine entity of darkness.
- Is connected to both the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] and dark forces that influence events in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Mother Night is the patron deity associated with lycanthropy and magic rituals.
- A deity (also known as a goddess) worshipped by the [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]] as a patron.
- Has sent rats each night bearing scraps for [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]] and [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] in their cages.
- Is invoked by [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]], who speaks with reverence about Mother Night’s wrath.
- A greater goddess whose whisper led the [[NPCs/Dreamer|Dreamer]] into corruption.
- Played a key role in transforming the [[NPCs/Dreamer|Dreamer]] into [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]].
- Symbolizes the source of evil that permeates the land.
- Personifies darkness.

## DM Notes

- Mother Night cult is tied to the broader supernatural forces affecting players' curse.
