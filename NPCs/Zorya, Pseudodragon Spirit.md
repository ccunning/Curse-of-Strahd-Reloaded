# Zorya, Pseudodragon Spirit

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain, and takes force damage when ending her turn inside an object.
- Can speak telepathically to any creature within 100 feet that understands a language, and can see through darkness better than humans.
- Has abilities like Ghostfire, Ethereal Escape (once per day), Misty Step, and Breathing Silver Fire in a cone.
- Informs players about how to access other spirits by guiding them through different rooms of the mansion.
- A pseudodragon spirit who appears in the story and speaks to players through the stained-glass windows.
- Has the ability to teleport, communicate telepathically, and assist in battle using her breath weapon.
- She is associated with the story of the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] prophecy and may have connections to ancient truths.
- Eventually reveals herself to be a source of silver fire and becomes a companion to the players.
- Hides in various places within the mansion as part of a game of hide-and-seek with players.
- A tiny undead pseudodragon spirit with immunity to most damage types and conditions.
- Lives within the spirit realm of [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] but may interact with living parties.
- A deceased pseudodragon familiar of [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]] who is now a playful but shy spirit.
- Her presence helps activate elements needed to summon [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]'s guidance.
- She can breathe silver fire and lights torches.

## DM Notes

- Serves as a key helper for navigating the mansion, aids in discovering other spirits; may play a part in accessing the gargoyle if players have her help.
