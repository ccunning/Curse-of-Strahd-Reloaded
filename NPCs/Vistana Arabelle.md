# Vistana Arabelle

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- A child whose bloodline connects to the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]] and whose presence is sought by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Needed to complete the ritual for escape, as a point of attack against the barriers.
- She is central to his plan for controlling access to the Fanes.

## DM Notes

- She will be hunted down by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s spies and becomes essential in achieving his escape.
