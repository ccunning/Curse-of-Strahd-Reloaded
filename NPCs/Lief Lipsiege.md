# Lief Lipsiege

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Mentions knowledge of the [[Places/Treasury|treasury]] and its contents when questioned, referencing “the old girl.”.
- Known for his scattered mind and memory holes but provides information on castle history.
- Placed within the hierarchy to manage castle affairs during the reign of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] accountant who embraced the new authority under [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Served a symbolic function in legitimizing Strahd’s return to power.
- Has knowledge about the poker mechanism in the third-floor study.
- Transformed from a living man into undying servant by [[NPCs/Khazan|Khazan]].
- Recognized Strahd as having full control over the land now.
- A wight serving as Strahd’s former accountant.
- Demonstrated loyalty and willingness to serve.

## DM Notes

- His knowledge of the castle's hidden treasures may lead to opportunities for treasure hunting or accessing valuable artifacts.
- His loyalty may affect how other characters approach authority or decisions related to governance or structure.
- Lief’s ramblings provide narrative clues about other lost characters like [[NPCs/Tatyana Federovna|Tatyana Federovna]].
