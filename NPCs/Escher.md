- Has knowledge about the prophecy regarding the descendant of the Ladies Three.
- Appears during the dinner when the players meet various consorts in the foyer.
- Has a secret arrangement with [[NPCs/Yan|Yan]] to find one of the Ladies' descendants.
- Displays jealousy toward the players and resentment for Strahd's favor.
- A symbol of Strahd's continued desire to attract new, capable servants.
- Lived in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] and served as Strahd’s consort and cupbearer.
