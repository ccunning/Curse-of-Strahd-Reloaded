# Quill

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- [[NPCs/Quill|Quill]] is frightened that his magical shield may have failed, allowing him to be vulnerable to magic and destruction.
- [[NPCs/Quill|Quill]] requests that someone place his feather in a lead and amber lined box to preserve his essence.
- Quill is a sentient quill who was granted flesh and sentience by the Archmage [[NPCs/Mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]].
- Quill seeks out a powerful transmutation mage who can help restore his life and soul.
- Quill has been guarding [[NPCs/Mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]]'s tower for over a year after his master left.

## DM Notes

- The note indicates that Quill might be seeking to be brought back to life using a spell by Mordenkainen, which could involve magical research or rare components.
- Quill's transformation implies the presence of powerful mages like Mordenkainen, and the potential for restoration magic.
