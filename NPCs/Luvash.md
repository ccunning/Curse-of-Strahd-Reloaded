# Luvash

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- A gruff man dedicated to protecting his family and people but driven by grief over [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]]'s disappearance.
- Is the elder brother of [[NPCs/Arrigal|Arrigal]] and one of the two leaders in the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] camp outside [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
- Hired the players to recover a clue from [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir]], related to [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]]'s disappearance.
- Has concerns over his family’s safety, especially regarding [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s influence.
- Wounded after being caught in a wolf trap during Arabelle's search.
- The head of the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] camp outside [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]], Arabelle’s father.
- A gruff and devoted father who protects his daughter Arabelle.
- Doesn't know that [[NPCs/Arrigal|Arrigal]] works as a spy for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
