# Elvir Martikov

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Has a strong resentment towards his father and believes [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]] would have preferred not to fight in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Believes his father was wrong to blame [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin]] for the theft, and that [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]]'s death is a waste.
- Shows signs of melancholy, but also sharp sarcasm and quick-tempered outbursts.
- Younger son of [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian Martikov]] and twin brother to Elric.
