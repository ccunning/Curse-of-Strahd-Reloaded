# Fyodor

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Child imprisoned within the windmill's third floor by [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]]'s coven along with [[NPCs/Myrtle|Myrtle]], and is held as leverage to obtain the contract.
- In an alternate version of events, Freek is noted as being named [[NPCs/Fyodor|Fyodor]] in this text, indicating he is part of the same group of children.
- Son of Franze, a Barovian refugee whose fate ties into the broader campaign lore regarding [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s manipulation.
- [[NPCs/Fyodor|Fyodor]] and his sister are not interested in the larger narrative but desire only to reunite with their father.
- The players may be tasked with rescuing them from the windmill or negotiating over their release.

## DM Notes

- Fyodor and [[NPCs/Myrtle|Myrtle]] serve as hostages whose potential fates are tied to key decisions made by the party, especially regarding [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s schemes.
