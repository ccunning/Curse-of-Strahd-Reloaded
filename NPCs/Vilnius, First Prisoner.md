# Vilnius, First Prisoner

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Has reactions including Illusionist's Trick (swapping places with an illusionary ally) and Horrifying Visage (frightening creatures).
- Uses dagger attacks with necrotic damage that poisons the target, as well as illusion spells like major image and phantasmal force.
- Vilnius has been trapped in the [[Places/Lecture Hall|Lecture Hall]] within [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] for centuries, transformed into a shadowy undead form.
- He orchestrated a murder at a dinner party by poisoning his master [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]], which led to the birth of [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]].
- Possesses a Shadowed Restoration ability allowing him to reform if [[NPCs/Jakarion|Jakarion]] is still alive, reviving with all hit points.
- He became the apprentice of [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]], a senior mage of [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]], and later ascended to be archmage.
- The main prisoner of [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]] in the Amber Temple, representing a major part of the temple's history.
- Possesses a quasit familiar named [[NPCs/Jakarion|Jakarion]], and both are bound together so neither fully dies or can escape.
- Was trapped inside the temple by the Dark Powers after his death, driven mad by insecurity and jealousy.
- A neutral evil undead creature with the abilities of an illusionist prodigy and amorphous movement.
- Can concentrate on up to five illusions at once and cast find familiar ritual without components.
- His spirit has been imprisoned within the [[Places/Lecture Hall|lecture hall]] where he was ultimately starved to death.
- He seeks Exethanter's staff kept in the [[Places/Shrine of Temptation|Shrine of Temptation]] as a means to escape his [[Places/Prison|prison]].
- His spirit haunts the Amber Temple and is the one that anchors the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] around the castle.
- Wears an amulet shaped like an upside-down “V,” originally belonging to Exethanter.
- Vilnius was originally an orphaned urchin who learned magic from pilfered scrolls.
- A former apprentice of Exethanter who poisoned him along with other mages.
- He attempts to take control of the [[Items/Staff of Frost|staff of frost]] through deception.
- A former apprentice of Exethanter who grew paranoid and killed him.

## DM Notes

- His amulet is needed to free the spirits in the Dining Hall; he is connected to a greater plot to destroy the barriers around [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Vilnius's story ties into the history of the Dark Powers and how they emerged from the Amber Temple's corruption.
- His presence could impact future events within the Amber Temple as players encounter other spirits or ghosts.
- Vilnius represents the corruption that permeated the ancient wizard society.
