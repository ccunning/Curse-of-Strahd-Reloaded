# Kala Krezkova

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- She was resurrected by the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] as the [[Items/Flesh Golem|flesh golem]] [[Items/Vasilka|Vasilka]]'s heart, though this changed her mentally.
- She acts strangely in the aftermath of her resurrection and expresses fear about darkness.
- She is the eight-year-old daughter of [[NPCs/Baron Dmitri Krezkov|Dmitri]] and [[NPCs/Anna Krezkova|Anna]] Krezkov, killed by a werewolf.
- She believes she saw the monster in the underground cellar and wants to confront it.
- She is haunted by dark dreams of death and the monster that killed her.
