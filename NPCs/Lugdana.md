# Lugdana

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Fell in battle while defeating demon prince [[NPCs/Chernovog|Chernovog]], dying from a grievous wound but not before vanquishing the demon and losing the *[[Items/Holy Symbol of Ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]]*.
- Recovered the *[[Items/Sigil of the Sun|Sigil of the Sun]]* from [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] with help from [[NPCs/Ismark Antonovich|Ismark Antonovich]] and [[NPCs/Livius Martikov|Livius Martikov]].
- A paladin of the Morninglord who led a campaign against vampire nests in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Led the fight against the [[Factions/Cult of Chernovog|Cult of Chernovog]] at [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
- Founded [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]] with [[NPCs/Livius Martikov|Livius]] [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]].

## DM Notes

- Her defeat of [[NPCs/Chernovog|Chernovog]] sets up future events involving the loss and mystery of the holy symbol.
- The *[[Items/Sigil of the Sun|Sigil of the Sun]]* she discovered later becomes known as the *[[Items/Holy Symbol of Ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]]*.
