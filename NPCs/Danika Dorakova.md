> **NPC** · 7 source files

## Key Facts

- Their impending nuptials caused tensions that led to a dramatic betrayal when one of the enchanted gems from the winery was stolen.
- Engaged to [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin Martikov]], son of [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian Martikov]], and their romance sparked jealousy and eventually marriage.
- [[NPCs/Danika Dorakova|Danika Dorakova]] is a character at the [[Places/Blue Water Inn|Blue Water Inn]] who informs players about [[NPCs/Ernst Larnak|Ernst Larnak]]'s presence.
- In combat, she fights fiercely to protect her family or guests—using negotiation first before fighting.
- She recognizes Ernst as the cloaked figure from the previous evening who delivered the invitation.
- Plays an active role in narrating the town lore and providing information about other characters.
- She later left the winery along with [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin]], leading them to build the [[Places/Blue Water Inn|Blue Water Inn]] in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
- Eventually became central figure for keeping secrets within [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- Her role supports linking the two events and helps transition into the main mission.
- Was the object of Urwin's suspicion during the theft of a gemstone from the winery.
- A helper in locating the players' destination during their visit to Blinsky Toys.
- Provides some comedic warning about beasts around the area near [[NPCs/Rictavio|Rictavio]]'s wagon.
- Has a strong dedication to her family and is fiercely loyal to those she loves.
- She runs the inn alongside her husband with a practical, no-nonsense approach.
- Assists in the rescue by working with wereravens to save [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] from the inn.
- Urwin [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]]'s betrothed who left with him along with his son in the past.
- Co-owner of the Blue Water Inn with Urwin, loyal to the player characters.
- She is the wife of Urwin [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]] and mother of two sons, Brom and Bray.
- A patron of the Blue Water Inn who can provide details about [[NPCs/Rictavio|Rictavio]].
- A native of [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] who can direct the characters to the toy shop.
- Works as a server in the taproom, responsible for hospitality.

- One of those capable of giving directions through the town.
- Works alongside Urwin to shelter [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] from [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]]’s search.
