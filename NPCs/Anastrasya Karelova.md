# Anastrasya Karelova

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- Has two forms: a human socialite form with charm abilities, and an abomination form with blood vomit and horrific screech.
- She can use charms, telekinesis, and attack her enemies with claws or flying sword in social and combat situations.
- [[NPCs/Anastrasya Karelova|Anastrasya]] is [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s third-youngest bride, a cunning and charming manipulator with a hidden agenda.
- Recognizes [[Items/Holy Symbol of Ravenkind|the Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]] on a player and shows clear recognition in her mannerisms.
- Has knowledge about her past that connects to ancient history involving old enemies like [[NPCs/Lugdana|Lugdana]].
- Desires to undermine [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]], [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]], and the players as competitors for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s affections.
- She has two forms: Vampire Socialite (undead humanoid, unaligned) and Vampiric Abomination.
- In her second form, [[NPCs/Anastrasya Karelova|Anastrasya]] gains bite attacks and the ability to vomit blood in a cone.
- She is found guarding the prisoner with four wight guards in the king's apartments.
- Anastrasya Karelova is a vampire who holds [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]] captive in the castle.
- Known for her loyalty and beauty, possibly involved in various intrigues.
- She has been placed to watch different regions to keep track of events.
- Fights to the death and unwilling to face Strahd’s wrath if she fails.
- Attacks the players when they attempt to leave with [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]].
- One of Strahd's vampiric brides; his third favored bride.
- She resides atop the castle in the King's Apartments.
- Flanked by two [[Factions/Wights|wights]] and one hidden wight in combat.
- Charismatic socialite with a manipulative nature.
- A vampire socialite guarding Gertruda's chambers.
- Another of Strahd's vampiric brides.

## DM Notes

- Her manipulative behavior might later reveal additional layers or agendas, especially if involving her hidden conflict with [[NPCs/Sasha Ivliskova|Sasha]].
- Her character plays a significant role in setting up potential conflicts between players and other consorts.
