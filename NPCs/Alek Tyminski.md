# Alek Tyminski

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Mentioned the 'stink of amber' before dying, suggesting deeper ties to cursed artifacts.
- Used an *[[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]]* to gain power and kill his neighbor during a lovers' quarrel.
- [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] has condemned him to die by execution as punishment for his crimes.
- His body is grotesquely disfigured due to possession by [[Factions/Dark Powers|dark powers]].
- A Vallakian butcher convicted of murder.

## DM Notes

- The presence of amber in the story hints at [[Factions/Dark Powers|dark powers]] or artifacts yet to be revealed.
- His fate sets precedent for how [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] handles those who challenge his justice.
