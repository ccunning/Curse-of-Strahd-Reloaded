# Clovin Belview

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- His ancestors came to seek a cure for a congenital illness that left them physically frail and prematurely aged in appearance.
- [[NPCs/Clovin Belview|Clovin Belview]] is a servant of the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]; he is the grand-nephew and -niece of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s butler, [[NPCs/Cyrus Belview|Cyrus Belview]].
- He is confident his own turn to be perfected will come next due to his long-standing loyalty.
- He believes the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] will soon complete the 'perfection' process of his family.
- He is skeptical of [[NPCs/Otto and Zygfrek Belview|Otto]]'s leaving his post at the gate.
