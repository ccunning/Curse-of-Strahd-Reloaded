# Beucephalus

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- The nightmare steed atop which [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] rides during his fortnightly departures from [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Has 104 hit points and an 'Ethereal Escape' feature that allows it to use 'ethereal stride'.
- Is a crucial part of the lore explaining [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s activities outside of the castle.
- Strahd's nightmare, ridden by him during an encounter on the path to [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
- Used in pursuit of answers during Strahd's wanderings through [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- [[NPCs/Beucephalus|Beucephalus]] is a nightmare that Strahd rides when he departs [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- He mounts [[NPCs/Beucephalus|Beucephalus]] before departing the castle for his night-time journey.
- Symbolized the loss of Strahd's humanity and his bond with darker forces.
- It is an essential component in his flying form during nightly patrols.
- A legendary horse previously owned by Strahd and known as Wonder-Horse.
- Has been involved in Strahd's strategic use of deception and testing.
- Serves as a steed for Strahd and represents his formidable presence.
- Used by Strahd to travel for unknown purposes each new moon night.
- Strahd's nightmare, a large night-black horse used in his ritual.
- Its remains are floating in the center of [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|the Heart of Sorrow]].
- Favored pet and most valued tool for transportation or battle.
- Strahd’s nightmare steed, depicted in the Crypt 39 (p. 93).
- Represented a key part of Strahd’s early vampiric journey.
- Became a demonic nightmare after Strahd's transformation.
- Part of Strahd's elite companions in combat or travel.
- Carries Strahd's animated armor in its saddlebags.
- Appears both at [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]]'s Cairn and [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
- Sacrificed by Strahd for his escape plan.
- Strahd's nightmare steed.

## DM Notes

- Players may learn more about Beucephalus' role when Strahd returns or during his absences.
- May play a role in key strategic maneuvers, especially in combat situations.
