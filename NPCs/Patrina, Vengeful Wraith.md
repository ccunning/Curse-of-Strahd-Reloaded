# Patrina, Vengeful Wraith

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Has two forms – Vengeful Wraith (incorporeal) and Half-[[NPCs/Lich|Lich]] (solid), with the ability to become a [[NPCs/Lich|lich]] with shadowblades, life drain, crackling doom, negative energy burst, and abilities such as Parry and Fell Rebuke.
- Has reactions including Indomitable (repeating saves), Counterspell, Ghostly Walk, Amber Tentacle, and is tied to amber sarcophagi.
- Possesses an incorporeal form that can move through objects but takes force damage if she ends her turn inside one.
- Wields a powerful artifact known as the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] which she can charm wielders with if they possess it.
- A neutral evil undead creature who can animate golems, create sarcophagi, and counterspell.
