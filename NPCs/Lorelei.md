# Lorelei

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Tells a false story of being abducted and trapped alone, with a twisted ankle she claims is due to a demon attack.
- Possesses [[NPCs/Nardag|Nardag]]'s greatsword hidden in an alcove; changes form to a toad-like slaad upon attacking.
- Reveals her true nature when players attack her, challenge her identity, or take her greatsword.
- A disguised death slaad ([[NPCs/Nardag|Nardag]]) posing as a lost woman in the West [[Places/Catacombs|Catacombs]].
- Fights alongside Nardag as the battle progresses.

## DM Notes

- She is directly connected to the West [[Places/Catacombs|Catacombs]] encounter and triggers the presence of multiple enemies.
