# Madame Irene Radanavich

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Played an instrumental role in setting up the conflict between Rudolph and the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] tribes.
- Died in combat against Rudolph during his pursuit into [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], cursing him before death.
- Mastermind behind kidnapping [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus van Richten]] to take revenge on Rudolph.
- Influenced future events by forcing Rudolph into a quest to save [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus]].
- Leader of the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] [[Factions/Radanavich Clan|Radanavich clan]] who allied with Baron Metus.
