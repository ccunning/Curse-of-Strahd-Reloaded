# Davian Martikov

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- His life reflected deeper themes involving family loyalty, destiny, and the fate of artifacts tied to supernatural forces.
- Lives in a hidden cavern beneath the winery with his family due to an invasion by druids known as the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]].
- Asks the players to defeat the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] and break the staff controlling the blights to reclaim the winery.
- His actions contributed to a turning point in family dynamics regarding legacy and divine intervention.
- His son [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin]] played crucial roles in both personal and prophetic matters linking him to heroes.
- Instructs the players to retrieve the stolen enchanted gemstone that was used in grape growing.
- His grandfather was [[NPCs/Livius Martikov|Livius Martikov]]—a historical figure involved in stealing relics.
- Has a long history with the druids of [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] and their enmity with the winery.
- Blamed his son [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin]] for the theft of a gemstone from the winery ten years prior.
- Founded his group after years of resistance against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] through espionage.
- He overheard a prophetic [[Items/Tarokka reading|Tarokka reading]] involving [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and the artifacts.
- Banished his son [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]] for defying orders to not fight in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Patriarch of the [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov family]] and leader of [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- The patriarch of the [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov family]] and owner of [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]].
- Provides magic items including the [[Items/Keeper's Whistle|Keeper's Whistle]] to help players.
- Led the struggle within his family over the theft of winery gems.
- The father of Urwin, Adrian, Elvir, and [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric Martikov]].
- Reveals information to players after saving the winery.
- Leader of [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]] and a wereraven.
- Descendant of [[NPCs/Livius Martikov|Livius Martikov]], father of Urwin.
- Elder wereraven and leader of the Keepers.

## DM Notes

- [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian]] serves as a guide and helper for players, but his own identity as wereraven could be important later.
- The prophecy involving Strahd may foreshadow upcoming threats or challenges.
- His family's legacy impacts current and future storylines.
