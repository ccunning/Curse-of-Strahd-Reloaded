# Father Donavich

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- Journeyed to [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] in search of [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]]'s betrothed, [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]], who is believed trapped in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Willing to fight to protect his son despite being perceived as a threat due to his son's vampiric nature.
- In his current state, he lacks the resolve to fight for himself but is driven by guilt and obsession.
- Known as the father of [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]], a young man who led an ill-fated rebellion against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s regime.
- Has been a significant support figure in understanding spiritual dynamics within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Cleric of the church who was attacked by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s zombies during the siege of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Has worked with Doru in repairing the church after his transformation into a vampire.
- He is a grieving, zealous father whose son Doru is transformed into a [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]].
- He maintains roles as the priest of the [[Places/Barovian Church|Barovian Church]] and father of Doru.
- Represented local faith and resistance in early conflicts with Strahd.
- His church became the target of punishment after the failed uprising.
- [[NPCs/Father Donavich|Donavich]] wants to free his son from Strahd’s clutches at any cost.
- Meets Doru in Barovia and helps reconcile him with his nature.
- Indirectly connected to key figures such as [[NPCs/Escher|Escher]] and Doru.
- Barovian priest, father of Doru the [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]].
- Priest who is the father of vampire spawn Doru.

## DM Notes

- Their search for [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]] could lead players to new areas or revelations about the castle’s secrets.
- His relationship with his son Doru provides emotional weight and background to certain story arcs.
- His suffering during the siege indicates an ongoing struggle for faith within the region.
