# Greater Strix

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Introduced both as greater and [[NPCs/Lesser Strix|lesser strix]] to provide opportunities for interaction.
- A type of creature mentioned in the campaign as relevant to combatting wereravens.
- [[NPCs/Muriel Vinshaw|Muriel]] is a raven who has been added to foreshadow [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- It uses the statistics of a manticore but is vulnerable to fire damage.
- A construct made of wood, skin, and feathers resembling a manticore.
- A type of magical bird creature created or aided by [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]].
- Part of the broader ecosystem involving ravens and lycanthropy.
- Deployed in the skies above the winery during conflicts.
- Has the ability to release silver barbs from its wings.
- Connected with [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] and druids of [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].

## DM Notes

- Foreshadows [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s prophecy and [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]]'s motivations.
- Introduces [[NPCs/Muriel Vinshaw|Muriel]] as a potential ally.
