# Arrigal

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Actively avoiding the players after giving them the note, possibly fearing being caught or blamed by the vampire court.
- Has knowledge of the female [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]] who assured him he wouldn’t be turned in—implying possible double agents.
- Knows that there is a traitor among [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s consorts but does not initially reveal the identity directly.
- Secretly tracks the players in search of [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]] to ensure she is safely returned.
- Tracks players and appears at the end to help retrieve [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]] from Van Richten.
- Provides the note indicating a spy in the court after players rescue Arabelle.
- A spy for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and reports to [[NPCs/Anastrasya Karelova|Anastrasya]], one of Strahd’s vampiric brides.
- Provides a diplomatic challenge for the players to mediate between factions.
- Knows not about Strahd's plot but suspects that Van Richten stole Arabelle.
- Believes he has been commanded by his master to take Arabelle back to camp.
- A member of the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] tribe who is concerned for Arabelle's safety.
- One of the two leaders in the [[Places/Vistani Encampment|Vistani encampment]] outside [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
- Secretly dedicated to serving Strahd for his family's well-being.
- Has a complex relationship with loyalty, betrayal, and ambition.
- A former [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] leader who has been spying on behalf of Strahd.
- Serves as a spy for Strahd von Zarovich reporting to [[NPCs/Anastrasya Karelova|Anastrasya]].
- Arabelle’s uncle and [[NPCs/Luvash|Luvash]]'s younger brother.
- [[NPCs/Luvash|Luvash]]’s younger brother and Arabelle's uncle.

## DM Notes

- May return to provide more information or complications in future arcs involving Vistani and Strahd's agenda.

- Could play a role in future revelations about Strahd’s inner circle and possible rebellion.
