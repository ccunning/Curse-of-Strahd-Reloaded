# Kiril Stoyanovich

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- In his second (mutated) form, he gains increased physical strength, can swallow and regurgitate others, has a [[NPCs/Shadow|shadow]] beam breath attack, and causes creatures to fall prone when they move near him.
- A large monstrosity and chaotic evil werewolf who has multiple forms including human, dire wolf hybrid, and a grotesquely mutated form.
- Has regeneration that can be negated by silvered weapons, keen hearing and smell, and the ability to curse humans with lycanthropy.
- Possesses multiple reactions: Indomitable, Pounce, Claw, Bite, Savage Frenzy, Alpha's Howl, Nightmare Howl, and [[NPCs/Shadow|Shadow]] Beam.
- Suffers from lycanthropy corruption which affects his behavior, and the curse can be removed or increased via his abilities.
- Influenced certain characters with negative consequences leading into darker chapters of their histories.
- Participated in significant events where his actions impacted others including Van Richten’s students.
- Transformed with the help of [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] and [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s vampire brides, gaining immense strength.
- Eventually connected to larger themes involving supernatural betrayal within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Wears a key to the silverthorn collars on a tanned human sinew cord around his neck.
- His attack served as a major catalyst in shaping [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]]'s path and influence.
- A **werewolf** and the husband of [[NPCs/Bianca Stoyanovich|Bianca]], who seized power from [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil Toranescu]].
- Led a group that attacked and devoured innocent people to empower his curse.
- Imprisoned [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]] and [[NPCs/Skennis|Skennis]] in the den, forcing them to eat only scraps.
- A werewolf who bit off [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]]’s leg when she went on a rescue mission.
- A mutated werewolf who hunts the players throughout this arc.
- Part of the ongoing conflict in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
