> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Her presence reflects the emotional toll of the family crisis on both children and parents.
- Players may be informed indirectly about her role or fate in future narrative.
- She agreed to give her heart to the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] in exchange for [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala]]'s resurrection.
- She is part of the Krezk family facing a mysterious curse and disappearances.
- She hides her fear and guilt from others and seeks to protect her family.
- Family dynamics and secret histories are linked to her character arc.
- She is the wife of [[NPCs/Baron Dmitri Krezkov|Baron Dmitri Krezkov]] and mother of [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] and [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala]].
- Her actions are motivated by grief, guilt, and desire to save Kala.
- She is not a werewolf but shares the family's secret curse.
