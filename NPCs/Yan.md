# Yan

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Died in a confrontation with Van Richten while attempting to deliver [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]] to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Told of his loyalty to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]], the timing of his orders, and what he was ordered to do.
- The death of [[NPCs/Yan|Yan]] signals a turning point in the campaign and sets off new events.
- Used a [[Items/Talisman of Echoes|talisman of echoes]] to interrogate him about Strahd's interest in [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]].
- Was present at the moment of Arabelle's kidnapping, working closely with [[NPCs/Escher|Escher]].
- Was captured by Van Richten after being seen bringing Arabelle to the riverbank.
- Served under [[NPCs/Escher|Escher]] and was assigned the task of kidnapping Arabelle.
- Is revealed to be one of Strahd’s key agents tracking descendants.
- A Vistana spy who works for Strahd with Escher.
- A Vistana thief who worked for Strahd.

## DM Notes

- His role as agent of knowledge; influence on future events tied to discovery of other spies.
- Key evidence of Strahd's intentions; crucial in resolving conflict involving Arabelle.
