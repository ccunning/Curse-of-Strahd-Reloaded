# Chernovog

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Was ultimately vanquished, but its presence was feared and it played a major role in the darkening of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Its defeat marked a turning point in history that affected future generations.
- Fought and defeated by [[NPCs/Lugdana|Lugdana]] and her allies in the battle at [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
- Summoned to [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] in an attempt by the cult to overthrow [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- A demon prince worshipped by the [[Factions/Cult of Chernovog|Cult of Chernovog]].

## DM Notes

- Its summoning sets up potential consequences or reappearances related to dark divine forces.
