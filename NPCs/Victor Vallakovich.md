# Victor Vallakovich

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- Equipped with advanced spells, notably *greater invisibility*, *counterspell* (at 3rd or 4th level, and potentially 5th under updated rules), *magic missile*, *remove curse* (not used in this guide), and specific preparation for combat as outlined for the 2014 version.
- Searches for an artifact named after [[NPCs/Khazan|Khazan]] to rescue his friend Stella Wachter from the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] but ultimately misleads players about its whereabouts.
- Is present in the war council, advocating caution against immediate action and stating he expects [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] to take Lady Wachter if they act rashly.
- Has a spellbook which differs in content from standard versions, including *sending* and *fear* spells with an absence of *remove curse*.
- Warns against early spending of 5th-level spell slots before battle with hags because of their potential ability to dispel enchantments.
- Maintains a critical role during the binding circle ritual, staying close to the windmill as its guardian due to protection needs.
- Feels betrayed by Lady Wachter for failing to protect Stella and becomes a bitter presence in her life and the group's future.
- Fears losing Stella's friendship partly due to his [[Items/Teleportation Circle|teleportation circle]] and his time spent with [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus]] in the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]].
- Player ally who offers assistance in the raid on [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]] via teleportation (misty step, fly) and invisibility spells.
- Is determined to help Stella return to her body and has been studying rituals and methods for entering the Ethereal Plane.
- He played a minor but significant part in events involving Stella Wachter's curse and her spirit sent to Ethereal Plane.
- Created a [[Items/Teleportation Circle|teleportation circle]] with Stella that inadvertently caused her soul to be exposed to the Ethereal Plane.
- A figure of contention within the Wachter family, especially regarding his relationship with Stella Wachter.
- Believes he can repurpose an existing pentagram for the etherealness ritual if Lady Wachter is not present.
- Appears aged in the future dream showing how his resentment and isolation impact his life post-departure.
- A lonely, isolated young man suffering from a mysterious illness and haunted by his father’s harshness.
- A teenage son of the Baron of [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] who has been working with [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus]] and Stella to restore her soul.
- Inherited magic abilities from the early study of the [[NPCs/Lich|lich]] [[NPCs/Khazan|Khazan]]'s spellbook in his father’s [[Places/Library|library]].
- Connected to larger narrative elements involving the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]], prophecy, and supernatural evolution.
- Possesses a spellbook belonging to the [[NPCs/Lich|lich]] Khazan, which he used to research teleportation magic.
- Knows that his spellbook has a sigil matching the signet ring, possibly linking him with Khazan.
- Has access to spells like Cone of Cold and is a potentially dangerous opponent if cornered.
- Uses his *mage hand* spell to hold open '[[Items/Ethereal Entities|Ethereal Entities]]' during the etherealness ritual.
- Can be a resource for information or may act as an obstacle if discovered by the players.
- He is in contact with Erasmus van Richten’s ghost who influences his actions indirectly.
- Has become desperate as time runs short, fearing Stella's soul may fully dissipate soon.
- Has a strong connection with Stella Wachter and appears jealous of Erasmus's presence.
- His learning accelerated during times of unrest and influence by [[Factions/Dark Powers|dark powers]].
- Has a copy of the spellbook '[[Items/Ethereal Entities|Ethereal Entities]]' and uses it during rituals.
- A mage who helps players perform rituals to restore Stella Wachter's soul.
- Is involved in the mansion's defense and knows about the secret passages.
- Friend of Stella Wachter and has a connection with Erasmus van Richten.
- Son of Baron Valentin Vallakovich and [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]].
- Son of the Baron, found in his workroom at the mansion.
- Offers to help in fighting against [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]]’s captor.
- Son of Baron Vargas and [[NPCs/Baroness Lydia Petrovna|Baroness Lydia Petrovna]].
- Despises [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]], who once killed his cat.
- [[NPCs/Baron Vargas Vallakovich|Baron Vargas Vallakovich]]'s only child.
