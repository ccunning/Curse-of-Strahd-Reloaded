# Khazan

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s arcane advisor who conducted deep research on lichdom and the magical energies in [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- He was deeply involved in understanding and manipulating the Amber Corruptions that affected many entities.
- Investigated [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|the Heart of Sorrow]] within [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]], which became central to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s escape plan.
- Studied the effects of the amber sarcophagi and their connections to vestiges like [[NPCs/The Vampyr|the Vampyr]].
- Ultimately fell to lichdom but was slain by Strahd himself, sealing his skull in a crypt.
- Had his own spellbook bearing the personal sigil, which matches that of the signet ring.
- His infant child died in childbirth two years after [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] was trapped in the [[Places/Mists|Mists]].
- Trained by Strahd in the arcane arts and eventually became one of his key magicians.
- His notes helped Strahd use the Fanes’ power to channel into [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|the Heart of Sorrow]].
- Van Richten has hidden himself in this tower, seeking to protect [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]].
- An ancient wizard whose tower was abandoned and located at [[Places/Lake Baratok|Lake Baratok]].
- The staff that he owned is now hidden in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] under Strahd.
- The tower door contains runes representing his eight schools of magic.
- His legacy influences the players' quest to find artifacts and clues.
- Built a powerful magical tower imbued with protective enchantments.
- An archmage and advisor to Strahd, who helped build his castle.
- Created the tower with an antimagic field to hide from Strahd.
- Built his own tower at [[Places/Lake Baratok|Lake Baratok]] with magical properties.
- A powerful archmage whose tower is located on Lake Baratok.
- Married Isabella and had children.

## DM Notes

- His research ties heavily into the lore of [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]]’s deeper powers and how they relate to Strahd's schemes.
- His tower will host the climax event involving the players’ mission; possible return of powerful artifact.
- Mysterious origin and magical prowess; key to understanding the history of the tower.
