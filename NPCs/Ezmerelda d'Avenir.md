# Ezmerelda d'Avenir

> **NPC** · 11 source files

## Key Facts

- Wields a +1 rapier, a +1 handaxe, a +1 silvered shortsword, and a hand crossbow; carries potions of greater healing, vials of holy water, wooden stakes, silvered crossbow bolts, and a runeguard stone.
- Former student of Dr. Rudolph van Richten and daughter of the d'Avenirs who kidnapped and sold his son, [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus]], to the vampire Baron Metus.
- [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]] has a prosthetic leg replacing her missing right limb, which she obtained during an earlier adventure with the [[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]].
- She is reluctant to share personal details until trust is established, but willing to cooperate after proving herself trustworthy.
- A 5th-level ranger with proficiency in Acrobatics, Arcana, Deception, Medicine, Perception, Performance, Stealth, and Survival.
- A cool, suave, professional monster hunter who is also a fiery, wise-cracking, and eternally loyal friend to those she trusts.
- Later plays a key role in helping guide adventurers through [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] due to her own knowledge of the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] traditions.
- [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]] is a Vistana, a former student of Dr. Rudolph van Richten and daughter of a family that kidnapped his son.
- Is irritated if players steal from her wagon, but is willing to help if they apologize or return stolen items.
- Her goal includes contacting restless spirits in the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]] via seance and investigating the [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]].
- Has a history with Dr. Rudolph van Richten and was formerly a [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] before becoming a monster hunter.
- Is identified as someone who traveled with the original group of players before the events of this arc.
- Owns a magic wagon that contains various adventuring supplies, including kits, scrolls, and artifacts.
- A character who joins the party during the dream and is part of those in the vision of future [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Wounded by a werewolf, [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril Stoyanovich]], which resulted in loss of leg and use of prosthetic craft.
- Selected as a replacement ally to avoid overpowered or underpowered options in the original module.
- She came to the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]] seeking knowledge about the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]'s corruption to save [[NPCs/Anna Krezkova|Anna]] Krezkov’s life.
- Eventually fled from Barovia after Van Richten told her the truth about the curse of the Vistani.
- Can cast longstrider, darkvision, cordon of arrows, and magic circle (with the runeguard stone).
- The wagon was parked near [[Places/Lake Baratok|Lake Baratok]] where it was observed by players with tracks of [[NPCs/Wolves|wolves]].
- She aided them in the past and has returned seeking [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]]'s rescue and to stop the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]].
- Has a pet chicken named Eggsmerelda who speaks and can provide information about her.
- Is involved in the future world's peace and the continuation of Barovia’s prosperity.
- Is present when [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] makes her choice and may influence her decision in the dream.
- She is planning a seance at the Abbey if she gains more information about the Abbot.
- Provides spell scrolls, spyglass, and silvered crossbow bolts for the infiltration.
- Recognizes the wanderer’s scarf and calls players family, saying they are 'cousin'.
- Returned during times of peril with others and attempted to track down Van Richten.
- Shares knowledge about [[Items/Tarokka Cards|Tarokka cards]] (the Shepherd) during the Abbot's redemption.
- Was attacked and had her leg bitten off by the Black Terror ([[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril Stoyanovich]]).
- Informs players that Ezmerelda will seek vengeance for breaking into her wagon.
- Ezmerelda supports players by providing useful resources to aid in their quest.
- Young woman who sought out Dr. Rudolph van Richten and became his apprentice.
- Provides a hook to Krezk and the Abbey of [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]], unlocking quests.
- She provides information on keeping guards distracted during infiltration.
- The wagon has traps inside including silver barbed nets and fire bottles.
- She will likely join the players' quest against the Abbot once they meet.
- Serves as a guide, advisor or companion to the players in their journey.
- She freely offers holy water as a tool to counteract [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]]'s powers.
- She has had a history with [[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]], particularly Kiril Stoyanovich.
- A key figure for familial bonds and recognition of shared heritage.
- A monster hunter who leads the party to locate the [[Places/Werewolf Den|werewolf den]].
- She is a monster hunter who is familiar with the Krezkov family.
- A legendary monster hunter and ally of Dr. Rudolph van Richten.
- She knows that Ezmerelda is looking for something in the woods.
- Monster hunter recruited from the town of Krezk in Act III.
- Assigned as [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s Enemy in the [[Items/Tarokka reading|Tarokka reading]].
- Can speak with animals and is loyal to Ezmerelda.
- She does not know where Ezmerelda currently is.
- A pet chicken belonging to Ezmerelda d'Avenir.
- Encountered in [[Arc K - The Fallen Abbey]].
- Joined during the adventure's progression.
- Monster hunter and ally in combat.

## DM Notes

- She will be instrumental in planning and executing the seance that reveals information about [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]] and the artifact.
- She is one of the few characters that remains to help guide the players through their journey after the dream experience.
- Ezmerelda’s journey leads to her assisting players in investigating the [[Places/Werewolf Den|werewolf den]] as part of a larger task in Arc L.
- She serves as both a link to past history and a bridge to present events involving Van Richten.
- Will be mentioned as having an item related to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s bounty on [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]].
- May play a larger role involving the Vistana or extended family narratives.
- Uses her expertise to interpret the card as 'odd' due to its iconography.
- Arc B: Welcome to Barovia.
