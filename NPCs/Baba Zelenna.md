# Baba Zelenna

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- A witch who secretly founded the [[Factions/Cult of Chernovog|Cult of Chernovog]] after resenting [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]]'s leadership.
- Her followers were involved in creating chaos in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] that led to the downfall of others.
- Led a ritual at [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane at Yester Hill]], breaking [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s bond with the fane.
- Sought to summon the demon prince [[NPCs/Chernovog|Chernovog]] into [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] to overthrow [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Was instrumental in setting the events of the [[Places/Grand Conjunction|Grand Conjunction]] in motion.

## DM Notes

- The events she orchestrated set up an important antagonist and conflict leading into future adventures.
