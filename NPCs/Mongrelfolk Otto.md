# Mongrelfolk Otto

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- He and [[NPCs/Zygfrek|Zygfrek]] are described as eccentric and squabbling, with [[NPCs/Otto and Zygfrek Belview|Otto]] being more positive and [[NPCs/Zygfrek|Zygfrek]] more grumpy.
- He has a fascination for interesting things, is braying and over-enthusiastic, and can be a loudmouth.
- Represented in early stages of supernatural resurgence, helping advance the narrative forward.
- His personality traits include curiosity, excitement, annoyance, and satisfaction (emotions).
- Connected to developments involving [[Items/Flesh Golem|flesh golem]] creation and the quest for [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]’s future.
- Used to search cemeteries in an effort to gather remains and create magical constructs.
- Involved in activities related to grave robbing and the creation of living constructs.
- [[NPCs/Otto and Zygfrek Belview|Otto]] is a servant of the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] and cousin of [[NPCs/Clovin Belview|Clovin Belview]].
- His primary motivation is a desire for food and attention.
- Two mongrelfolk attending the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] of Krezk.

## DM Notes
