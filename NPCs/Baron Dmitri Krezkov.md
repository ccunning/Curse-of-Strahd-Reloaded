# Baron Dmitri Krezkov

> **NPC** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- He is a werewolf, born with lycanthropy that manifests on full moons after his thirteenth birthday.
- Baron Krezkov is known for being grim yet warm in public, but privately grieving and guilt-ridden.
- Tries to keep order in his community and protect loved ones from supernatural threats.
- Asks players to rescue his son [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] from the [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]] in exchange for sanctuary.
- Is aware of the events occurring in the [[Places/Werewolf Den|werewolf den]] and seeks help to resolve them.
- Has deep emotional connections with family, particularly regarding his missing son.
- His family's secret curse has been kept hidden from the village and their children.
- Resents [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil Toranescu]] for 'luring' his sister [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]] away from the village.
- He asks players to find his missing son, [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]], through [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]]'s scrying.
- Was mentioned in the first arc, where the players discovered Ilya's location.
- He is married to [[NPCs/Anna Krezkova|Baroness Anna Krezkova]], the mother of Ilya and [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala]] Krezkov.
- He has an elder sister [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika Toranescu]] who left Krezk fourteen years ago.
- [[NPCs/Baron Dmitri Krezkov|Dmitri]] is secretly a werewolf and deeply anxious about his family's curse.
- He has a wife named [[NPCs/Anna Krezkova|Anna Krezkova]] and a brother named Zuleika Toranescu.
- Baron Krezkov is the burgomaster of Krezk and father of Ilya and [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala]].
- He is the patriarch of the Krezkov family and burgomaster of Krezk.
- Leader of the [[Places/Village of Krezk|village of Krezk]] and a werewolf by secret identity.
- Maintains strong bonds with his wife Anna and brother Zuleika.
- The burgomaster of Krezk, father of Ilya and brother of Anna.
- Anxious about the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]'s intention to take Anna's heart.
- Has a sister Zuleika who also leads a [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]].
- Baron of the [[Places/Village of Krezk|village of Krezk]].

## DM Notes

- Baron Krezkov has hidden knowledge about family curse and a connection to the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]].
- Could be a participant in later plots involving lycanthropy or the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]].
