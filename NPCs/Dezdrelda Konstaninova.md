# Dezdrelda Konstaninova

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Her body was strung up in the [[Places/Wine Cellar|wine cellar]] of [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] after being exsanguinated for blood supply during dinner.
- His wife and the owner of the skull that [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]] plays with in [[Places/Torture Chamber|the torture chamber]].
- Connected only through her tragic fate as a wife and victim of [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]]’s wrath.
- [[NPCs/Anton Konstantinovich|Anton]]'s wife, tortured and ultimately killed by Volenta due to her cruelty.
- She died in the cellar, her blood drained to feed the vampires.
- Represents a dark part of [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]]'s moral landscape.
- She was drained for blood and fed to ghouls.
