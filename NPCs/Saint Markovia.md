# Saint Markovia

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Her spirit can be contacted during a seance with [[Items/Tarokka Cards|Tarokka cards]], where she communicates about the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]'s corruption.
- She marched against [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] but was destroyed by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]], causing many at the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]] to fall into madness.
- She is referenced as having once encountered [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and was present when his curse was revealed.
- Her spirit can be summoned using a Tarokka card (the Four of Glyphs: the Shepherd).
- Her spirit inspired a conflict between Strahd and his followers throughout history.
- Declared holy war on [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] after her death, but was slain by Strahd.
- [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]] was a priest and prophesied by an oracle named Tasha Petrovna.
- A monastery under the influence of [[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]], who is known as the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]].
- She founded [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|the Abbey of Saint Markovia]] as both a convent and hospital.
- She can cleanse players of [[Items/Amber Corruption|amber corruption]] through her pool/shrine.
- A priest of the Morninglord who built an abbey in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Opposed [[NPCs/Saint Andral|Saint Andral]]’s support for King Barov’s legacy.
- Her remains were buried beneath the [[Places/Castle Crypts|castle crypts]].
- Connected with religious themes and redemption.
- House where angel’s feather guides the player.
- The founder of the Abbey of St. [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Markovia]].

## DM Notes

- Saint Markovia’s role and knowledge become essential in understanding both past events and potential redemption of the Abbot.
- Her spirit appears in visions during the seance, giving insight into her relationship with [[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]] (the Abbot) and Strahd.
- Her presence is felt via visions in her pool and as a guiding spirit for the redeemed Abbot.
- Player encounters Abbot here or during [[Arc K - The Fallen Abbey]].
- The Abbot was once her chosen prophet.
