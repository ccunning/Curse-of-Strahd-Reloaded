# Offalia Wormwiggle

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Member of [[Factions/The Bonegrinder Coven|the Bonegrinder Coven]] and a [[NPCs/Night Hag|night hag]] who uses *Ray of Enfeeblement*, *Acid Arrow*, *Cause Fear*, *Bestow Curse*, and *Hold Person*.
- In the second phase as an elephant, uses a *Gore* attack along with *Stomp*, *Fiend’s Trumpet*, *Tusks*, and *Matron’s Rebuke* reactions.
- Known to be easily distracted and can be led to other areas of the windmill using illusions or performance checks.
- Attacks players with *Claws*, *Ray of Enfeeblement*, *Acid Arrow*, or *Ray of Sickness* attacks during combat.
- She uses her reaction to force creatures to fall into fits of laughter with maniacal mirth.
- In [[NPCs/The Nightmare Three|the Nightmare Three]] form, she becomes a Huge elephant and uses charge and stomp.
- She can cast ray of enfeeblement, cause fear and bestow curse as a bonus action.
- She has multiple spellcastings including ray of enfeeblement and bestow curse.
- Has access to *Melf's Acid Arrow* as a spell during its normal coven form.
- [[NPCs/Offalia Wormwiggle|Offalia]] is one of the three night hags forming [[Factions/The Bonegrinder Coven|the Bonegrinder Coven]].
