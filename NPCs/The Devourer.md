# The Devourer

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- The third sister of the three [[Factions/Ladies of the Fanes|Ladies of the Fanes]] who was sealed in an amber sarcophagus.
- [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] extracted her vestige and forcefully resealed it in [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|the Heart of Sorrow]].
- Her divine spark was sealed in amber at [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] near [[Places/Mount Ghakis|Mount Ghakis]].
- Hunted the spirits of [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]] and turned dreams into nightmares.
- Was slain by her sisters and her corpse became [[Places/Whispering Wall|the Whispering Wall]].
- A shadowed, twisted creature formed from the corrupted [[NPCs/Dreamer|Dreamer]].
- Spirits of [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]] were later sought by [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina Velikovna]].

## DM Notes

- The sliver of darkness floating in [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|the Heart of Sorrow]] is the vestige of [[NPCs/The Devourer|The Devourer]].
