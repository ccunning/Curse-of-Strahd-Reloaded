# Minodora Taltos

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- A poltergeist spirit who once served as a clerk to the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]] during the war with [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Knows about the phantom warriors and can guide the players toward [[NPCs/Brother Marek|Brother Marek]] for further information.
- Provides useful directions to the [[Places/Upstairs Gallery|upstairs gallery]] where [[NPCs/Brother Marek|Brother Marek]] haunts.
- Haunts the Knights' Quarters, leaving frost-written warnings about intruders.
- Can be convinced easily if players identify themselves as allies.

## DM Notes

- May reveal more clues on how to access or interact with [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]'s spirit and other helpful spirits.
