# Elric Martikov

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- A deceased character whose bloodstained bed and large ravens feather were found in the North Tower Peak.
- Died during a walk in the woods, according to [[NPCs/Muriel Vinshaw|Muriel]], who won't elaborate further on the circumstances.
- Considered by Elvir to be a fool who died fighting [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] despite having been advised against it.
- His death is connected to [[NPCs/Muriel Vinshaw|Muriel]]'s emotional state and sense of personal struggle.
- Died at [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] during the revolt, causing great sorrow for the family.
- Joined a rebellion in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]], going against his father’s orders.
- Was imprisoned during [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]]'s rebellion against Castle Ravenloft.
- Youngest son of [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian Martikov]] and twin brother to Elvir.
- The person who bestowed wereraven lycanthropy upon Muriel.
- Muriel's former betrothed and the twin brother of Elvir.
- His death was due to vivisection by [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla Vilisevic]].
- Was involved with [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- Muriel’s late fiancé.
