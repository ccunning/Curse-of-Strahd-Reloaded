# Ilya Krezkov

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- The missing son of Burgomaster [[NPCs/Baron Dmitri Krezkov|Dmitri Krezkov]] and [[NPCs/Anna Krezkova|Anna]] Krezkov, presumed lost in the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Wood]] near Krezk.
- He is connected through his mother and father to significant lore involving family legacy and betrayal.
- His discovery leads [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]] to investigate, resulting in a critical part of her quest to help [[NPCs/Anna Krezkova|Anna]].
- His capture or rescue becomes key to resolving conflict within the [[Places/Village of Krezk|village of Krezk]] and with [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Reveals knowledge of his curse, how the players know him, and what his parents know about it.
- [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] is [[NPCs/Baron Dmitri Krezkov|Baron Dmitri Krezkov]]'s elder son and the missing person sought in scrying spell.
- Believes he was cursed by his own father and blames a false [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]] for his condition.
- Found near [[Places/Mount Baratok|Mount Baratok]], in a cave that the [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]] uses for denning purposes.
- He became a dangerous werewolf after faking taking a wolfsbane potion and escaping.
- He believed the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]'s deception about werewolf heritage and his family's curse.
- According to [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]]’s scrying, [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] was captured and chained by a werewolf.
- The son of Baron Dmitri Krezkov, kidnapped and imprisoned in the [[Places/Werewolf Den|werewolf den]].
- Wears a *[[Items/Silverthorn Collar|silverthorn collar]]* that prevents his transformation or regeneration.
- He is the thirteen-year-old son of Dmitri and Anna Krezkov, currently missing.
- He fled into the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Wood]], where he's been sought by [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda d'Avenir]].
- He was bitten by a werewolf attack which killed his younger sister [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala]].
- A thirteen-year-old **werewolf** who has been starved for three weeks.
- His whereabouts are tied into secrets regarding [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]]’s cult.
- He disappeared on the night of Octyavr 22, near a full moon.
- Baron Krezkov suspects his family curse is affecting Ilya.

## DM Notes

- Ilya's fate directly ties to Anna Krezkov's potential salvation, and his whereabouts are tied closely to events in Arc E and L.
- Ilya's abduction is connected to [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]] cult, which will be relevant to the campaign.
