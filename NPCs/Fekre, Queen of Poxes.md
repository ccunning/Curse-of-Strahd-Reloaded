# Fekre, Queen of Poxes

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Known for the spell 'Stinking Cloud' and a boon that forces targets to succeed on Constitution saves or be poisoned after critical hits.
- The condition is that the player's body is covered in boils and pustules, disadvantaging Wisdom (Medicine) checks made to stabilize.
- Belongs to [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] and serves as one of the sources for [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] corruption.
- Participates in the [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] corruption process at Stage Three.
- A vestige associated with [[Items/Amber Shards|amber shards]].
