# Zygfrek

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- In her private moments, she’s described as a quiet, melancholy, self-loathing woman who desires the 'perfection' promised by the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]].
- She appears as a brooding, grumpy cynic who is always finding something to insult or complain about in public.
- Her true emotions include annoyance, suspicion, insult, melancholy, or thoughtfulness.
- [[NPCs/Zygfrek|Zygfrek]] is a servant of the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] and cousin of [[NPCs/Clovin Belview|Clovin Belview]].
- Her primary motivation is bitterness and self-loathing.
