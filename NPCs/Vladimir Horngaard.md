# Vladimir Horngaard

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- Is the source of the other revenants' undeath through the oaths they swore him, and his relationship with [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Godfrey]] is one of mutual love but forgotten history.
- Possesses multiple forms - Vengeful [[NPCs/Revenant|Revenant]] (with regenerative powers) and Knight of Death (which includes Burning Hatred/Rejuvenation features).
- He refuses to let his knights harm or kill [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s forces, because he feels [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] must suffer in the realm of ghosts until death.
- Is a master of the Order's undead nature, has an oathbind ability that causes the victims to be stabilized and subjected to geases.
- Controls the high beacon and stands watch over it; players must either convince or defeat him to access [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]’s spirit.
- Has banished all but [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Godfrey]] from [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] due to his refusal to continue fighting Strahd after the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] descended.
- A lawful evil [[NPCs/Revenant|revenant]] consumed by vengeance and hatred who is the commander of the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]].
- Has abilities including Umbral Snare, Reaving Sweep, Sworn Retribution, Ghostly Command, Martial Reprisal, etc.
- Blindly believes that all evil in this land stems from Strahd, and that he must suffer forever as punishment.
- Led knights into conflict over their loyalty, ultimately banishing them from the mansion due to defiance.
- His presence is tied to a curse that binds revenants like Godfrey through oaths he made during life.
- A commander of the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]], now a revenant bound by hatred toward Strahd.
- A revenant who was originally a valiant knight but has become consumed by hatred for Strahd.
- Known for his extreme vengeance-driven hatred, which is considered corrupted even in death.
- His rebellion and refusal to follow orders showed tension between honor and necessity.
- Seeks the death of the players to prevent further meddling with Strahd von Zarovich.
- The commander of the Knights of the Silver Dragon and husband to Sir Godfrey Gwilym.
- Led resistance against Strahd’s invasion and eventually became known as a revenant.
- Has forgotten much of his past life, particularly his love for Sir Godfrey Gwilym.
- Returned after his death as a revenant to avenge the destruction of the Order.
- Commander for [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]], leading knights during the Battle of the [[Places/Luna River|Luna River]].
- Marked a turning point in history involving faith, obedience, and loyalty.
- A captain who played a key role in the Order of the Silver Dragon.
- Allowed only Sir Godfrey Gwilym to remain during this conflict.
- Revenant knight commander of the Order of the Silver Dragon.
- Enraged by the players' incursion into [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Symbolized the last stand of the good forces in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].

## DM Notes

- The players' primary obstacle in accessing Argynvost's gargoyle is Vladimir himself; may appear during the final phase of the mission.
- Reveals that he is bound by an oath not to allow attacks on Strahd and will attempt to force players to swear one as well.
- The knightly rebellion he sparked sets up future conflicts with other factions involved in faith or justice.
- Commands his knights to seek out the players' destruction at any cost.
- A threat in a future arc/post-defeat of the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]].
