# Walter, the Graveborn

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Possesses actions including tentacle multiattack and bite attack and bonus actions such as Gore Spray, Wail, Withdraw reaction, and Lash Out.
- An undead creature revealed inside the Flesh Mound, depicted as a misshapen rib cage containing Walter Durst's infant corpse.
- Has an association with the singing melodies played in the conservatory as part of the ghostly haunting.
- Mentioned in relation to the nursery’s dark events, implying something about his fate inside the manor.
- Known to be in the nursery where a severed human finger was discovered beside a crib bearing his name.
- May be connected with the dark rituals or experiments taking place under Elisabeth's orders.
- Its heart becomes exposed and vulnerable when using certain attacks like Gore Spray or Wail.
- Has special mechanics like 'Innocent Heart', which takes damage on behalf of flesh mound.
- Symbolized how innocence could be destroyed by the influence of darker powers.
- A bastard child of [[NPCs/Klara|Klara]], born while her husband Gustav was still alive.
- Used by Elisabeth as a vessel for dark power and consumed by the cult.
- Transformed into an abomination, serving as both a weapon and victim.
- Represented the twisted side-effects of exposure to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s forces.
- A half-sibling of Rose, Thorn, and possibly another child.

## DM Notes

- His story might be revealed more fully in interactions or notes within the manor, serving as a key to understanding how families were affected by dark forces.
- Walter's return in the flesh mound; his connection to the [[Places/Ritual Chamber|ritual chamber]]; possible future escape after defeating the flesh mound.
- It is likely that Walter’s story, like others found in [[Factions/Death House|Death House]], will involve a tragic ending tied to the cult’s practices.
