# Dorina

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Her unfinished business involves retrieving her [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] and escaping [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- A Barovian witch who brought an [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] of Khirad to [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- She died frozen in the temple and now haunts the east guard post.
- She seeks possession of a player in order to leave the temple.

## DM Notes

- [[NPCs/Dorina|Dorina]]'s ghost may interfere with party activities or cause complications if not properly handled.
- Her encounter with players might involve moral decisions about her request for help.
