# Erasmus van Richten

> **NPC** · 9 source files

## Key Facts

- Holds a number of magical items: amulet of proof against detection and location, charm bracelet, hat of disguise, lucid lens, and ring of mind shielding.
- A doctor and scholar who is indirectly connected to [[NPCs/Arturi Radanavich|Arturi]]'s curse following the murder of his son through events in the Radanavich caravan.
- Known for being hard to kill and potentially playing a key role in uncovering truth about [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] or his connections.
- [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]] followed his trail through the [[Places/Mists|Mists]], ultimately arriving in search of information on [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s resurgence.
- His skills include Arcana (10), Insight (7), Medicine (10), Perception (7), Religion (7), Sleight of Hand (5).
- Successfully destroyed Metus who had taken over [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus]], but ultimately lost both wife Ingrid and son [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus]].
- A ghost appearing in the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] who helps Stella navigate and avoid being taken by wayward spirits.
- He is mentioned with awe by [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]] but keeps his whereabouts secret due to danger and fear of exposure.
- Kidnapped by Baron Metus and sold to the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]], who then forced her family into servitude and vengeance.
- His kidnapping by [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] was a major catalyst for his father's subsequent actions in the land of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Ultimately disappears from direct involvement with main narrative after being taken by the Radanaviches.
- Had been tracking the movements of Strahd's agents after discovering hints of danger involving [[NPCs/Escher|Escher]].
- Mentioned in reference to manuscripts authored by him that are valuable for understanding [[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]].
- His influence has caused various behaviors in [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]], especially during interactions with spirits.
- Has been actively working on helping keep Stella safe while she exists only in the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]].
- Three months prior, he wrote letters to students planning a trip to [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] but was presumed dead.
- Used a disguise to gain access to the Vistani camp by stealing paints and creating the carnival.
- His fate influences major storylines related to Vistani relationships, vengeance, and identity.
- After his son Erasmus was kidnapped by Vistani, he began hunting monsters and seeking revenge.
- Helped rescue Stella from the Ethereal Plane after her soul was severed by a gallows speaker.
- Influences [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]] and acts as a presence in Victor’s life, particularly regarding Stella.
- Tracked [[Factions/Radanavich Clan|the Radanavich clan]] into Barovia using a *[[Items/Mist Talisman|mist talisman]]* to find and punish them.
- Has a spectral form that appears as a youthful man with a warm light and elven features.
- Reveals he's been helping Stella since shortly after she arrived in the Ethereal Plane.
- Informs players about the unusual activities of lost spirits in the Ethereal Plane.
- Is familiar with gallows speakers and has shared knowledge about them with Victor.
- Born in a land far beyond the valley, becomes one of Barovia's greatest defenders.
- Is the father of the late son Erasmus who died during the Radanaviches rebellion.
- Owns magical items including an amulet of proof against detection and location.
- A famed vampire hunter, though disguised as carnival ringmaster named [[NPCs/Rictavio|Rictavio]].
- He suggests tools like grappling hooks for climbing outer walls of the castle.
- Van Richten was a legendary vampire hunter who mentored Ezmerelda and others.
- Van Richten offers maps to guide the players through certain castle areas.
- His knowledge involves specific rooms and corridors of [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Hid [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]] in the wagon he created and took on the alias of [[NPCs/Rictavio|Rictavio]].
- A ghost who communicates with players by inscribing messages in frost.
- Has appeared as a ghost haunting the wagon where [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]] is hidden.
- He claims he knew Strahd from the past and led a revolt against him.
- His role is significant for both his teachings and mysterious fate.
- Wants Victor and the players to assist in restoring Stella’s soul.
- Afterward, he became a wandering hunter of supernatural terrors.
- Ghost of Dr. Rudolph van Richten mentioned in final summary.
- A symbolic character representing loss within the campaign.
- The true identity of Rictavio, revealed in his [[Places/Guest Suite|guest suite]].
- Gives players insight on how to move in the Ethereal Plane.
- A legendary monster hunter who seeks to protect Arabelle.
- Has Sneak Attack (5d6 damage) and Insightful Fighting.
- A vampire hunter whose actions are targeted by Strahd.
- A vampire hunter, the father of Erasmus (his son).
- Is seeking allies from [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- A ghost residing in the [[Places/Arasek Stockyard|Arasek Stockyard]].
- Haunted and driven from hiding in Act II.
- Part of the forces defending Barovia.
- The deceased son of Van Richten.
- Son of Rudolph van Richten.
