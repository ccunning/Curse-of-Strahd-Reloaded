# Ithuriel

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Can change shape, heal others, raise the dead, and has multiple divine actions including Indomitable, Punish, Ascend, Descend, and Shift.
- Master of [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|the Abbey of Saint Markovia]], creator of the [[Items/Flesh Golem|flesh golem]] [[Items/Vasilka|Vasilka]] and the [[NPCs/Mongrelfolk Otto|mongrelfolk Otto]], [[NPCs/Zygfrek|Zygfrek]], and [[NPCs/Clovin Belview|Clovin Belview]].
- A serene yet inhumanly dispassionate holy man who is actually the angel [[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]] sent to honor [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]].
- Eventually redeems himself and becomes the pegasus form of a deva with unicorn healing features.
- Possesses a runeguard stone which allows him to cast magic circle with only a verbal component.
- Eventually fell into madness due to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s influence, descending into darkness.
- Became part of the narrative as a warning figure regarding the world beyond.
- Represented one of few outsiders who entered [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] during [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s era.
- Belonged to the angel’s feather found by players who seek redemption.
- A deva sent from the Upper Planes to honor [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]]’s memory.
- Lived at [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|the Abbey of Saint Markovia]] and tended to [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]'s sick.
- Used to represent external interest in Strahd and events outside.
- A deva serving the god Lathander, known as the Morninglord.
- Known in Barovia as the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot of Saint Markovia]].
- Player seeks guidance through the feather.

## DM Notes

- May appear directly or influence events around the Abbey.
