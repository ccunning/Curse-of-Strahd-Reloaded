# Neferon

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Uses various spells including *dimension door*, *chain lightning*, and *fireball* in combat.
- He knows details about secret items within the temple and has access to magical resistance.
- His true identity is revealed later in the narrative involving his encounter with players.
- Arrived in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] through the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] and assumed control over [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Has a phylactery to protect himself from death due to his arrival in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- An arcanaloth who masquerades as Heinrich, a caretaker of [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Is allied with [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and acts as an advisor or diplomat for him.
- Controls the flambeaux and has a phylactery hidden in the temple.
- An arcanaloth who disguised himself as [[NPCs/Heinrich Stolt|Heinrich Stolt]].

## DM Notes

- [[NPCs/Neferon|Neferon]] serves as a potential ally or enemy depending on how the characters negotiate with him.
- His interest in the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] indicates that he values the weapon for strategic reasons.
- [[NPCs/Neferon|Neferon]]'s real form and intentions are key to future encounters within the Temple.
- He may act as a source of information regarding [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s plans.
