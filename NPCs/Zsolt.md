# Zsolt

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Warm and friendly with a jovial tone to manipulate players into letting their guard down.
- His demeanor turns serious when players show signs of preparation with silvered arms.
- Claims to offer protection when players aren’t carrying silvered weapons.
- Introduces the story of a supernatural female killer in the woods.
- One of the [[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]] posing as a trapper.

## DM Notes

- May appear again, particularly after revealing werewolf threats; may serve as foreshadowing of future [[NPCs/Wolves|wolves]].
