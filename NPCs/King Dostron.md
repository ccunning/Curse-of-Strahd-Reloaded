# King Dostron

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- His reign caused the decline of [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]]'s power and led to their retreat into the mountains.
- Invasively conquered [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], massacring the inhabitants and desecrating holy places.
- His armies withdrew upon his death but left behind ruins in the eastern mountains.
- He is associated with the stolen spear that [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] took from the [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane]].
- Damned up the [[Places/Luna River|Luna River]] to create farmland, destroying the people's faith.
- His fortress is located on what is now the [[Places/Pillarstone of Ravenloft|Pillarstone of Ravenloft]].
- Built an enormous stone fortress atop a mountain east of the valley.
- His death came long after his invasion, leaving his realm in ruin.
- A tyrant who invaded the valley and scattered [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]].
- A cruel and ambitious monarch from a nearby kingdom.
- Historical figure whose crypt houses a spear.
- Connected to artifacts in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
