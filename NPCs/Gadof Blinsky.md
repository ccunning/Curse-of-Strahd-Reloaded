# Gadof Blinsky

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Owns a pet monkey named Piccolo from [[NPCs/Rictavio|Rictavio]] (who’s not actually his, but was given to him).
- Is afraid of [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]] but works towards success like Fritz von Weerg as a toymaker.
- Creates dolls resembling [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]], a reference linked to Strazni family history.
- His shop plays a role in giving clues related to the quest through his interactions.
- A cheerful toymaker interested in darker arts and making toys for children.
- The owner of the toy shop, known for creating macabre yet joyful toys.
- Sells and creates a prosthetic leg for [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda d'Avenir]].
- Terrorized by [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]] and fearful of consequences.
- Makes dolls resembling [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]] for Izek Strazni.
