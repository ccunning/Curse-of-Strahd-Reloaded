- Thorn asks the players if they can bury the children's remains before proceeding any further into [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Thorn is one of the [[NPCs/Durst children|Durst children]], a spirit who possesses players during the adventure.
- Thorn is Rose's younger brother and the youngest child of Elisabeth and [[NPCs/Gustav Durst|Gustav Durst]].

- His spirit eventually thanks the players for allowing him to rest before vanishing.
