# Urwin Martikov

> **NPC** · 9 source files

## Key Facts

- He delivers letters to players if certain conditions are met related to their actions against [[NPCs/Baron Vargas Vallakovich|Baron Vargas Vallakovich]] or interactions with refugees.
- Is involved in spy activities for keeping information on [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s movements and is deeply affected when his secrets are uncovered by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- A member of the [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov family]] who approaches the players for help investigating a wine shipment shortage at [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]].
- In the dream world, he is aged significantly, emotionally shattered from his capture, and has largely withdrawn from public life.
- Was present when the enchanted gem from [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] was stolen, which led to a conflict between family members.
- Are part of the local community of [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] where they serve roles in public life and may offer hints or support.
- Not directly involved in the main conflict but provide context for important NPCs like [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]] and Lady Wachter.
- He recognizes and describes [[NPCs/Ernst Larnak|Ernst Larnak]] as the courier who delivered the invitation to Lady Wachter's home.
- His theft from the winery ties directly into prophecies guiding him toward helping heroes against Strahd.
- Played pivotal roles in creating the [[Places/Blue Water Inn|Blue Water Inn]] and becoming agents for [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- Is noted to have been captured by Strahd but was not broken and eventually released back into [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
- He can provide information about [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]] and shares legend about her family's tragic past.
- Shows emotional depth through fear for his sons and dedication to freedom from Strahd’s tyranny.
- Accused by his father of stealing the gemstone from the winery, leaving with [[NPCs/Danika Dorakova|Danika Dorakova]].
- Offers to assist the players in tracking [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]] by uncovering details about his movements.
- He has regeneration that activates if not harmed by silvered weapons or necrotic damage.
- Mentions that [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]]'s strix have been patrolling the valley in greater numbers.
- One of the attendees at the War Council and a key figure among Vallakian leadership.
- Located at the eastern undead army while notifying about its existence and location.
- Acts as a guide in Vallaki, sharing information about local culture and politics.
- Is the father of [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian Martikov]], though he avoids discussing their relationship.
- Known for being a kind, helpful host but also a cunning spymaster and tactician.
- Sources of information about [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]], including confirming he is the Baron’s son.
- Provide background on characters and events relevant to current storylines.
- He’s married to [[NPCs/Danika Dorakova|Danika Dorakova]] and the father of two sons, Brom and Bray.
- Provides information about [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]]'s schedule and activities to the players.
- His claim of seeing a raven steal the gem led to a rift with his father.
- He and his wife Danika protect [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] from Izek when he visits the inn.
- Sent a message for help after being driven out of the winery by druids.
- Co-owner of the [[Places/Blue Water Inn|Blue Water Inn]] and father of Brom and Bray [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]].
- Offer insight regarding the inner dynamics among different factions.
- He is a shapechanger with raven-humanoid hybrid form capabilities.
- [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin Martikov]] is the innkeeper of the Blue Water Inn in Vallaki.
- [[NPCs/Muriel Vinshaw|Muriel]] intends to seek him out at the Blue Water Inn in Vallaki.
- Joined Danika in fleeing to Vallaki after confrontation at home.
- Son of [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian Martikov]] and brother to Adrian, Elvir, and [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]].
- A character who informs players about the eastern undead army.
