# Livius Martikov

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Was the founder of a society that would later evolve into an important group of non-human entities involved in Barovian affairs.
- Retired from adventuring after gaining wealth and notoriety for helping the paladin [[NPCs/Lugdana|Lugdana]] and burgomaster [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark]].
- Grandfather of [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian Martikov]], and former wereraven who helped steal the *[[Items/Sigil of the Sun|Sigil of the Sun]]* and *[[Items/The Tome of Strahd|Tome of Strahd]]*.
- His name might be significant in linking various lore and groups within the campaign world of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- The wereraven [[NPCs/Livius Martikov|Livius]] is [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark]]'s companion and the founder of [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- Founded the secret society known as [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]] to help heroes of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- His descendants were noted in various story arcs involving future generations.
- Discovered the *[[Items/Sigil of the Sun|Sigil of the Sun]]* and stole it from [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- He appears as a raven during the second level nightmare sequence.
- Founded the Keepers of the Feather in honor of his service.
- A prominent wereraven who helped [[NPCs/Lugdana|Lugdana]] in her quest.
