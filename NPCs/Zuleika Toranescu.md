# Zuleika Toranescu

> **NPC** · 6 source files

## Key Facts

- Knows about the curse of lycanthropy, the history of the [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]], and [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]]'s transformation via [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]].
- This hints at familial ties between certain members of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]'s nobility and supernatural factions.
- Agrees to allow players to retrieve the *[[Items/Holy Symbol of Ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]]* if they rescue [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil]] and free her.
- Wears a *[[Items/Silverthorn Collar|silverthorn collar]]* that prevents her from regenerating or transforming into a werewolf.
- Players will learn more about her involvement during future arcs related to the werewolf curse.
- She posed as an apparition to [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]], convincing him of the family's lycanthropic heritage.
- A **werewolf** priestess of [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]] and the elder sister of [[NPCs/Baron Dmitri Krezkov|Baron Dmitri Krezkov]].
- Though she appears in [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]]'s room, it's unclear if she is real or another disguise.
- Her role is connected with family curse and broader lycanthropic events in Krezk.
- Mentioned in the context of sending someone to rescue [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil]] from his dungeon cell.
- She abandoned Krezk twenty years ago to join the [[Places/Lake Baratok|Lake Baratok]] [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]].
- Her presence has caused turmoil within the family after her long absence.
- Imprisoned in a cage alongside Ilya Krezkov by [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril Stoyanovich]].
- Emil is surprised and grateful that players were sent by [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]].
- She is the elder sister of [[NPCs/Baron Dmitri Krezkov|Baron Dmitri Krezkov]] and a werewolf.
- She is a werewolf cleric connected to [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]] cult.
- Part of the werewolf pack/lycanthropy-themed conflict.
- Requests freeing her mate to retrieve a holy symbol.
- [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]] is the brother of Baron Dmitri Krezkov.
- Werewolf priestess of Mother Night.
- Sister of Dmitri Krezkov.

## DM Notes

- More details on Zuleika’s activities may emerge through connections with lycanthropy, curses, or [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Her loss is a point of tension between Dmitri and Emil Toranescu.
