# The Vampyr

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Its presence involves temptations to accept its dark power, especially in the context of the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] hilt.
- A powerful vampiric entity whose sarcophagus is located in the [[Places/Amber Vault|Amber Vault]].
- It's part of a broader narrative involving the influence of undead forces.
- Offers the gift of eternal life and youth through perfect undeath.
