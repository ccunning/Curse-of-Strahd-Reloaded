# Arabelle

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Was hidden in the Vistana camp and later brought to [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] by Van Richten disguised as [[NPCs/Rictavio|Rictavio]].
- Disappeared on her way to the [[Places/Luna River|Luna River]] after being drugged by [[NPCs/Yan|Yan]] and a Vistana youth.
- Bears signs that make [[NPCs/Escher|Escher]] recognize her as a descendant of one of the Ladies Three.
- Knows her own story and was expecting the players to find her due to premonitions.
- She is now a Vistana herself and has been hiding at the tower of Van Richten.
- Has been concealed in a wagon disguised as '[[NPCs/Rictavio|Rictavio]]’s [[Places/Carnival of Wonders|Carnival of Wonders]]'.
- Rescued and hidden by Dr. Rudolph van Richten in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]]’s stockyard.
- Child of the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] who goes missing and must be found in Act II.
- A young Vistana girl kidnapped by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s servant, [[NPCs/Escher|Escher]].
- Gives a prophecy that reveals [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s final plan.
- The daughter of [[NPCs/Luvash|Luvash]] and the sister of [[NPCs/Arrigal|Arrigal]].
- Connected to a prophecy after being rescued.

## DM Notes

- Key figure for ending narrative tension in campaign; foreshadows significant future storyline involving Strahd and [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Escher's investigation into her as a target for Strahd.
