# Seeker

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- The ravens near the tree known as ‘the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]]’ were set there as guardians of her memory.
- A mysterious figure who may appear more prominently in the storyline or future events.
- Another name by which the spirit or figure that [[NPCs/Katarina|Katarina]] made a pact with is known.
- Bestowed lycanthropy upon others; her werewolf servants were called wereravens.
- One of the four sisters in the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]], representing autumn and foresight.
- Governs wisdom, harvest, and the passage of age; her symbol is the raven.
- Reveals her connection to larger mysteries within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]'s lore.
- Has influence over [[NPCs/Katarina|Katarina]] and her actions in the past.
- Had a sacred spear stolen by [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] from the [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane]].
- Part of the Ladies Three beside the [[NPCs/Weaver|Weaver]] and [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]].
