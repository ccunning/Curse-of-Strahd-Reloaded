# Sasha Ivliskova

> **NPC** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- The presence of [[NPCs/Sasha Ivliskova|Sasha]] hints at potential internal conflict within [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s inner circle and suggests possible future plotlines where the party may encounter her directly or indirectly.
- [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] reveals her existence as a hidden conspirator within [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s household, adding to the layered deception in the overarching narrative.
- [[NPCs/Sasha Ivliskova|Sasha]] is mentioned with delight by [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]], indicating that she finds this betrayal fascinating or even amusing.
- Her nature as a [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]] also ties into other vampiric-related lore throughout the setting.
- Displays discomfort at being one of Strahd's brides but harbors deep resentment towards him.
- Was locked away in a crypt for 400 years before being freed shortly after Strahd awakened.
- Is not allowed to share information about Strahd's plans or abilities, even when asked.
- She is described as quiet, brooding, and deeply troubled by her vampiric existence.
- Reveals that [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]] is being held prisoner on the third floor of the castle.
- One of the first victims who suffered under Strahd’s rule post-transformation.
- Known traitor referred to by [[NPCs/Arrigal|Arrigal]]; serves as an undercover operative.
- Motivated to avoid Strahd’s wrath and protect others from his influence.
- Formerly imprisoned in a crypt beneath [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] for centuries.
- An early bride of Strahd who was sealed in a crypt beneath Ravenloft.
- Guides players through [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] on their way to meet Strahd.
- Shares knowledge about the castle’s layout, inhabitants, and traps.
- His treatment of her highlighted his transformation into a monster.
- Secret friend to [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]], [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]]'s betrothed and Strahd's prisoner.
- Symbolized early patterns of brutality and exploitation by Strahd.
- Strahd's first and eldest bride, wed nearly four centuries ago.
- Despises Strahd with all her being and desires to see him dead.
- Sasha is Strahd's first bride and a secret friend to Gertruda.
- Represented the beginning of Strahd's rise to dominance.
- Strahd's eldest vampiric bride and one of his consorts.
- Strahd von Zarovich’s eldest wife and [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]].
- A vampire spawn who is forbidden to disobey Strahd.

## DM Notes

- Sasha likely has a major part in later story arcs involving Strahd's family or his secret schemes, adding intrigue to future developments inside the castle.
- Plots with players after dinner, instructing them to press a pedal on the organ in the dining hall to meet her.
- She will be present at the fourth-floor landing of [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|the Heart of Sorrow]] once players arrive there.
- She will be instrumental in uncovering secrets and possible betrayal later in the storyline.
