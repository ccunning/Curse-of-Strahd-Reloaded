# Wensencia

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- She fights the players if they intervene in her intentions but flees or is driven off after sufficient intimidation or persuasion.
- A student of [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] and a Barovian witch who seeks to harvest parts of the dire elk for alchemical potions.
- She has a dire wolf compatriot who can attack those who oppose her unless defeated or otherwise removed.
- [[NPCs/Wensencia|Wensencia]] serves [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] and is hostile toward anyone who threatens or disrespects her master.
- She believes in [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s return as a dark messiah and follows his bride, [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla Vilisevic]].
- Serves as a potential distraction or obstacle to retrieving necessary clues from the area.
- She can provide insight into how one approaches Baba Lysaga and what she might expect.
- Attempts to harvest the elk’s body regardless of attempts by players to dissuade her.
- She greets players upon their arrival at [[Places/Berez|Berez]] and guides them to Baba Lysaga.
- [[NPCs/Wensencia|Wensencia]] is a Barovian witch studying under Baba Lysaga for three months.

## DM Notes

- Will encounter players again if they interact with her; could pose challenges in future arcs involving alchemy, lore, and spiritual influences.
- She may appear again during other events involving [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]] or [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s influence.
- Wensencia plays a key role in the cultic practices connected to Strahd’s revival.
