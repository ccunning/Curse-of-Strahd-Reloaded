# Varushka

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- The spirit of [[NPCs/Varushka|Varushka]] becomes a powerful undead entity named [[NPCs/Varushka|Varushka]], Nightmare Maid, in her final form which she uses to battle those who would leave her nightmare.
- Varushka is a servant of [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] who was trapped in the castle after the arrival of the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] and later became the spirit haunting the nightmare realm.
- In the fifth level of her nightmare, the memory of Varushka explains how [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] has fed upon her multiple times, draining her blood.
- The recurring nightmare involves her experiences at the castle and may affect player outcomes or reveal truth.
- Known to have been present during [[NPCs/Katarina|Katarina]]’s time at [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] and later became connected to her.
- She appears as a hanged woman in the first level of her nightmare, with puncture marks on her neck.
- In her second form, her abilities shift to include Life Drain, Mist Garrotte, and Vampiric Mist.
- Varushka is not able to leave the [[Places/Guest Suite|guest suite]] and is frightened by anyone visiting the closet.
- She is a ghost or spirit tied to Castle Ravenloft and serves as a conduit to its past crimes.
- Has a personal history with the castle, linked to the tree where [[NPCs/Katarina|Katarina]] left gifts for her.
- In her first form, she can use actions like Warp Flesh, Animate Minor Object, and Papercut.
- She appears in the castle to assist players during combat encounters if they are bloodied.
- A former servant of Castle Ravenloft who hauntingly appears to the players in nightmares.
- She has two forms: Nightmare Maid (challenge 8) and Soul of Despair (challenge 10).
- Varushka has reactions such as Indomitable, Swap, Adhere, Erupt, and Misty Step.
- Varushka is a chaotic evil Medium undead creature, known as the Nightmare Maid.
- Spirit in Castle Ravenloft, haunting players during dinner arc.
- Varushka was the sister of Katarina with whom she is reunited.
- Has a hidden cache associated with [[Items/Katarina's Necklace|Katarina's necklace]].
- Her appearance uses the statistics of a poltergeist.

## DM Notes

- She serves as a metaphor or reminder of past actions that haunt not just her, but all involved in the castle’s dark history.
- Her appearance and role in nightmares suggest possible lore revelations or consequences from Castle Ravenloft's atrocities.
- Varushka appears as both a ghost and a final boss during the players' experience within the dream realm.
- The presence of Varushka's necklaces may have some connection to events later in the campaign.
