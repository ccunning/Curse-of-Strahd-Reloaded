# Rosavalda "Rose" Durst

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- She serves as Thorn's fiercest protector to the outside world but is portrayed as a lost, fearful, and traumatized young girl to those she trusts.
- Appears in ghost form through various visual cues and interactions within the house, especially in relation to her children Rose and Thorn.
- A character mentioned in passing, likely connected to the players' ability to use 'mending' cantrip when lifting the [[Places/Portcullis|portcullis]].
- A ghostly apparition who manifests after the flesh mound dies and is described with grotesque features and an [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]].
- She embodies traits of sympathy, endearment, and gratitude in how she behaves toward players and fellow companions.
- Appears to hinder the players' escape from [[Factions/Death House|Death House]], making threats against them and causing structural chaos.
- Possesses magical abilities including spells like _mending_, _light_, and _shocking grasp_ learned from a diary.
- She and her brother Thorn serve as sentries while the party rests in the clearing after escaping [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- Rose asks the players if they can bury the children's remains before proceeding any further into [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- The [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] from her jewelry box serves as a key to unlock the secret door in the [[Places/Library|Library]].
- Recognizes an amber shard similar to a player's own in [[Arc A - Escape From Death House]].
- Rose is one of the [[NPCs/Durst children|Durst children]], a spirit who possesses players during the adventure.
- She is likely a key figure connected to the lore of the house and the family history.
- Used her newfound magic to murder husband and nursemaid in the manner of necromancy.
- Her spirit eventually thanks the players for allowing her to rest before vanishing.
- A character associated with the Durst family, who may appear within Death House.
- Rose is Thorn's older sister and the eldest child of Elisabeth and [[NPCs/Gustav Durst|Gustav Durst]].
- Her motivations center on protecting Thorn and helping their spirits find peace.
- A woman from the Durst family who fell under the influence of amber and power.
- A ghost who has been seen in the house, guiding players and offering help.
- Notably described in a key interaction with the Amber Shard mechanism.
- His portrait hangs in the main hall showing her with their children.
- The older sister of Thorn; daughter of Gustav and Elisabeth Durst.
- A character likely involved with [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] or related lore.
- Eventually slaughtered by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] herself after her failed pursuit.
- Her name is referenced in letters found throughout Death House.
- Created a cult devoted to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and offered sacrifices to him.
- Led an attack against adventurers for Strahd’s entertainment.
- Desires to find peace for both herself and her brother.
- A member of the Durst family, married to Gustav.
- Appears in the children's room with Thorn.

## DM Notes

- Elisabeth plays a central role in guiding the players' actions and decisions, particularly around finding the secret rooms related to the family.
- Her relationship with her husband and children may be explored further through interactions with ghostly forms or documents found inside.
- Her desire to find peace implies that some form of spiritual or physical resolution may be required for her character arc.
- Rose may play an important role in assisting players through puzzles and gaining access to specific areas of the manor.
- Her appearance signals that her power will soon return; sets stage for further encounter in Act II.
- May have a role involving further amber shard encounters or [[Places/Amber Temple|Amber Temple]] lore.
