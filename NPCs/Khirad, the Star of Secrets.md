# Khirad, the Star of Secrets

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- The condition is permanent whispering voice and frozen smile, with inability to shout or speak loudly.
- Known for 'Phantasmal Killer' spell and a boon giving advantage on Intelligence checks.
- Khirad is the Star of Secrets, whose gift transforms individuals into a [[NPCs/Nothic|nothic]] form.
- His riddle relates to the relationship between [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]].
- He is connected to the concept of hidden truths and knowledge.
- A vestige found in [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] with an [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]].
- Participates in Stage Three corruption process.
