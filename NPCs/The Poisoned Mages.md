# The Poisoned Mages

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- These spirits can influence situations if asked or if players show [[Items/Amber Shards|amber shards]] or carry corruption from Stage Three.
- The spirits of seven mages and apprentices (five apprentices, two senior mages) who died in [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- They know that Vilnius is the one that killed them, and they're trying to destroy him to free their souls.
- Bound to the temple by [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]] as punishment for their betrayal and current hate towards Vilnius.
- They were murdered by Vilnius when he poisoned [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]]'s wine.

## DM Notes

- The outcome of this encounter with Vilnius may determine if the spirits are freed—linked directly to the future of the [[Places/Mists|Mists]].
