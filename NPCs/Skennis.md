# Skennis

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Can be convinced of kindness or pacified with food or healing, and offers passage to others.
- Known in the past for his wisdom and leadership, now an old and bitter figure.
- Former **werewolf** pack leader, now an old blind wolf confined to a den.
- Now treated poorly by [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]], who keeps him alive only through scraps.
- Was deposed by [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil]] but resented the replacement.
