# Brother Marek

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- He reluctantly allows players to speak with [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]] if convinced by persuasive words or knowledge of prophecies like the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] or [[Places/Amber Temple|Amber Temple]].
- In death, a cold and apathetic shell of a man with a sarcastic cynicism who sees the Order’s fate as damnation for their failures.
- Has feelings of melancholy, annoyance, skepticism, bitterness, resignation, bemusement, and disapproval towards his circumstances.
- Keeps watch over spiritual activities in [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] and is said to hold interest in all happenings in the spiritual realm.
- He is deeply cynical about the order's fate and believes no good can come from trying to stir up [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]'s spirit.
- Wishes only good for the players but wants them to abandon efforts to stir up the spirits of [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]].
- His interaction provides guidance regarding the Younger Dragon and how to find Argynvost’s prophecy.
- Prefers to remain within the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] but may respond when spoken to or prayed before.
- The former chaplain of the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]], haunted in the [[Places/Upstairs Gallery|upstairs gallery]].
- The spirit of a former chaplain of the Silver Dragon who died defending the mansion.
- His spirit inhabits a stained-glass window, appearing as [[NPCs/Saint Andral|Saint Andral]] in this form.
- Players can reach him by retrieving and reading aloud from his holy book.
- Can offer additional guidance on Argynvost’s prophecy once approached.

## DM Notes

- He provides the key to accessing Argynvost's prophecy in a cryptic way—referencing the twin dragons or the younger dragon above.
- His book is necessary for accessing his spirit, possibly key in understanding Argynvost's wishes and completing the quest.
- He holds critical information about King Barov I, the ancient founder, and [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s lineage that affects gameplay later.
