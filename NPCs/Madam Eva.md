# Madam Eva

> **NPC** · 7 source files

## Key Facts

- Her age and nature raise questions about her true identity and potential connection to other supernatural or historical elements of the setting.
- In [[Act I - Into the Mists/Arc C - Into the Valley|Arc C - Into the Valley]], interacts with the raven’s feather and recognizes the [[NPCs/Roc|roc]].
- Refuses to reveal specific details about [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s plans or the nature of a person's fate unless directly asked by the individual.
- Instructs the players to deliver a toy to [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]], her great-grand-niece, as part of an exchange for information.
- Gave Rudolph van Richten a *[[Items/Mist Talisman|mist talisman]]* allowing him to escape [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] after completing his revenge.
- In combat, she prefers non-violent negotiation but can cast spells like harm spell to stop enemies.
- Foretold a future where [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin]] would contribute to saving the land by guiding heroes against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- She has been referenced in previous texts, notably in connection with prophecy and guiding others.
- An avatar of the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]], believed to be a mystical figure connected to the Fates and Ancient One.
- [[NPCs/Stanimir|Stanimir]] holds knowledge about the [[Items/Stone Crest|stone crest]]'s secret connection to the [[Factions/Ladies of the Fanes|Ladies of the Fanes]].
- Gives the players a mission to retrieve a toy in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] for her great-grand-niece, [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]].
- She sends [[NPCs/Stanimir|Stanimir]] with tokens for [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]] after her appointment as burgomaster.
- Was instrumental in preparing some individuals for roles involving supernatural resistance.
- She is revealed to be an avatar of the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]] and has intimate knowledge of player pasts.

- Appeared before characters such as adventurers and others in future narratives.
