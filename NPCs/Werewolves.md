# Werewolves

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Players' decision about rescuing him could reflect deeper themes about freedom and humanity versus beast.
- Described in Arc L - The Den of [[NPCs/Wolves|Wolves]] as a creature needing to be saved from [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s control.
- A humanoid creature with wolf-like traits, known for lycanthropy and attacks on humans.
- Two [[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]]—[[NPCs/Zsolt|Zsolt]] and [[NPCs/Mathilda|Mathilda]]—who have been hunting for players near [[Places/Lake Baratok|Lake Baratok]].
- Have lycanthropy, regeneration, and pack tactics that make them deadly encounters.
- Connected with the broader plan involving escape from the castle or resistance.
- Warn players about a phantom Vistana woman named ‘the murderess’ with stories.
- The werewolf whose rescue is linked with players' journey into the mountains.
- They pretend to be trappers from Krezk who warn of danger on the road ahead.
- Can shift between forms (humanoid, wolf, hybrid) using shapechange action.
- May be crucial to the future success or failure of players against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Has abilities like keen hearing/scent, pack tactics, and regeneration.
- Attack if players aren’t carrying silvered weapons or ammunition.
- The curse can be activated by killing an innocent humanoid.
- Affected by silvered weapons and necrotic damage.

## DM Notes

- Setups for more extensive werewolf storylines; connects to potential future coven of [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]] in [[Arc H - The Lost Soul]].
- Sets up potential future changes for player characters who become infected.
- His rescue may unlock additional pathways to power or understanding.
- He might play a role in confronting Strahd directly in another arc.
- Introduces lycanthropy as a major theme.
