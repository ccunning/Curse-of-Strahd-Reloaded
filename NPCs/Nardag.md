# Nardag

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Can escape to the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] via *plane shift* but chose to remain in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] for two reasons: to bask in miasma from [[Factions/Dark Powers|Dark Powers]] and to keep [[NPCs/Bluetspur|Bluetspur]] as a companion.
- Knows how to use *cloudkill*, *greatsword*, bite, and *major image*, and can sense the presence of [[Factions/Dark Powers|Dark Powers]].
- Hid within an amber husk in the West [[Places/Catacombs|Catacombs]] when not disguised as a human woman named [[NPCs/Lorelei|Lorelei]].
- Named its blue slaad offspring [[NPCs/Bluetspur|Bluetspur]], which it fed cadavers from the East [[Places/Catacombs|Catacombs]].
- A death slaad that arrived in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] through a cloud of mist from Limbo.

## DM Notes

- Its presence is tied to both its greatsword and a blue slaad, suggesting ongoing combat and a future fight with [[NPCs/Nardag|Nardag]].
