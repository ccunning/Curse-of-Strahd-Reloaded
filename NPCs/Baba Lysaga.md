# Baba Lysaga

> **NPC** · 7 source files

## Key Facts

- [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] is a chaotic evil Medium humanoid (human, shapechanger) with powerful magic abilities including spellcasting, poison attacks, and polymorphing into a [[NPCs/Swarm|swarm]] of insects.
- In her second form, she gains new abilities such as control water, grasping vine spellcasting, and the ability to ambush attackers with swampwalk and grasping vine reactions.
- She has two forms: her normal form as a witch mother and a second, more monstrous form when she sinks into the swamp of [[Places/Berez|Berez]] after being reduced to 0 hit points.
- She is protected by a 'Blessing of [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]]' that provides immunity to divination magic and can concentrate on up to three spells at once.
- Eventually became the leader of a group that later splintered into factions, including those who resented her dedication to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Later involved in the destruction of the [[Items/Brightblade|Brightblade]] through her connection with the spirit of [[NPCs/Prince Erevan Löwenhart|Prince Erevan Löwenhart]].
- Her role expanded after the fall of the original regime and served as a figure of power in the valley.
- Taught [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Fiona]] about magic and [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]], though [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Fiona]] ultimately rejected her influence over time.
- Knew about the curse that afflicted [[NPCs/Queen Ravenovia|Queen Ravenovia]] and possibly influenced [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s early life.
- [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] can see the location and circumstances of [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]]’s captivity through *scrying* spell.
- Her flying skull provides her with a fly speed of 30 ft. and grants half cover while riding it.
- Players must convince her to perform a scrying spell using a hair of [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] and a gold bracelet.
- Gave [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]] a transformation brew that enhanced his curse, giving him strength and madness.
- A witch from [[Places/Berez|Berez]] and servant of the druids involved in the conflict at [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
- Baba Lysaga is a powerful, mad and fickle witch residing in the ruins of Berez.
- If angered, she may polymorph players into goats and lock them in her [[Items/Goat Pen|goat pen]].
- Created greater and [[NPCs/Lesser Strix|lesser strix]] to patrol against [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- Used the druids' help to steal one of the enchanted gemstones from the winery.
- Known among characters for her witchcraft and manipulation of werewolf powers.
- A powerful hag mentioned as having recently increased patrols of the valley.
- Banished Fiona from the coven of Berez upon Fiona's return against her will.
- Strahd's former nursemaid who was banished due to her obsession with him.
- Became interested in Fiona Wachter after finding her lost during a storm.
- Has connections with various magical forces and allies like the druids.
- A powerful witch who began recruiting a coven of witches in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- A swamp witch from Berez who has influence over the werewolf curse.
- Granted [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]] a power that caused him to become extremely brutal.
- Resided in the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Woods]] after Strahd's arrival in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Is believed to be involved in Kiril's rise as pack leader.
- A swamp witch who created the strix to hunt wereravens.
- Her activity is noted by [[NPCs/Muriel Vinshaw|Muriel]] in addition to [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin]].
- An influential witch residing in the land of Berez.
- She gifted the strix to Strahd upon his awakening.
- Is involved in larger plans within Barovia's lore.
- She resides in a hut perched atop a massive stump.
- Swamp witch who kidnaps children from the winery.
- Final enemy to be defeated in Act IV.

## DM Notes

- Potential future player actions involving removing the gem from the [[Places/Creeping Hut|Creeping Hut]] (which animates the hut) will be significant.
- Her later involvement with the loss of Fiona's magical abilities and her role in guiding [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]]'s journey may be relevant.
- The mention of the Swallowed by the Swamp feature may become relevant for lore or future mechanics in Berez.
- Baba Lysaga is connected to Strahd through [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]], and her cult practices involve lycanthropy.
- She appears to be connected with the mystical nature of Barovia and its transformational power.
- She has ties to Mother Night and holds dark knowledge relevant to players' curse.
