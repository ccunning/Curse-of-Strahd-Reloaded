# Argynvost

> **NPC** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- The dragon's spirit is believed to be lingering at [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] and can guide the players towards a new front in the conflict with darkness.
- Upon death, his spirit haunts [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] but refuses to speak to Vladimir or the players unless sufficiently convinced otherwise.
- The leader and founder of the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]], who later revealed himself to be a silver dragon in disguise.
- His spirit has stirred recently after [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s reawakening and is seeking heroes capable of standing against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- He is believed to have transformed into a dragon to fight evil, and is remembered as a dragon of honor and valor.
- Slain during the war with Strahd, his bones were burned by Strahd and his skull taken to [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- His journal reveals a desperate final stand before his death; he vows to fight for his land and ideals.
- Players must ultimately retrieve his skull from [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]]'s depths to fulfill the prophecy.
- Appears in [[Arc M - The Dragon's Manor]] as a spirit whose appearance prompts the scale to glow.
- His spirit speaks only with a strong desire for truth, hope, and justice against Strahd.
- Built Argynvostholt as headquarters for the order and later became its patron.
- When restored, his spirit greets the player as heir for the Order’s legacy.
- His skull is one of the precious objects to be recovered during the heist.
- Was killed during the Battle of the [[Places/Luna River|Luna River]] against Strahd’s forces.
- The founder of the Order and the namesake of the castle Argynvostholt.
- An adult silver dragon and founder of the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]].
- [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]] is a historical figure whose skull is kept in the castle.
- It is said to be at his tomb which is located inside the castle.
- A silver dragon who helped found the Order of the Silver Dragon.
- Became engaged in conflict with Strahd over control of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- His death marked the fall of the Order of the Silver Dragon.
- His mansion in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] is known as Argynvostholt.
- Silver dragon spirit residing in Argynvostholt.
- Gives a prophecy to players during Act III.

## DM Notes

- His prophecy can be gained through the [[Places/Dragon Gargoyle|dragon gargoyle]]'s brazier if they recite the oath of the Order; the prophecy gives them a powerful clue about how to fight Strahd.
- His remains must be retrieved for players to restore him or invoke his prophecy; his spirit is tied to a gargoyle at the beacon.
- The players can communicate with [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]'s ghost using his portrait or the shrine in his study.
- Reveals more about his skull and spirit manifestation during [[Arc Q - A Shining Beacon]].
