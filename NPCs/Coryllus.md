# Coryllus

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Together with [[NPCs/Diona|Diona]], he seeks to find his missing companions and protect them from harm.
- His loyalty to [[NPCs/Diona|Diona]] makes him crucial in guiding players through their own journeys.
- He has a pragmatic outlook and believes [[NPCs/Meda|Meda]] and [[NPCs/Duras|Duras]] are dead.
- A berserker who accompanied Diona to [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].

## DM Notes

- His experience and insight into the [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] traditions could prove useful for navigating the temple.
- [[NPCs/Coryllus|Coryllus]] may challenge or support the players on multiple fronts based on character decisions.
