# Ismark Antonovich

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Named as a significant historical figure, mocked after [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s return by villagers calling him '[[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark]] the Lesser'.
- If the players ask him about [[NPCs/Lugdana|Lugdana]], he shares that she has been separated from him during a duel with [[Factions/Wights|wights]].
- Used deception in recounting how good came from [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] to justify the use of its artifacts.
- Told the people that an angel in raven form had delivered the *[[Items/Sigil of the Sun|Sigil of the Sun]]* to inspire faith.
- [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark]] is a memory of a man who lived in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] and was a former keeper of the Feather.
- He introduces himself to players, claiming to be looking for [[Items/Sigil of the Sun|the Sigil of the Sun]].
- Named '[[NPCs/Ismark Antonovich|Ismark the Great]]' for bravery, though he was later killed by enemies.
- He has information about the presence of [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]] in the castle.
- A burgomaster who helped [[NPCs/Lugdana|Lugdana]] in her campaign against vampires.
- He carries a raven companion named [[NPCs/Livius Martikov|Livius]].

## DM Notes

- His mention of the Sigil of the Sun could link to further story elements related to magical items and their powers in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- His death at an early stage of history prepares for his legacy being overshadowed later by events and characters like [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]].
- Ismark's knowledge of magic items might assist the party's journey towards finding certain valuable artifacts.
