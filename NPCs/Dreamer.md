# Dreamer

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Became known as [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]] and caused great suffering before being slain by her sisters.
- Was not invited to build a shrine by [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]] due to its dark nature.
- Fell into jealousy upon being shunned and was corrupted by [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]].
- One of the four sisters in the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]], representing winter and dreams.
- Governs rest, hope, and the grave; her symbol is the butterfly.
