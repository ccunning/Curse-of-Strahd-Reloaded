# Baron Vargas Vallakovich

> **NPC** · 7 source files

## Key Facts

- Eventually becomes a pivotal figure for [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]]’s narrative arc and future conflicts with the supernatural.
- Suffers an ominous death at [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]]'s hands by suffocation via pillow, though Vargas watches passively.
- Currently missing or absent from the narrative as it is not directly discussed in this section.
- Owns a mansion that is haunted by a spirit believed linked to the death of the Baroness.
- Condemned [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]] for his blasphemy following the latter's transformation by *[[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]]*.
- The event of his assassination sets up the power shift within the Vallakovich family.
- Narcissistic noble who aims to make the town of [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] happy and escape the [[Places/Mists|Mists]].
- He controls his guard captain, Izek Strazni, and employs dogs named Claw and Fang.
- Known for cruelty and fear tactics including public humiliation and imprisonment.
- Is uninterested in the plight of [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]]; dismisses any interest in her safety.
- Commands the guards and enforces strict laws and festivals to maintain control.
- His rule causes despair among refugees and sparks tension throughout the city.
- He has a [[Places/Library|library]] and is known for his control over local resources and people.
- His presence may be felt indirectly through references to his past actions.
- Important in understanding family ties and potential threats to his house.
- Possesses a personal servant named Izek Strazni who acts as his enforcer.
- Is a potential target if [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s scheme involves obtaining a descendant.
- Father of Vargas, a baron of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] who eventually inherited the title.
- Is father to [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor Vallakovich]] and husband to [[NPCs/Baroness Lydia Petrovna|Baroness Lydia Petrovna]].
- Has a connection with various magical history including [[NPCs/Khazan|Khazan]]'s lore.
- Controls a large portion of [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]]’s politics and social norms.
- The ruler of Vallaki with a tyrannical, ego-driven approach.
- Father of [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor Vallakovich]], the player character.
- Ruler of Vallaki and antagonist to [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona]].
- Leader of the town of Vallaki.
- Uses Izek Strazni as enforcer.

## DM Notes

- May return with greater relevance later during the campaign or storyline involving family intrigue.
- His role in manipulating events involving [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]] and potential threat from his own son Victor.
- The silent complicity of Vargas during this moment may lead to greater consequences.
- Introduces themes of rebellion and resistance that lead into other story arcs.
- Establishes the core conflict of the town under his control.
