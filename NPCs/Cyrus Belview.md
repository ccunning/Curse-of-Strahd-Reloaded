# Cyrus Belview

> **NPC** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- The [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] tells them the truth that [[NPCs/Cyrus Belview|Cyrus]] is a servant of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] under the guise of being perfected, but they don't know this yet.
- He was supposedly taken to [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] after being 'perfected' under the pretense he had reached a new stage in his journey.
- [[NPCs/Cyrus Belview|Cyrus]] is the patriarch of the Belview family, an ancient ancestor whose descendants have continued the quest for perfection.
- He is mentioned multiple times as someone seeking to be 'perfected,' highlighting ongoing tension among [[Factions/The Belviews|the Belviews]].
- Offers to draw players a rough map of the castle if they retrieve his favorite cooking spoon from the garrison.
- Motivated to help restore [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]], believing [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] holds the knowledge to restore his family.
- All [[Factions/The Belviews|the Belviews]], including Marzena, held Cyrus dear, and his disappearance distressed them deeply.
- Believes he and his family can be restored to human form once Strahd completes castle restoration.
- Provides food in the [[Places/Kitchen|kitchen]] during dinner and plays a role in revealing the [[Places/Wine Cellar|wine cellar]]'s truth.
- Has a gruff personality with conspiratorial rants, but is afraid of zombies in the garrison.
- Former patriarch of the Belview family, transformed by a blessing curse from the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]].
- Has only served Strahd for a few months and is irritated by interruptions to his work.
- He has physical handicaps and a poor memory, often rambling in conspiratorial rants.
- Shares lore about his family's transformation by the Abbot and Strahd's role in it.
- Cyrus is the former patriarch of the Belview family and butler of Castle Ravenloft.
- A figure whose wooden spoon is found in one of the alcoves near Guards’ Quarters.
- His spoon is a clue related to his presence near undead zombies in the corridor.
- Is present in the [[Places/Kitchen|kitchen]] speaking to three zombies.
- A family or group related to implied incest themes.
- A mongrelfolk butler working in Castle Ravenloft.
- They may trigger sensitive topics for players.
