# Majesto

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Lady Wachter’s [[NPCs/Imp|imp]] familiar who acts as a scout, able to utilize *crow*, *spider*, and *rat* forms while remaining invisible with the *invisibility* ability.
- Can provide information to players about the guard duties of dretches outside the attic and the location of Lady Wachter's hidden group.
- Has a somewhat selfish nature but can be reminded of the mission using persuasion checks if necessary.
- Used in combat to attack hags concentrating on spells with the *sting* action.
- Serves both as support during escape and combat scenarios.

## DM Notes

- [[NPCs/Majesto|Majesto]] may be called upon again in other arcs of the campaign due to his connections with Lady Wachter and his familiarity with combat situations.
