# Rictavio

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- Hides in plain sight, using a wagon with [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]] inside, which he uses to disguise himself as a Vistana.
- Recognized by players through his wagons' yellow paint colorings that match those elsewhere.
- Disguised identity of Dr. Rudolph van Richten, who is leading efforts to protect [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]].
- Known to have previously interacted with the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] Seer [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]] prior to his mission.
- Uses disguises including a hat of disguise and other magic items to hide his identity.
- Tells stories that often involve fictional monsters and themes from the world of D&D.
- Used his disguise and the paint from [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] camp to hide his wagon as a carnival.
- Appears as flamboyant and irreverent carnival ringmaster with third-person speech.
- The ringmaster of the [[Places/Carnival of Wonders|Carnival of Wonders]], an alias used by Rudolph van Richten.
- Linked to the [[Places/Carnival of Wonders|Carnival of Wonders]] via foreshadowing (fresh paint on its sign).
- His true identity is actually Dr. Rudolph van Richten, a famed vampire hunter.
- His carnival is built using the stolen painting and colors of the Vistani.
- Serves as a disguise to protect Arabelle while keeping her in plain sight.
- Secretly works against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and serves as mentor to [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda d'Avenir]].
- Secretly gathers information about the party during social interactions.
- Has a wagon in [[Places/Arasek Stockyard|Arasek Stockyard]] under his name for covering his tracks.
- He hides Arabelle’s secret identity from others while protecting her.
- Innocently brought Arabelle to [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]], believing she needed safety.
- Van Richten disguising himself as the ringmaster of the carnival.
- Maintains secret identity to avoid detection by agents of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- A flamboyant carnival ringmaster living in the [[Places/Blue Water Inn|Blue Water Inn]].
- Used to infiltrate the Vistani camp and keep Arabelle safe.
- Tries to avoid conflict unless defending innocent victims.

## DM Notes

- Will continue to play key role in protecting Arabelle; central figure in the narrative arc.
- Continued use of the cover as he continues his protection work; impact on player trust.
- His role in hiding Arabelle, possible future betrayal by his own allies.
- His tale is part of the overall arc involving Van Richten's legacy.
- His true identity is revealed in Arc E - The Missing Vistana.
