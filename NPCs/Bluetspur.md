# Bluetspur

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Attacks creatures that pass through or perceive the illusion; triggers [[NPCs/Nardag|Nardag]]'s attack if detected.
- Disguised by [[NPCs/Nardag|Nardag]]’s *major image* spell to appear as an empty corridor in the East [[Places/Catacombs|Catacombs]].
- A blue slaad who grew rapidly from a tadpole fed on cadavers by Nardag.
- Fights alongside Nardag when its disguise is revealed.

## DM Notes

- Its role as Nardag's ally ties into the dual slaad fight in the East [[Places/Catacombs|Catacombs]] and future combat scenarios.
