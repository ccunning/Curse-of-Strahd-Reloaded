# Weaver

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Gave her gems to wereravens for safekeeping when [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]] feared [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]]’s corruption.
- One of the four sisters in the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]], representing spring and growth.
- Made sacred gemstones that provided nourishment for those she blessed.
- The last of the Ladies Three, along with the [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]] and [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]].
- Governs birth, family, and new life; her symbol is the spider.
