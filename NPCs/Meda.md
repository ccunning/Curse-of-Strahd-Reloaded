# Meda

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Her transformation shows the dangers of being exposed to powerful forces within [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- She fell into the vault below after accidentally pushing [[NPCs/Duras|Duras]] down while fleeing from him.
- After discovering the truth, she accepted Khirad's dark gift and transformed into a [[NPCs/Nothic|nothic]].
- A berserker who went missing after a confrontation with a flameskull.
