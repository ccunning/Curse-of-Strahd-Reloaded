# Emil Toranescu

> **NPC** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- His fall represents one example of internal conflict among supernatural beings bound to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s control.
- Can provide information on escape routes, including that of the key for his collar held by Rahadin.
- Infiltrated the castle dungeon, indicating betrayal or misgivings toward the vampire master.
- His presence was key in establishing the nature of loyalty and trust within ancient orders.
- Aided in teaching [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]] how to master her curse, and knew he would be betrayed by [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]].
- Was imprisoned by [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]] after winning the power struggle, though not permanently injured.
- Has three levels of exhaustion and wears a [[Items/Silverthorn Collar|silverthorn collar]] to prevent transformation.
- Accused by [[NPCs/Baron Dmitri Krezkov|Dmitri Krezkov]] of 'luring' [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]] away with tales of lycanthropy as a gift.
- A human shapechanger and werewolf lycanthrope locked in a cell in the South Dungeon.
- Replaced by Kiril Stoyanovich as leader of the [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]] after a power struggle.
- Alpha werewolf leader who failed to keep loyalty to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and was imprisoned.
- Fled through the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] with [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]]'s family before being confronted by Kiril.
- Character imprisoned in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] during Act P – Ravenloft Heist.
- Can be recruited to assist the players in confronting the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]].
- Defeated Kiril in a challenge for leadership five years earlier.
- [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil]] is a werewolf held prisoner in the castle [[Places/Dungeons|dungeons]].
- He is one of the objects to be rescued during the heist.
- He is described as a prisoner with a [[Items/Silverthorn Collar|silverthorn collar]].
- Former **werewolf** pack leader and mate of Zuleika.
- Brother-in-law to the Krezkovs.
- Rescued by the players.

## DM Notes

- His rescue is linked with his silverthorn collar that must be unlocked by a master key held by Rahadin or [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]].
- He holds information about the true nature of the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]'s manipulation of [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] and [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala]] ([[Factions/The Belviews|the Belviews]]).
- His imprisonment hints at deeper connections or future rebellions involving supernatural factions.
- [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil]] is imprisoned due to his werewolf form and is sought by the PCs as part of a rescue mission.
- His presence is linked to the tragedy/curse of the Krezkov family.
