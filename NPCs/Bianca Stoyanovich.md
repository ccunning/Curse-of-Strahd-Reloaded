# Bianca Stoyanovich

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Wants to keep [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]] safe, happy, and sane while protecting the pack and innocent Barovians from his wrath and hunger.
- Initially inspires sympathy with her anxiety and uncertainty but later endears herself through her good spirits.
- An herbalist living in the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Woods]]; stole items from [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]]'s wagon for nostalgic reasons.
- [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]]'s mate and wife, as well as the disinherited daughter of a noble family outside of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- An excitable, refined woman who is anxious and desperate to bring Kiril back from madness.
- Knows the layout of the [[Places/Werewolf Den|werewolf den]], including [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]]'s imprisonment and [[Places/Prison|prison]] guards.
- Can reveal her true nature as a **werewolf** after persuasion or intimidation checks.
- Infiltrated [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]]'s wagon and discovered its secret entrance.
- A **werewolf** and wife of Kiril Stoyanovich.
