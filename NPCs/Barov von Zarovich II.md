# Barov von Zarovich II

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Gained support from [[NPCs/Saint Andral|Saint Andral]]'s church as part of a holy war against the chaos in the region.
- Was the father of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and Sergei, and thus the line that shaped [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]'s history.
- Successfully conquered the [[Factions/Dusk Elves|dusk elves]], which allowed him to expand Zarovia.
- Led a crusade in search of his ancestral kingdom’s splendor.
- Descendant of King Barov I and heir to his legacy.
