# Kavan

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Has a cairn marked with runes describing him as 'Forsaken of the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]]' and 'First and Last Chieftain of the [[Places/Balinok Mountains|Balinok Mountains]]'.
- Defied his people's strictures and ventured into [[Places/Whispering Wall|the Whispering Wall]] where he learned secrets of the [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]]’s sacred spear.
- Banished from [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]] by the druids for hubris and betrayal, buried in a lonely cairn far from his forefathers.
- Former chieftain among [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]], who arrived in the valley long before his own birth.
- Warns players about [[NPCs/Svarog|Svarog]]’s strength; invites them back to his cairn if they defeat him.
- Led rebellions that eventually led to the destruction of [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]]'s cultural unity.
- Warred on other tribes and re-united them beneath his banner after stealing the spear.
- An ancient chieftain of the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] who appears as a spirit to provide guidance.
- Was banished by the druids after his actions, and cursed to dwell in a lonely cairn.
- A ghost of a former chieftain from the [[Places/Balinok Mountains|Balinok Mountains]], known as 'Blood-Drinker'.
- Wielded [[Items/Svarog's wand|Svarog's wand]], once used by those who connected his people to the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]].
- Stole the [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]]’s spear and imbued it with dark magic—creating [[Items/Bloodthorn|Bloodthorn]].
- Waged a bloody war upon neighboring tribes before forming a unified front.
- Once stole the Huntress's Spear and defaced shrines of the Ladies Three.
- Recognizes the wolf’s tooth as an artifact and symbol of the Huntress.
- Provides information about the druids of [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] and their plans.
- A young chieftain who destroyed the hierarchy of the druidic caste.
- Shares history regarding schism between Forest and [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]].
- Called the Blood-Drinker due to his use of the spear in war.
- Entered [[Places/Whispering Wall|the Whispering Wall]] and became corrupted by it.
- A knowledgeable figure who adds depth to the mythos.
- A young warrior who united the First Folk tribes.
- Encountered in [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]].

## DM Notes

- Reveals the existence of [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]] in [[Places/Tsolenka Pass|Tsolenka Pass]] as a potential location if players seek to reverse land corruption.
- His recognition may connect to further lore regarding the [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane]] or the Huntress.
- The location of [[Items/Svarog's wand|Svarog's wand]] may be key to further developments with the First Folk.
- [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]]’s lore introduces the [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] as a faction in need of exploration.
- His tale ties into the conflict between the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] and Mountain Folk.
- His burial site may be significant for future rituals or quests.
