# Patrina Velikovna

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- She orchestrated a plan to become a [[NPCs/Lich|lich]] and reclaim her people's power by draining the souls of players into the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]].
- She was involved in binding various vestiges within [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] and sought powerful artifacts like [[NPCs/Tenebrous|Tenebrous]]’s gift.
- Placed a platinum hilt of a shattered sword into the western sarcophagus of X42.01 [[Places/Amber Vault|Amber Vault]] before vanishing.
- [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina]] seeks vengeance against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd von Zarovich]], who she holds responsible for her suffering and death.
- Used the power of Khirad (the Star of Secrets) to manipulate [[NPCs/Meda|Meda]] into transforming into a [[NPCs/Nothic|nothic]].
- Used the power of [[NPCs/The Vampyr|the Vampyr]] and [[NPCs/Tenebrous|Tenebrous]] to become powerful, with ambitions to become a [[NPCs/Lich|lich]].
- Was betrayed by her lover and imprisoned in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] before turning her back on [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- A powerful dusk elf and former archmages' student who was imprisoned within the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] hilt.
- Transformed [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]]’s spirit into a ghost and sealed him away within the [[Places/Ritual Chamber|Ritual Chamber]].
- Played a significant part in manipulating events that led to the transformation of Strahd.
- Sought the secret of the Sunsword hilt hidden in a demiplane within the [[Items/Vampyr|Vampyr]]'s [[Places/Prison|prison]].
- A dusk elf who once worked with [[NPCs/Khazan|Khazan]] at [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] and later took control of it.
- Played a crucial role in pushing forward her own agenda within the landscape of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- A dusk elf archmage who discovered the Amber Temple nearly two thousand years ago.
- Wanted to seize Strahd’s authority by manipulating events and using prophecies.
- Brought Strahd von Zarovich and [[NPCs/Khazan|Khazan]] to the temple for her own purposes.
- Used this intel to contact [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir]] in dreams seeking to rise in power.
- Connected to darker forces and influenced many key historical shifts.
- Discovered the secrets of the Amber Temple and studied black magic.
- Sealed [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]]'s spirit away and plotted against him in secret.
- Sought immortality for herself and attempted to control Strahd.
- Disappeared a little over a year after Strahd’s first visit.
- A banshee who overheard information from Rahadin.
- [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir]]’s sister, an archmage with dark skills.

## DM Notes

- Her actions are directly related to how certain artifacts like the *sunlight sword* and key locations within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] were influenced by her.
- She is set up to have a strong presence or return during major story arcs involving manipulation or change in control of the valley.
- The players may learn more about her history and motives when dealing with her spirit or artifacts.
- Her return and actions are central to finishing the major campaign arc involving the Amber Temple.
- [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina]] is the key figure behind the Amber Temple’s fall and current state.
- Her death in the temple could influence [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]]' future actions.
