# Muriel Vinshaw

> **NPC** · 6 source files

## Key Facts

- Claims she is not a druid, and that she detests trees; she's seen a wolf with a bad face but not Strazni.
- She is associated with [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] through earlier scenes, though not explicitly named until later chapters.
- Uses a dagger from her boot that can be used to make a cut on her palm during summoning rituals.
- The blue-winged raven that communicates with players after they call out to the raven flock.
- A cheerful, scattered young woman serving as a field agent for [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- She has been injured by silver barbs and cannot fly or regenerate until they are removed.
- Has a blue streak in her hair, which is a hint to her true nature if questioned directly.
- Known to be spiritually descended from one of the original wereravens serving the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]].
- Later arrives at the falling castle to assist the players and [[NPCs/Sasha Ivliskova|Sasha]] with their escape.
- Possesses a regeneration ability if not harmed by silvered weapons or necrotic damage.
- Initially hides in raven form, then eventually joins the party after being rescued.
- A former employee of [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] who accompanies players on their journey.
- Seems to have been present during [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]]’s fall, possibly with a connection to him.
- Overheard part of the [[Items/Tarokka reading|Tarokka reading]] before being chased away by a [[NPCs/Greater Strix|greater strix]].
- A raven character who serves as an ally to the players, especially during combat.
- Actively involved in gathering intelligence for the [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|Keepers]] during [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s rule.
- Sends her flock to gather allies east of [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] near the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Woods]].
- She is a shapechanger who can take raven or hybrid forms with flight capability.
- Played a role in the early escape missions and may serve as guide or contact.
- Her presence signals a growing alliance between the parties and the keepers.
- Former fiancé of [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]] [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]], who was imprisoned during [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]]'s rebellion.
- Is connected to the Keepers of the Feather, providing them with support.
- Member of the Keepers of the Feather and recognizes [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian]] from a cloak.
- Appears in wolf form and later shifts into raven form to aid players.
- She is emotionally affected by learning of his final resting place.
- Also has 'plummet' feature for surprise attack from aerial descent.
- Is a secret wereraven and member of the Keepers of the Feather.
- Formerly the wereraven disguised as the creature at [[Places/Tser Pool Encampment|Tser Pool]].
- Appears to have been eavesdropping on [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] at [[Places/Tser Pool Encampment|Tser Pool]].
- Has a personal contact named [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] in the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Wood]].
- Is betrothed to Elric [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]], who she believes died.
- A key field operative with the Keepers of the Feather.
- A wereraven and member of the Keepers of the Feather.
- Betrothed to Elric Martikov but now mourns his loss.
- Elric Martikov's fiancée who recognized his feather.
- A character who sends ravens to reinforce [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
- Joins players to investigate the winery in Arc J.
- A wereraven who is also a shapechanger.
