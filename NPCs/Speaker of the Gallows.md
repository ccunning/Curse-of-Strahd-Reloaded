# Speaker of the Gallows

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Connected to both the historical events behind the Wachter family's curse and Lady Wachter's decision to protect herself with a binding circle.
- Has two distinct forms: [[NPCs/Speaker of the Gallows|Speaker of the Gallows]] (first form) and [[NPCs/Ba'Al Verzi Avenger|Ba'al Verzi Avenger]] (second form).
- Has a spectral appearance with skeletal hands and tattered robe showing faces in its cloak.
- Manifests as a malevolent presence haunting [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]] and attacking anyone within range.
- Aims to kill Stella Wachter and Lady Wachter's sons as an act of vengeance for his family.
- Its haunting was the source of the deaths of Balthazar, Lavinia, and Frederich Wachter.
- [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]]'s transformed state after death, formed by the spirits of castle guards.
- Can transform into a more powerful avenger form named [[Factions/Ba’al Verzi|Ba’al Verzi]] Avenger upon death.
- The vengeful spirit of [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]], a traitor killed by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Possesses reactions that include ghostly step, death's revelation, and indomitable.
- Clutches [[Items/The Tome of Strahd|the Tome of Strahd]] which he stole after murdering Lady Wachter's parents.
- An enemy of the Wachters who seeks revenge even after Leo’s original death.
- Has abilities like deathly visions, lashing chains, and foretelling touch.
- A supernatural entity with truesight and ethereal movement capabilities.

## DM Notes

- Represents a major antagonist whose backstory links directly to characters in Arc E and beyond.
- Signifies an increased threat level for Lady Wachter's family in the campaign narrative.
