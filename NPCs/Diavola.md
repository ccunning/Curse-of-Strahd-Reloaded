# Diavola

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Part of the supporting cast involved in managing the castle's security and upkeep during the storm.
- Called upon when the drawbridge tower runes are damaged by lightning.
- Requires a full coven to re-cast the runes, taking until morning.
- Flees back to her companions as instructed.
- A Barovian witch who reports to [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]].

## DM Notes

- More [[NPCs/Barovian witches|Barovian witches]] may become relevant if player actions trigger further involvement with the coven.
- She appears to be a significant part of the local supernatural forces.
