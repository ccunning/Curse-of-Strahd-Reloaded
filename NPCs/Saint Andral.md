# Saint Andral

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Died at the age of 82 in the valley, after [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s transformation.
- Beneath his honor was a crypt in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] named St. Andral’s Church.
- Later became Most High Priest of the Morninglord’s church.
- Supported King Barov's claim to Zarovia and crowned him.
- A spiritual leader among the Church of the Morninglord.
