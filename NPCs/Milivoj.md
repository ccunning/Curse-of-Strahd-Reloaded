# Milivoj

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Imprisoned in a cell in the South Dungeon after being captured in earlier events.
- Has a strong family attachment; serves his siblings despite difficult conditions.
- Despite his gruff exterior, is dedicated and caring toward family.
- Relocated to his family home to deepen character motivation.
- The theft was orchestrated with clues pointing toward him.
- The prime witness to the crime (the theft of the bones).
- Secretly works for coffin maker [[NPCs/Henrik|Henrik]] van der Voort.
- Works as the groundskeeper at St. Andral’s Church.
