## Key Facts

- Demonstrated his ability to manipulate people through disguise and deception.
- Part of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s method for controlling events outside his castle.
- Symbolized the extent of Strahd's reach and planning in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Used to appear as a nobleman and hide his identity from others.
