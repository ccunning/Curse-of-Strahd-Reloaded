# Parriwimple

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- He fights for peace but will use his strength to restrain combatants if necessary, especially to protect his uncle.
- He is the nephew of Bildrath Cantemir, the general-store owner, and wishes to honor his late parents' memories.
- A cheerful, simple-minded orphan who desperately seeks to prove his usefulness after the death of his parents.
- His primary motivation is to help neighbors and take care of his uncle.
