# Tatyana Federovna

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- Used symbolically or historically in the castle’s narrative as a reminder of past events and relationships.
- Referred to by Lief as a significant other with implications for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s emotional state and past choices.
- Her portrait is described as being associated with [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]], though she has no direct connection with her.
- Named in the description of the portrait of [[NPCs/Tatyana Federovna|Tatyana Federovna]] mentioned by [[NPCs/Lief Lipsiege|Lief Lipsiege]].
- Referred to as a former love of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] whose portrait is kept in the third-floor study.
- She was the original soul of [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]] and has been reincarnated multiple times.
- She was once offered a choice by [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]] to end her life, which she accepted.
- She is connected to the concept of reincarnation within certain mystical cycles.
- Fell in love with Sergei after meeting him, but was pursued by Strahd as well.
- Was killed by Strahd during an attempt to escape him after he murdered Sergei.
- Her soul was sought after by Strahd for millennia, compelling his actions.
- Landed in a chasm near [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] as part of the [[Places/Mists|Mists]]’ influence.
- A character involved in a suicide, mentioned as potentially triggering.
- She leapt from the [[Places/Pillarstone of Ravenloft|Pillarstone of Ravenloft]] in an earlier life.
- Became an integral part of Strahd's descent into vampirism.
- Her death reflects the dark themes of the campaign.
- Strahd's love interest and Sergei's fiancée.

## DM Notes

- While not directly active, [[NPCs/Tatyana Federovna|Tatyana]]'s legacy continues to influence events in the castle.
- Her role in reincarnation will drive significant character arcs and narrative tension.
- Players may uncover more about her or find artifacts tied to her.
