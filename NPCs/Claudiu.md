# Claudiu

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Shares his family's melancholia but is more pessimistic about future battles.
- Feels that resisting [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] is hopeless and anyone who does so is doomed.
- Suffers from deep guilt after [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]]'s death, especially towards Elvir.
- Son of Dag and Stefania, brother to [[NPCs/Martin|Martin]], Viggo, and [[NPCs/Yolanda|Yolanda]].
