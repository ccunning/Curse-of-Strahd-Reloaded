# Marina Ulrich

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- One of [[NPCs/Tatyana Federovna|Tatyana]]’s reincarnations and the first person [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] hunted in pursuit of her spirit.
- Used as part of the ongoing cycle that led to the repeated loss of [[NPCs/Tatyana Federovna|Tatyana]]’s soul.
- Fell prey to the [[Places/Mists|Mists]]' promises before being killed and sent to a monument.
- Symbolized how even after death, her presence continued to haunt [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Set up a pattern of repeated losses for Strahd’s pursuit.
