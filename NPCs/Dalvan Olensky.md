# Dalvan Olensky

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- His body was later found in [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Woods]] by newcomers entering [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]; his clothing resembled that of a [[NPCs/Skeletal Rider|skeletal rider]] encountered at the crossroads.
- His story is known only through [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]]'s revelations—she does not reveal specifics about how his prophecy unfolded.
- He insisted upon hearing and defying his fate, leading to tragic consequences.
- A Barovian who once visited [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]]’s tent after the siege on [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
