# Tarakamedes, the Grave Wyrm

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Known for 'Fly' spell and a boon that grants speed equal to walking speed in flight, descending 1 foot per every 10 feet horizontally traveled.
- The condition is malformed skeletal wings sprouting from the back with need to eat bones or dirt daily or gain exhaustion.
- Connected to the Temple and related to events that lead to full transformation through [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- A vestige connected to an [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] tied to [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Participates in Stage Three of corruption.
