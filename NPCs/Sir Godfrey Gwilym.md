# Sir Godfrey Gwilym

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- Feels sadness, remorse for Vladimir’s situation, hope that someone will arrive to restore [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]'s power, and distrust towards those seeking [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- A tortured [[NPCs/Revenant|revenant]] bound by his oath to Vladimir, the husband of [[NPCs/Vladimir Horngaard|Vladimir Horngaard]] (whom he believes has forgotten their relationship).
- Sir [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Godfrey]] is a central figure in the past of [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] and has been largely forgotten by Vladimir due to his trauma from war.
- Knows the history of the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]], its founder [[NPCs/Argynvost|Lord Argynvost]], and how [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] desecrated Argynvost’s remains.
- Is the keeper of Sir [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Godfrey]]’s bindings, magical black wires used to force obedience to Vladimir's will during undeath.
- The players may find references to his rings or portrait as evidence of shared history during their investigation.
- Serves as a guide to the mansion and is cautious about the players’ potential connection with [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Introduces the players to Argynvost's spirit and explains that Argynvost has been stirring and seeking heroes.
- He was instrumental in the Order's earlier days and is invoked when discussing the order's values and history.
- Connected with important figures such as [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir]] and others attempting to uncover ancient secrets.
- An ancient [[NPCs/Revenant|revenant]] knight of the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]] bound by oath to [[NPCs/Vladimir Horngaard|Vladimir Horngaard]].
- A knight mentioned as waiting in [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] for those who fight evil inside [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- His portrait hangs in [[Places/Vladimir’s Bedroom|Vladimir’s bedroom]], showing him as one of two men with featureless faces.
- One of only two knights remaining with Vladimir Horngaard after his banishment of others.
- His presence adds a significant element of historical context in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]’s narrative.
- Served as a bridge between past and present during the time when loyalty was tested.
- Killed during the Battle of the [[Places/Luna River|Luna River]] along with Horngaard and other knights.
- Was once a member of the Order of the Silver Dragon and is loyal to his oaths.
- Desires to meet heroes who will seek out the whispers of Argynvost's spirit.
- Married to Vladimir Horngaard and fought in the Order of the Silver Dragon.
- Represented a valiant sacrifice made for justice and peace in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- His story remains part of the history behind the Order's destruction.
- A knight of the Silver Dragon and husband to Vladimir Horngaard.
- Visited Argynvostholt where he was warned against entering.
- An influential figure in the early days of resistance.

## DM Notes

- His influence over Argynvost’s spirit and the possibility of being contacted by him if the players are in possession of a powerful item, such as the '[[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]]' foretelling.
- His interactions may influence whether the players are allowed access to Argynvost's beacon; may have information on retrieving Argynvost's remains.
- Sir Godfrey’s warnings or interactions foreshadow possible dangers ahead for explorers entering the temple area.
