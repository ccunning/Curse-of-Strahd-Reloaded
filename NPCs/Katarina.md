# Katarina

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Fled [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s rampage during his transformation into a vampire and came upon the tree where [[NPCs/Varushka|Varushka]] once waited for her.
- Made a pact with the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]] to become powerful enough to stop [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and restore sunlight to [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] once more.
- She seems to be important to [[NPCs/Varushka|Varushka]] emotionally but is unknown to players at this point in the campaign.
- Created a note that leads players to discover a locket hidden in an old oak tree near the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Woods]].
- Left a wooden box containing half of a locket necklace made of tarnished silver as a gift to Varushka.
- The connection between [[NPCs/Katarina|Katarina]] and Varushka's story reveals personal loss and emotional depth.
- A girl mentioned by Varushka as someone who gave her a half of the necklace.
- Became a recurring presence after the fall of Strahd as a symbol of hope.
- Used her newfound powers to manipulate fate and plan against Strahd.
- Served as a maid in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] during Strahd's rise to power.
- Former servant of [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]], also known as [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]].
- Went into exile at [[Places/Tser Pool Encampment|Tser Pool]] where she became [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]].
- Strahd’s bastard daughter born to a [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] woman.
