> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- They are the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]'s servants who approached the Krezkovs after [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala]]’s death.
- They delivered the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]'s message and invitation to visit him in the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]].
- They serve as agents of the Abbot in controlling events around Krezk.
- They appear to play a key role in communicating with the players.
- They are part of his network of influence over the villagers.
