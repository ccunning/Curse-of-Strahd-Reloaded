# Doru

> **NPC** · 6 source files

## Key Facts

- Led a failed assault on [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] that only awakened [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and led to himself being transformed into a [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]].
- Previously known to have been transformed into a vampire through his own bloodlust, now largely controlled.
- His primary motivations involve reconciling with his [[NPCs/Father Donavich|father Donavich]] and ensuring [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]]'s safety.
- In battle, he avoids conflict unless endangered, often fleeing but fighting fiercely if needed.
- He agrees to assist in saving [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]] but declines to go into the castle due to past trauma.
- [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]] knows key parts of the castle layout including the locations of tombs and corridors.
- [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]] is a [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]] whose vampiric nature struggles against his desire to stay good.
- Seeking his betrothed Gertruda whom he believes has gone missing to [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Has joined [[NPCs/Father Donavich|Donavich]] on a quest to find Gertruda, having reconciled with his father.
- He offers rough maps to help players navigate the castle during the heist.
- Had connections with [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric Martikov]] who was imprisoned while aiding Doru.
- Accidentally awakened [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]], leading to the imprisonment of Gertruda.
- Recognized Rudolph van Richten from *Van Richten's Guide to Vampires*.
- His actions initiated the current timeline of events within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Became [[NPCs/Escher|Escher]]’s companion after Strahd summoned him back to [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- He is known to [[NPCs/Escher|Escher]] as a former friend and betrothed to Gertruda.
- A vampire spawn who formerly served under Lord Zarovich.
- A young man determined to lead a crusade against Strahd.
- A vampire spawn at war with his bloodthirsty nature.
- Involved in a rebellion against Castle Ravenloft.
- Gertruda is motivated to confirm Doru's safety.
- Reconciled with his father, priest Donavich.
- Son of Father Donavich, a Barovian priest.
- Doru is the betrothed of Gertruda.
- Encountered early in Act I.

## DM Notes

- Due to being converted by Strahd, Duoru is directly connected to key future narrative elements and may be encountered again during adventure sequences involving vampires or past actions.
- His connection to both the vampire spawn world and the human faith may provide useful insights.
- May be an important clue or ally when players investigate the whereabouts of Gertruda.
