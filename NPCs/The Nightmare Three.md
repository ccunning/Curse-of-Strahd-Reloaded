# The Nightmare Three

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Each member assumes new combat strategies and abilities typical to their transformed bodies: Bella has Bite, Tail, and Tail Swipe attacks; [[NPCs/Offalia Wormwiggle|Offalia]] uses Gore, Charge, Stomp, Tusks, Fiend's Trumpet, and Enervating Ring; [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] gains Eyebite, Inflict Nightmares, Matron's Rebuke, [[NPCs/Shadow|Shadow]] Step, and Absorb Elements reactions.
- They retain all previous statistics but gain enhanced combat power in their new monstrous forms, making them more dangerous than before.
- In this form, Bella becomes a Huge crocodile, [[NPCs/Offalia Wormwiggle|Offalia]] becomes a Huge elephant, and [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] remains enveloped in [[NPCs/Shadow|shadow]] forms.
- The second form of [[Factions/The Bonegrinder Coven|the Bonegrinder Coven]] after it reaches 0 hit points, transforming into one medium and two huge fiends.
- The form change occurs instantly upon reaching zero HP, transitioning directly into a phase of increased threat level.
- Each has increased stats and unique abilities like charge, fiery attacks, inflicting nightmares, etc.
- Consists of a huge crocodile (Bella), an elephant (Offalia), and Morgantha shrouded in shadows.
- A transformed form of [[Factions/The Bonegrinder Coven|the Bonegrinder Coven]] when reduced to 0 hit points.
- Has access to new spells and action types not present in hag form.
- Is a more formidable opponent after the transformation.

## DM Notes

- As a transformational stage, representing the coven's true fury and the potential for further escalation in their conflict with the party.
