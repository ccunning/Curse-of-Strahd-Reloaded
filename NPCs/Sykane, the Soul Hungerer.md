# Sykane, the Soul Hungerer

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- The condition is yellow glowing eyes with disadvantage on Charisma (Deception).
- A vestige associated with an [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] found in [[Places/Tsolenka Pass|Tsolenka Pass]].
- Participates in the corruption process at Stage Three.
- Found in an arc related to the Trials of the Mountain.
