# Willemina Rikalova

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Her story highlights the cruelty of the Baron and the risks faced by refugees.
- She is seen as someone with possible influence over [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]]’s behavior.
- Suffers public humiliation as part of the town politics and justice system.
- Is involved in the struggle with Udo Lukovich, her imprisoned son.
- An old woman found by players and later arrested by Baron Vargas.

## DM Notes

- Sets up her narrative importance in relation to [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]] and his history.
- Represents victimization of common individuals under oppressive rule.
