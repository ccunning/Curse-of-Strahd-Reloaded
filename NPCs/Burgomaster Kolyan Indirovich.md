# Burgomaster Kolyan Indirovich

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Named as one of the few leaders who remained to defend [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] against the threat posed by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Supported [[NPCs/Lugdana|Lugdana]]’s legacy and was influential in encouraging resistance against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s return.
- Burgomaster of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] who welcomed Rudolph van Richten upon his arrival in the valley.
- Later became central to the local governance under fear of Strahd's influence.
- His son is [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark Kolyanovich]], a brave hero who died during the siege.
- Deceased father of [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]] and [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark Kolyanovich]].
- Buried in Barovia near the end of Act I.

## DM Notes

- He is likely to appear or be remembered directly as part of a narrative that involves local heroes or defense.
