# Klara

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Murdered by Elisabeth and her son Walter during the climax of their ritual.
- A nursemaid of the Durst family accused of having an affair with Gustav.
- His story revealed how the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] touched ordinary lives even in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Part of tragic love and betrayal that influenced events in the valley.
- Symbolized the fall of normal society under corruption.
