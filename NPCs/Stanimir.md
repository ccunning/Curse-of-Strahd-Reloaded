# Stanimir

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Shares general information about [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] and [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] lore but avoids discussing certain secrets like soulless Barovians and [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]].
- Known to use *major image* spell instead of *vampiric touch*, indicating advanced magical knowledge or misdirection.
- Knows that a coven of night hags lives in the old windmill known as Old Durst Mill.
- Does not reveal details about [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s business nor his current whereabouts.
- A jovial, old Vistana who helps players navigate the [[Places/Tser Pool Encampment|Tser Pool encampment]].
- The [[Items/Stone Crest|stone crest]] he carries holds significance for the [[Factions/Ladies of the Fanes|ladies of the Fanes]].
- [[NPCs/Stanimir|Stanimir]] is an old [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] man carrying a wagon painted with colors.
- He delivers tokens of esteem from [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]] to [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]].

## DM Notes

- May have deeper knowledge relating to the prophecy revealed during the [[Items/Tarokka reading|Tarokka reading]].
