# Anton Konstantinovich

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- He is either turned into a zombie in [[Places/Torture Chamber|the torture chamber]] or imprisoned inside a cage hanging above it.
- Has severed fingertips and was traumatized by his experience at the castle during the dinner scene.
- Worked as a servant in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]], serving wine to guests while under [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]]’s torture.
- His wife [[NPCs/Dezdrelda Konstaninova|Dezdrelda Konstaninova]] was fed to ghouls after her corpse was drained for blood.
- He shares information with players about stairways leading from [[Places/Brazier Room|the Brazier Room]].
- Tortured alongside his wife Dezdrelda by [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]] for her twisted amusement.
- Serves as a symbolic reminder of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s brutal treatment of his inmates.
- A tongueless man whose mouth has been cut out, with no tongue or speech.

## DM Notes

- He is either imprisoned as a zombie or in a cage and provides important information for escape strategies.
- His condition and role suggest more atrocities connected to Volenta or the castle's dark history.
- Could play a part in revealing deeper motives or history related to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s court or victims.
