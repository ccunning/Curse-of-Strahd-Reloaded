## Key Facts

- Shares motivation for not wanting to hide any longer, after her experiences with [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]] and seeing the importance of fighting directly.
- She is the adopted sister of [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark Kolyanovich]], unknowingly the sister of [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]] and the reincarnation of [[NPCs/Tatyana Federovna|Tatyana Federovna]].
- In conflict, she prefers to speak first before drawing her rapier, which she wields reluctantly unless defending someone else.
- The possibility of her soul being taken is posed as a choice by [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]] to prevent [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] from taking control of her.
- Is described as the one whose soul was sacrificed to secure peace for [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] in the future vision shown to the players.
