# Leo Dilisnya

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- His actions were crucial points in triggering major events involving revenge, supernatural justice, and the fate of others.
- Served a role in linking events from the present storylines back to earlier occurrences related to the curse’s roots.
- May have direct or indirect influence over other characters whose journeys involve conflict with the supernatural.
- Was turned into a [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]] by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]], then sealed in a vault beneath [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]] for 400 years.
- The source of a curse that affected the Wachter lineage and caused his own death from starvation.
- Holds [[Items/The Tome of Strahd|the Tome of Strahd]], which he stole from [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]] after killing Lady Wachter's parents.
- He was last seen in the servants' quarters before fleeing outdoors possibly into the courtyard.
- A powerful entity who destroyed Stella Wachter's spirit upon her arrival in the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]].
- Rises as a gallows speaker after his death, avenging the Wachter family and seeking justice.
- His presence connects with past legends of destructive vengeance tied to [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Former guard of [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] who gave orders to kill [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] on [[Places/Sergei's Wedding|Sergei's wedding]] day.
- A former captain of Strahd's guard, who has gone missing and is referred to by [[NPCs/Varushka|Varushka]].
- Tracked down by Strahd as a [[Factions/Vampire Spawn|vampire spawn]] and imprisoned beneath the Wachter manor.
- His presence suggests a role in the broader history or politics of the castle.
- His story revealed both personal vengeance and strategic value.
- Used by [[NPCs/Lovina Wachter|Lovina Wachter]] to ensure his slow, drawn-out death.
- Symbolized the consequences of betrayal in Strahd’s domain.
- One of the traitors who attempted to assassinate Sergei.
