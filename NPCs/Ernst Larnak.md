# Ernst Larnak

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Ernst is revealed as an antagonist in the later Arc S - The Moonlit Murders following the events of this arc.
- His role expands beyond mere information sharing—he aids in planning actions against [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]].
- He delivers invitations and updates on [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]]’s schedule to the players.
- [[NPCs/Ernst Larnak|Ernst Larnak]] is [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]]’s spy, courier, and confidant.

## DM Notes

- His familiarity with the town and access to information indicate deep integration into [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]]’s politics.
- Ernst's knowledge suggests his future role as a double agent in a later arc.
