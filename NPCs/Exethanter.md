# Exethanter

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- He was bound by [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina]] after refusing to help her reach lichhood, remaining trapped within [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] for centuries.
- His ghost disappeared from the temple about a year after his death and is believed to be involved in [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina]]'s affairs.
- [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]] provides crucial backstory about ancient events and how corruption spread throughout the temple.
- His death at the hands of Vilnius initiated a series of events leading to the creation of [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]].
- Was betrayed by his apprentice Vilnius and poisoned with an enchanted ewer that he used to drink from.
- His [[Items/Staff of Frost|staff of frost]] is now in possession of Vilnius, who seeks it for freedom from the temple's [[Places/Prison|prison]].
- His knowledge about the temple's layout and secrets could be valuable for players seeking artifacts.
- The amulet he wore was stolen by Vilnius, who used it as part of the key ritual to control him.
- If shown to him during [[Arc S - A Sword of Sunlight]], he becomes more friendly and welcoming.
- Was poisoned by Vilnius, along with seven mages (five apprentices and two senior mages).
- He discovered and took in Vilnius as an apprentice when observing his magical aptitude.
- Helps players access information on rituals and [[Items/The Tome of Strahd|the Tome of Strahd]] when they seek it.
- A former archmage and leader of the secret society that built [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- His ghost was sealed away in the [[Places/Ritual Chamber|Ritual Chamber]] after Patrina overpowered him.
- Provided guidance on the secrets of immortality within the temple.
- Lived with his mages and apprentices in the ancient Amber Temple.
- One of the powerful entities that helped shape [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s journey.
- Symbolized the influence of dark magic and its seductive nature.
- Key to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s decision not to embrace immortality immediately.
- [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]] was the archmage of the Amber Temple before Vilnius.
- An archmage who led a secret society of good-aligned wizards.
- Gives insights into Strahd’s plans using [[Items/The Tome of Strahd|the Tome of Strahd]].
- Former archmage and guardian of the Amber Temple.
- A [[NPCs/Lich|lich]] residing in the Amber Temple in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- A [[NPCs/Lich|lich]] who welcomed Strahd to the Amber Temple.
- His spirit is held captive by [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]].
- The [[Items/Crumpled Page|crumpled page]] originated from his [[Places/Library|library]].
- Recognizes the page upon being approached.
- Lich with restored memory.

## DM Notes

- His ghost may serve as either a guide or a potential obstacle depending on how the characters interact with him.
- His death and spirit tie into the ongoing narrative about corrupted mages and the need for retribution.
- He can offer guidance regarding powerful spells and artifacts, including the Rite of Conjured Divinity.
- Exethanter's death and his staff play central roles in understanding the Amber Temple's dark history.
- May play role in guiding use of the page towards solving riddles or completing puzzles.
- The information he possesses might unlock additional areas of the Amber Temple.
