# Ismark Kolyanovich

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- His motivations include keeping his village and [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] safe and honoring his ancestor’s memory despite personal insecurity.
- [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark]] is a courageous, reliable, and compassionate leader who is deeply uncertain of matching his ancestor’s legacy.
- Willingly lets players rest in the burgomaster's mansion if they arrive via teleportation brazier.
- He is the adoptive brother of [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]] and the great-grandson of [[NPCs/Ismark Antonovich|Ismark the Great]].
- He has a strong sense of guilt and a desire to step beyond his legacy.
- Sister is Ireena Kolyana; father of [[NPCs/Burgomaster Kolyan Indirovich|Burgomaster Kolyan Indirovich]].
- First NPC met by players when arriving in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Burgomaster of [[Places/Village of Barovia|the village of Barovia]].
- Surprised to be reunited with Ireena.
