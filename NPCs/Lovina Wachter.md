# Lovina Wachter

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Used by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] as leverage in future dealings to maintain control over the valley.
- A noblewoman from the House of Wachter who sought revenge for [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]].
- Helped [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] track down and capture him in the Valley of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Had a personal stake in ensuring that Leo’s fate would be sealed.
- Represented an example of familial loyalty to the Wachter House.
