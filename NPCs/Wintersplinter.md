# Wintersplinter

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Born under specific conditions in the [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] ritual involving an enchanted gemstone.
- The final goal of the ritual; destruction of this entity is a key objective for players.
- Can be defeated by players and its heart contains the stolen enchanted gemstone.
- Is described as a fearsome [[NPCs/Tree Blight|tree blight]] threatening [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] winery.
- A being that the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] attempt to summon using a ritual at [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
- The result of a druidic ritual to create new life from [[Items/Gulthias tree|the Gulthias Tree]].
- Linked with druids’ plans and may be related to the [[NPCs/Tree Blight|tree blight]].
- Expected to march upon the winery soon, threatening its fate.
- Related to the destruction of the winery in Act III.
- A feared figure or entity connected to Yester Hill.
- Tree blight that attacks [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]].

## DM Notes

- [[NPCs/Wintersplinter|Wintersplinter]] will be the primary antagonist in the final section of this arc.
