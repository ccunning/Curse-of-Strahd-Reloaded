# Barovian witches

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- One is [[NPCs/Diavola|Diavola]], the leader with additional 2nd-level spell slot and potion of greater healing.
- Includes hexwitches, bogwitches, and stormwitches with varying spell preparations.
- Used the cauldron to transform a zombie into a zombie plague spreader.
- Lead a secret coven that experiments with undead creation.
- A group of six witches residing in the South Tower.
