# Prince Erevan Löwenhart

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- A key figure in the history of the Von Zarovich family and the sword’s legacy.
- His line's descendants played a significant role in post-[[NPCs/Beucephalus|Beucephalus]] events.
- His moonblade, shattered by Barov, was used to forge the [[Items/Brightblade|Brightblade]].
- Dusk elf prince who opposed King Barov and refused to pay tribute.
- His story connects directly to themes of vengeance or retribution.
- Symbol of defiance, justice, and the suffering of a fallen race.
- His spirit was sealed within the [[Items/Brightblade|Brightblade]] by [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]].
- Had his blade broken by Rahadin, leading to his execution.
- An elven prince mentioned as a father figure to Rahadin.

## DM Notes

- May be relevant in future confrontations with Rahadin or in resolving racial or historical conflicts.
