# Franz

> **NPC** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] uses his plight as a bargaining tool to manipulate the players, even suggesting that his presence might lead to punishment if they do not comply with her requests.
- He plays no direct active role in the narrative unless the parties choose to interact with the coven further post-rescue of his children.
- Lives in a tent outside [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|the Abbey of Saint Markovia]], likely involved in some form of research or exploration with [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]].
- Is said to be one of the individuals who received information or care from [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] during Arc H - The Lost Soul.
- Connected indirectly with the activities of the coven and the use of the windmill as a base for their operations.
- Not explicitly involved beyond being a client or dealer, but key to the narrative of Morgantha’s operations.
- His personality and role within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] are not fully defined beyond his mention in relation to [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]].
- His story leads players to discover that Morgantha's lair is a windmill used to entrap children.
- Barovian refugee whose children [[NPCs/Fyodor|Fyodor]] and [[NPCs/Myrtle|Myrtle]] are held captive in the windmill by Morgantha.
- He is now deeply distressed, believing his children are suffering in the hands of Morgantha.
- He serves as a contact and dealer for Morgantha’s dream pastries in the coven context.
- Has a [[NPCs/Night Hag|night hag]]'s nightmare haunting that paralyzes him and slowly wastes his body.
- A character mentioned in relation to Morgantha, the dream peddler of night hags.
- His capture is tied to a larger story about refugees within [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s domain.
- A widower refugee suffering from a wasting disease caused by nightmares.
- Sold his two children [[NPCs/Myrtle|Myrtle]] and [[NPCs/Fyodor|Fyodor]] to Morgantha for dream pastries.
- Known by the hags through their relationship with him and his family.
- [[NPCs/Franz|Franz]] is a character who owns a tent in the Lost Soul region.
- He may be connected to the [[Factions/Barovian Refugees|Barovian refugees]].

## DM Notes

- [[NPCs/Franz|Franz]] represents one of the many innocent victims caught within [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s schemes, indicating the scope of his influence on refugees beyond the immediate party.
- Likely central in the unfolding mysteries during the next arcs, especially related to Morgantha’s identity or her coven’s actions.
- Could serve as a link in tracking down more information about how Morgantha influences events within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Could be part of larger plots involving Morgantha’s activities.
