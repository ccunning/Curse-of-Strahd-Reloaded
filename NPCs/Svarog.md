# Svarog

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Wields a yew wand which is sought after as part of an objective for [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] after defeating him.
- Initially part of the circle, but becomes an active combatant when players enter the circle.
- Leader of the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk druids]] attacking [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] winery.
- Has special abilities such as 'Gust of Wind' and 'Bind Elements'.
- A [[NPCs/Druid Assailant|druid naturalist]] who wields a wand made from petrified wood.
- Is the one who retrieves [[Items/Svarog's wand|Svarog's wand]], as mentioned by [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]].

## DM Notes

- Player characters will fight [[NPCs/Svarog|Svarog]] in the climactic battle on [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
