# Belladonna Sunbane

> **NPC** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Member of [[Factions/The Bonegrinder Coven|the Bonegrinder Coven]] and a [[NPCs/Night Hag|night hag]] who specializes in *blindness/deafness*, *phantasmal force*, *crown of madness*, and *putrid radiance*.
- In the second phase as a crocodile, uses *Bite* and *Tail* attacks, along with *Tail Swipe*, *Hallucinatory Mist*, and *Tail Lash* reactions.
- Must be located in the windmill at [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]] along with other members to ensure their names are known for binding rituals.
- She can use crown of madness, phantasmal force, and is part of [[NPCs/The Nightmare Three|The Nightmare Three]] form where she becomes a Huge crocodile.
- In Nightmare Three form, has a large crocodile body and uses her *Tail* and *Bite* attacks effectively in combat.
- Possess dark magical abilities and may have played roles in abducting children including [[NPCs/Myrtle|Myrtle]] and [[NPCs/Fyodor|Fyodor]].
- She has multiple spellcastings including putting out green light bursts and casting phantasmal force.
- Is susceptible to a spell like *Counterspell* or *Absorb Elements* if attacked by magical means.
- Part of a group that can threaten Lady Wachter's allies during an assault on [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]]’s lair.
- Attacks players with *Claws*, *Blindness/Deafness*, *Putrid Radiance*, or *Spectral Shadows*.
- She uses her bonus action to maintain control over targets affected by crown of madness.
- Their fate is tied with freeing and defeating [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] through a full confrontation.
- In the coven, she can cast blindness/deafness and use spectral shadows as a reaction.
- Bella is one of the three night hags forming [[Factions/The Bonegrinder Coven|the Bonegrinder Coven]].
- Daughters of Morgantha, likely part of her coven of night hags.
