# Myrtle

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Together with her brother, [[NPCs/Myrtle|Myrtle]] can be used as a lever against [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] if players attempt to negotiate after the binding circle is raised.
- Child imprisoned with [[NPCs/Fyodor|Fyodor]] in the windmill, used to obtain the contract and to bargain for safe passage or rescue.
- They’re not particularly involved in the greater story beyond their roles as leverage points for negotiation.
- Held captive due to their father [[NPCs/Franz|Franz]]’s role within the narrative of refugees affected by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s actions.
- If they are rescued, they return to their father, [[NPCs/Franz|Franz]], who is part of the local refugee community.
- Are likely held as victims inside the windmill where their mothers dwell and practice dark rituals.
- Their fate is central to uncovering how to properly bind the [[NPCs/Night Hag|night hag]] coven in [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]]'s lair.
- Young children abducted by Morgantha, who are imprisoned in the windmill near [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]].
- Their father Franz believes they are suffering under the hags' manipulation and cruelty.
- May become important assets in bargaining or rescuing if freed from Morgantha’s grip.

## DM Notes

- Their presence in the windmill ties directly into broader campaign elements concerning refugees and [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s influence over life in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
