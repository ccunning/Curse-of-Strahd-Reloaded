# Tenebrous

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Its presence affects events and interactions within the [[Places/Amber Vault|Amber Vault]] and is crucial in understanding the deeper lore.
- [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina]] sought the power from this entity but ultimately failed to gain entry unless she was prepared.
- Known for offering the secret of lichdom to those worthy enough to receive it.
- A vestige embodying the lost divinity of Orcus, also known as [[NPCs/Tenebrous|Tenebrous]].
