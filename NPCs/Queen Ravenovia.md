# Queen Ravenovia

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Her presence marked a significant moment in the lives of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and Sergei.
- Buried in the crypts of [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] upon arrival with her son Sergei.
- [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and Sergei's mother, the Queen of Zarovia before Strahd’s reign.
- Died shortly after arriving in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] during the transition period.
- Symbolized the legacy of the Von Zarovich family.
