# Night Hag

> **NPC** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Are dangerous opponents with magical abilities; binding them requires understanding their names and contracts.
- A creature found in the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] and involved in stealing souls through nightmares.
- Part of a coven including [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]], her daughters Bella Sunbane and [[NPCs/Offalia Wormwiggle|Offalia Wormwiggle]].
- Known for having heartstones that can be used to enter or traverse the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]].
- Can use heartstones to travel ethereally instantly.
