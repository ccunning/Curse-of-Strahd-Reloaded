# Father Lucian Petrovich

> **NPC** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- His presence provides tactical benefits via his *spirit guardians* spell and modified *divine eminence* feature.
- Appears to mediate between conflict and the party, often providing calm during tense situations.
- Mentors orphan boy Yeska, who has become part of his life after the loss of his own family.
- Is interrupted by [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]] during a sermon, who proceeds to search his office for [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]].
- Used as a diplomatic buffer who can offer spiritual guidance or assistance.
- Plays a central role in missions to restore bones and uncover secrets.
- Has knowledge about local events, notably St. Andral's missing bones.
- Has been instrumental in keeping [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] safe prior to her kidnapping.
- Has a hidden room in the church used as a third bedroom for Ireena.
- Protects Ireena while she's at the church during [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]]’s first hunt.
- A priest who accompanies the players to the [[Places/Coffin Maker's Shop|coffin maker's shop]].
- Involvement in helping resolve confrontation with Baron Vargas.
- He is the kind and compassionate priest of [[Places/St. Andral's Church|St. Andral's Church]].
- Has a personal struggle with faith under [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s influence.
- Priest of [[Places/St. Andral's Church|St. Andral's Church]] and key NPC in Act II.
- A priest from St. Andral's Church in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
- A priest of St. Andral’s Church in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
- Works closely with groundskeeper [[NPCs/Milivoj|Milivoj]].

## DM Notes

- His connection to Ireena’s concealment within the church plays a role in the political tension of Vallaki.
- Shows potential involvement in upcoming storylines involving religious or historical aspects.
- He may provide insight into the greater conflict and the nature of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s influence.
- Establishes character dynamics between good and evil forces in the town.
