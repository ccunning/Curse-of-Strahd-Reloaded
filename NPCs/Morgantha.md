# Morgantha

> **NPC** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- The primary leader of [[Factions/The Bonegrinder Coven|the Bonegrinder Coven]], a [[NPCs/Night Hag|night hag]] with the ability to assume multiple forms and possess magical powers including *etherealness*, *clairvoyance*, *dispel magic*, *lightning bolt*, and *eyebite*.
- Reveals three secrets when parleying with the players; these include [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s secret temple, his plan to escape from the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] via three shrines, and that his eldest wife [[NPCs/Sasha Ivliskova|Sasha Ivliskova]] is a traitor.
- Known to have imprisoned the children [[NPCs/Fyodor|Fyodor]] and [[NPCs/Myrtle|Myrtle]], and is willing to bargain with player characters for their release, especially after a binding circle is raised by Lady Wachter.
- She leads the coven in both normal and Nightmare Three phases and has the ability to use her *soul bag* to trap souls upon death for nefarious purposes.
- Inhabits the windmill near [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]] where she prepares dream pastries with a special effect known to change perceptions or trigger dreams.
- Seeks to avoid detection while traveling through [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], possibly protecting other fiendish activities from being discovered within her.
- Holds a [[Items/Charm of Mass Heroism|charm of mass heroism]] which bestows the effect of a *potion of heroism* on creatures within 30 feet upon activation.
- [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] is a cheerful old lady who runs a dream pastry business and is also the black-hearted matron of a [[NPCs/Night Hag|night hag]] coven.
- Has a lair located inside a windmill in [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]], where she keeps her [[Items/Heartstone|heartstone]] and possibly her young daughters.
- Lends her [[Items/Heartstone|heartstone]] under condition that players deliver a [[Items/Soul Leech|soul leech]] to Rahadin, [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s chamberlain.
- A peddler who sells dream pastries to refugees and has been involved in the abduction of children.
- She can become part of a hag coven that transforms into [[NPCs/The Nightmare Three|The Nightmare Three]] when reduced to 0 HP.
- Known for not revealing much about her own identity and past, especially regarding her husband.
- Connected with the dream pastries and introduces the concept of dreams and their consequences.
- A peddler disguised as an old woman and the mother of two night hags named Bella and [[NPCs/Offalia Wormwiggle|Offalia]].
- In combat, she seeks to parley or escape using etherealness if peaceful resolution fails.
- Used nightmares and heartstones to kidnap and enslave children from the refugee camp.
- The mother of a coven of night hags, including Bella Sunbane and [[NPCs/Offalia Wormwiggle|Offalia Wormwiggle]].
- Symbolizes the rising presence of dark forces seeking control over life and death.
- Connected with events beyond the village due to her influence on various factions.
- Has two daughters in her coven and plans are revealed for future quests.
- Founded a coven in Old Bonegrinder, selling dream pastries to refugees.
- Is a member of a coven of night hags who resides in an old windmill.
- Uses her wares to gain followers or influence among the refugees.
- A night hag who came to [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] drawn by its unrest and darkness.
- She acts as the dream-pastry dealer for [[NPCs/Franz|Franz]] in the Lost Soul.
- She is the mother of Bella and Offalia, two night hags.
- A peddler selling dream pastries in the refugee camp.
- Gave [[NPCs/Cyrus Belview|Cyrus Belview]] the *[[Items/Hag Eye|hag eye]]* for spying purposes.
- She appears during the players’ journey to [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
- Known for being watchful and mysterious.
- A night hag disguised as a peddler.

## DM Notes

- [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]]’s role in the larger narrative is central, as she holds knowledge about Strahd's pact, his escape plot, and the traitor within his household.
- She can be bribed with heartstone under certain conditions but has a tendency to demand harsh tasks or body parts as collateral for trust.
- She likely serves as an early indicator that more sinister players are beginning to enter the arena and affect plotlines.
- Her true form and motivations will likely become critical in later arcs dealing with hags or dark forces.
- Plays a minor but influential part in revealing threats beyond the immediate setting.
- Introduces an ongoing subplot with night hags and dream manipulation.
- Her actions lead into major arcs such as Arc H - The Lost Soul.
