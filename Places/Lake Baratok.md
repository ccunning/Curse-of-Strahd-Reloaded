# Lake Baratok

> **place** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Players discover evidence of [[NPCs/Rictavio|Rictavio]]'s presence there through tracks and paint stains.
- Where [[NPCs/Bianca Stoyanovich|Bianca]] was found sitting on the shore after stealing from [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]]'s wagon.
- Contains a tower with a hidden trapdoor where [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]]’s wagon is stored.
- Has a rocky causeway with footprints leading toward the sound of music.
- Has a strange set of lily pads stained with bright yellow paint.
- Located near the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Wood]], where [[Places/Werewolf Den|werewolf den]] is situated.
- Known for a magic wagon that belongs to Ezmerelda d'Avenir.
- Location between the [[Places/Luna River|Luna River Crossroads]] and [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
- Contains the tower of Van Richten located on its shore.
- A lake located in the northwestern region of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].

## DM Notes

- Central location for the story involving [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]]; will feature [[Places/Van Richten's Tower|Van Richten's tower]] with significant puzzles.
