# Ethereal Plane

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Contains dangers like wandering entities and hostile spirits that make it difficult for Stella to return.
- Required to access in order to perform rituals or rescue Stella from her current condition.
- Interacts with the Material Plane, enabling spiritual communication through mirrors.
- A metaphysical realm where Stella's soul remains trapped after being severed.
- Where night hags are active, particularly those linked to [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]].
