# Coven's Quarters

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- They are near the main part of the castle and connect to various access points.
- These rooms are used by witches who prepare their brews for Plague.
- [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla Vilisevic]] also makes her way through these quarters.
- The uppermost floor of the South Tower of the castle.
- House a group of six [[NPCs/Barovian witches|Barovian witches]].
