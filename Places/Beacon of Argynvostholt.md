# Beacon of Argynvostholt

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- It is where players encounter [[NPCs/Vladimir Horngaard|Vladimir Horngaard]], and contains information about [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]'s spirit and prophecies.
- The tower also includes a [[Places/Dragon Gargoyle|dragon gargoyle]] that can activate to give guidance through a prophecy.
- An observation tower located on the rooftop of the manor.

## DM Notes

- Inside the beacon are clues pointing toward how to defeat [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]], as well as key interactions with Vladimir.
