# Berez

> **place** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Contains ruins that were destroyed, reflecting the long span of time and events within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Important in establishing ties between the past and future generations in the narrative.
- Was involved in various magical developments due to its proximity to ancient fane.
- Later became a ruin and was claimed by [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]], who built her hut there.
- Connected to events related to the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] and [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s ongoing campaigns.
- Used as a strategic point during the early development of the valley.
- Location where a key interaction with [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s Enemy takes place.
- Includes the monument at which [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] confronts [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]].
- Known to be occupied by various magical creatures and spirits.
- Serves as a meeting place for witches and supernatural beings.
- A town located in the valley of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] near the [[Places/Swamp Fane|Swamp Fane]].
- Location of an ancient ruin where [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] led [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Fiona]].
- Connected to [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] and ancient prophecies.
- A location where Baba Lysaga resides.
