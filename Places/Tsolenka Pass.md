# Tsolenka Pass

> **place** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- Represents obstacles that prevent or delay travelers seeking knowledge or salvation.
- A mountain pass where the hidden settlement of the [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] resides, [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]].
- Is associated with lore related to the old gods and ancient Barovian history.
- Blocked by vrocks who prevented his progress and halted his advancement.
- A natural barrier that affects access to important supernatural locales.
- [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]], a settlement in these mountains, is undisclosed to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Demonstrates how geography can be manipulated by powerful forces.
- Area through which [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir]] attempted to reach [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Unknown to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] due to [[NPCs/Elder Burebis|Elder Burebis]]'s protection.
- Location players ascend to reach [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- The pass through which the [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] roam.
- Path along [[Places/Mount Ghakis|Mount Ghakis]].
