# Children’s Room

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Contains items that reflect their personalities and childhood memories, such as toys with initials carved in them.
- Contains clues about family relationships and possible connections to other cults within the manor.
- A room dedicated to the children of the household, particularly Rose and Thorn.
- Players may interact with ghosts of Rose and Thorn to receive guidance or aid.
- Involves the use of possession mechanics via magical items for players.

## DM Notes

- Interactions with the ghosts within the Children's Room may unlock other secrets related to children and their fates within the manor.
- This room might serve as a crucial hub or starting point in solving puzzles or finding keys necessary for escaping [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
