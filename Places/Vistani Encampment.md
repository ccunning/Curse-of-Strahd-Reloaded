# Vistani Encampment

> **place** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Places where players may interact or encounter [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] related to [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]]'s rescue.
- Connected to themes of identity, unity, and community within campaign.
- Provides key narrative framework for player relationships with locals.
- Spies play critical roles in spreading information back to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Used by [[NPCs/Arrigal|Arrigal]] and [[NPCs/Luvash|Luvash]] to seek their lost daughter [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]].
- [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir]] of the [[Factions/Dusk Elves|dusk elves]] is less of a concern for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Centralized home base for the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] tribe in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Home to important spies like [[NPCs/Arrigal|Arrigal]].

## DM Notes

- May provide crucial intel paths for players, or be manipulated against them by Strahd’s spies.
- Important in future storylines involving diplomacy and interaction with nonplayer factions.
