# Amber Temple

> **place** · 9 source files

## Key Facts

- Knowledge of the temple’s exact whereabouts is withheld by [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]], indicating that it is a crucial piece of information in a larger narrative puzzle.
- A hidden temple of forbidden lore said to be guarded by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd von Zarovich]] where he first met the entity with whom he forged his pact.
- It represents an artifact or relic significant to both [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s personal history and the overall cosmic forces affecting the campaign.
- [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Godfrey]] warns the players not to seek its power unless they prove themselves worthy through a successful mission at [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]].
- [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] mentions having no knowledge of this secret yet knows enough about its nature to be meaningful to the players involved.
- The Temple was part of the original purpose of the Order, which sought to protect the world from what it contains.
- Considered one of the most evil places within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] and is guarded against by the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]].
- A significant location whose exterior is described in X1. Temple Facade; reaching it marks a story milestone.
- Placed at the end of the game as a dangerous and isolated site to delay the players' access to key artifacts.
- Is described negatively due to its association with dark gifts, betrayal, and spiritual decay.
- A structure built by wizards who came after [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]] to contain [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]]'s vestige.
- A place of corruption located in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], tied directly to the [[Items/Amber Shards|amber shards]]' influence.
- An ancient structure built over two thousand years ago to contain and study vestiges.
- Said to contain the origins of magic, artifacts, and secrets tied to the [[Items/Amber Shard|Amber Shard]].
- Central location in prophecy connected to future salvation or downfall of Barovia.
- A temple in Barovia housing the amber sarcophagus from where the shard was taken.
- Its atmosphere is filled with a dark miasma that affects those who rest within.
- Destination for the players seeking to recover the broken hilt of the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]].
- Used to establish the basis for supernatural powers used by certain characters.
- Made of amber to insulate against magical effects and corrupting influences.
- Its defenses were ultimately overcome by corruption within its own ranks.
- Mystical center of origin believed linked to ancient and powerful beings.
- Defenses were constructed at [[Places/Tsolenka Pass|Tsolenka Pass]] to protect it from enemies.
- Housed magical artifacts and sarcophagi containing other fallen gods.
- Serves as a possible future location for exploration and discovery.
- Has a complex layout including various chambers, vaults, and traps.
- Sealed [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]]’s divine spark in amber near [[Places/Mount Ghakis|Mount Ghakis]].
- Desecrated by Strahd and key to understanding ancient lore.
- Place visited by many explorers, including [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir Velikov]].
- Players enter this during [[Arc S - A Sword of Sunlight]].
- Constructed by a secret society of good-aligned wizards.
- Contains valuable treasures and is respected by Strahd.
- Contains vestiges associated with amber relics.
- A temple with [[NPCs/Neferon|Neferon]] as a dedicated guardian.
- Has ancient artifacts related to divine magic.
- A crucial location for placing The [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]].

## DM Notes

- This temple is implied to be one of key landmarks connected to Strahd's pact and possibly central clues in locating other sacred elements of his domain.
- May appear as an ultimate goal or hidden threat in future arcs involving corruption and moral struggle.
- Given its nature, it’s likely tied to important revelations or climactic battles in future narratives.
- Its location within Mt. Ghakis makes it isolated but potentially surrounded by danger.
- The temple holds many secrets that players must uncover throughout their journey.
- Important to future lore around relic usage and pact possibilities.
- Arc V: The Sunsword.
