# Dragon's Vault

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Inside the vault resides several powerful items including the [[Items/Bracelet of Warding|bracelet of warding]], [[Items/Amulet of Knight’s Passage|amulet of knight’s passage]], [[Items/Moon-touched Rapier|moon-touched rapier]], and the banner.
- The name 'Dragon's Den' is also referenced in one of the locations leading to it.
- A hidden room within the manor used by [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]], containing magical artifacts.

## DM Notes

- These items serve important roles in helping players confront [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]. The amulet may help them navigate to [[Places/Tsolenka Pass|Tsolenka Pass]], and others aid them physically or magically.
- Players must go through a concealed compartment within the wall to access these treasures.
