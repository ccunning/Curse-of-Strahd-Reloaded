# Lake Zarovich

> **place** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Features prominently in the dream sequence as a place where [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] is approached by the Dark Power's mask, [[Factions/Ezra|Ezra]], who reveals information about the future peace achieved via sacrifice.
- Is referenced as an area with potential for conflict and mystery, connecting to the [[NPCs/Dreamer|Dreamer]]'s role in the narrative and her heart’s influence over [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s power.
- Has a significant location where the goddess of the [[Places/Mists|Mists]], [[Factions/Ezra|Ezra]], awaits in the form of a cloaked woman who closely resembles Stella Wachter.
- Appears familiar in her dreams and has strong emotional resonance, though not yet confirmed tied directly to past events or prophecies.
- Located in a foggy area near [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] and seems symbolic of deeper connections with the world’s mythos.
- A mysterious lake visited by [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] during her journey through [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Threatens [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] due to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s actions in [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|the Heart of Sorrow]].
- Location of [[Places/Whispering Wall|the Whispering Wall]] during Act V.

## DM Notes

- The setting is essential to the dream vision and contains symbolic elements—like the lake’s frozen surface—that link directly to [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]'s deeper mythologies.
- The location where the choice about Ireena’s soul is made implies that its waters are powerful enough to manipulate fate or destiny.
- Could play a major role in uncovering secrets about Barovia's history or Strahd's plans.
- Possibly linked to prophetic visions that will grow clearer as the campaign develops.
