# Mists

> **place** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- The [[Places/Mists|mists]] are described as dark, endless, and filled with mystery, often obscuring the path and hindering progress.
- These [[Places/Mists|mists]] surround the area around [[Factions/Death House|Death House]] and are a key part of setting the atmosphere in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Created by [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]] upon [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s return to [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] as a vampiric domain.
- Key to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s escape and return to the material plane during the [[Places/Grand Conjunction|Grand Conjunction]].
- Created by [[Factions/Dark Powers|Dark Powers]], requiring specific conditions and sacrifices to breach.
- Dark, impassable walls that encased the valley after Strahd's transformation.
- Isolated [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] from the outside world and allowed its dark magic to thrive.
- Actively used as an obstacle in later adventures involving outsiders.
- Used to trap those who entered, preventing escape or rescue.
- The boundary between worlds that imprison Strahd.

## DM Notes

- The presence of the Mists is linked to the overarching magical nature and danger of the realm of Ravenloft.
- Will serve as the central thematic element of struggle against Strahd’s ultimate escape.
