# P10. Garrison

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- An area in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] that was once reserved for the castle guards, featuring multiple subareas like Basement Floor and First Floor.
- Players encounter it after exploring passages leading from the dining hall.
- Holds numerous zombies, which frighten [[NPCs/Cyrus Belview|Cyrus]] from entering it himself.
- The garrison is a place reserved for the castle’s armed forces.
- It includes archer posts and secret doors to other areas.
- Area connected to the servants' wing via [[Places/Hall of Bones|Hall of Bones]].
