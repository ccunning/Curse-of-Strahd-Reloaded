# Van Richten's Tower

> **place** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Players uncover clues within the tower leading to [[NPCs/Arabelle|Arabelle]]'s kidnapping through burnt journal entries and other items.
- Features gargoyle statues that come alive when the door is improperly opened or someone climbs [[Places/Scaffolding|scaffolding]].
- Has a complex door mechanism involving eight magic runes which must be pressed in order to open.
- Used for magical study and research, later became a significant site during the lichdom process.
- Inhabited by [[NPCs/Khazan|Khazan]]’s descendants and served as an important base of operation.
- Later destroyed during [[NPCs/Khazan|Khazan]]’s failed attempt to become a demilich.
- Connected to future events involving [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s plans and the [[Places/Mists|Mists]].
- Contains a permanent antimagic field that blocks scrying.
- A magical structure built by Khazan in [[Places/Lake Baratok|Lake Baratok]].
- A tower located on [[Places/Lake Baratok|Lake Baratok]], built by Khazan.

## DM Notes

- Serves as Van Richten’s hidden base during the arc; connects to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s knowledge about it.
