# Audience Hall

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Area of the castle accessed from the elevator trap that connects to [[Places/King's Hall|King's Hall]].
- Located on the second floor of the castle where [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] would hold audiences.
- It connects to other corridors and leads players toward the [[Places/King's Hall|King's Hall]].
- Contains a secret door behind the throne which accesses the garrison.
- Previously connected to the King’s Hall via the elevator.
