# Dungeons

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Contains subareas such as Dungeon Hall, North Dungeon, South Dungeon and [[Places/Torture Chamber|Torture Chamber]].
- A dark area of the castle accessed from the King's Apartments and various staircases.
- These are located far below the castle and house prisoners like [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil]].
- They hold [[Items/Silverthorn Collar|the silverthorn collar]] used by [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta Popofsky]].
- Home to various torture devices and imprisoned characters.
- Connected to the South Tower Stair in a complex layout.
- [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]] guards them with her zombies.
