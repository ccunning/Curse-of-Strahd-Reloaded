# The Mass Grave

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Its purpose ties deeply into the past lives of individuals within [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]], particularly in relation to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s destruction of the order.
- Contains remains of nearly 1000 souls who died in defense of the mansion, including those possibly associated with the Order.
- Used to protect players from hostile spirits within [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] by allowing them to appear as members of the Order.
- Nearby area is home to ghouls that threaten those digging without proper preparations or intentions.
- A burial site north of [[Places/Berez|Berez]] where the players can find garments bearing the insignia of the Order.
- Players may encounter [[NPCs/Wensencia|Wensencia]] here, attempting to harvest an elk for her alchemical research.
- A location mentioned in the context of a massacre during the war.

## DM Notes

- Is required to obtain disguise gear; will be relevant in future encounters and possibly linked with other narrative threads.
