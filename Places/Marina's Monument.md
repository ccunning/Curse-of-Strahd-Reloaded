# Marina's Monument

> **place** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- References suggest that Marina’s presence was historically tied to changes in land and magical events.
- It lies in the area marked by the [[Places/Swamp Fane|Swamp Fane]] and was part of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s punishment for her death.
- Located within [[Places/Berez|Berez]] ruins near the swamp, marking the site of a former monument to Marina.
- The monument is associated with [[NPCs/King Dostron|King Dostron]] and the damming of [[Places/Luna River|Luna River]] tributary.
- Players might explore this location if they travel around ruins beyond the main path.
- Part of the narrative for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s Enemy’s role in the [[Items/Tarokka reading|Tarokka reading]].
- Location where [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]] confronts [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]].

## DM Notes

- Marina's role connects to larger themes of past history, betrayal, and Strahd's control over [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Arc X: Whispers of the [[Places/Mists|Mists]].
