# Svalich Woods

> **place** · 8 source files

## Key Facts

- A location where [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s spies encounter either a wolf or a druid during the day, or swarms of bats at night.
- A dense woodland through which the journey from [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] to [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] takes place.
- The woods near the clearing resemble the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Woods]] where the [[NPCs/Durst children|Durst children]] once lived.
- However, despite their similarity, the players find themselves in unfamiliar territory.
- Home to the [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]], where the players will encounter various [[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]].
- Has a quiet hollow used by [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]] to stake out the den before infiltration.
- A dense forest located near [[Places/Lake Baratok|Lake Baratok]], where the [[Places/Werewolf Den|werewolf den]] is located.
- The dense forest that players can traverse to avoid the eastern undead army.
- Connected to old ruins, ancient artifacts, and mystical activity in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Home to many dangerous creatures and used by various [[Factions/Non-Player Characters|non-player characters]].
- Contains a natural depression with a cave-like root structure beneath it.
- The site where both the initial attack and the missing search took place.
- Important location in the lives of those who sought refuge from conflict.
- Known for its dark canopy and misty environment ideal for hidden camps.
- Placed within the larger valley and affected by the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] of isolation.
- A part of the campaign’s lore, connecting to werewolf lore in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] fled there after his lycanthropy erupted and killed his sister.
- Features a path that turns into the undergrowth near [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]]'s cairn.
- Used for establishing dangerous environment near town boundaries.
- Players encounter [[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]] who track them through the woods.
- [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda d'Avenir]] has been searching for [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] in these woods.
- Provides cover and setting for stealthy or hidden activities.
- It is a dangerous forest that borders the [[Places/Village of Krezk|village of Krezk]].
- Forest area adjacent to the [[Places/Old Svalich Road|Old Svalich Road]] in Barovia.
- Woods where [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] dwelled and used to be located.
- Contains a pack of dire [[NPCs/Wolves|wolves]] encountered by players.
- Used for tracking player movement through espionage.
- Known as the domain of dark magical energies.
- Where [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] dwells according to [[NPCs/Muriel Vinshaw|Muriel]].

## DM Notes

- Mentioned as a location relevant to the setting's lore and past events within Barovia.
- May become more central in future campaigns as areas of exploration involving druids.
