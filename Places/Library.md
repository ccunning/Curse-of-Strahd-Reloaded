# Library

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- An ancient journal or note hidden in the [[Places/Library|library]] reveals information about the household's internal conflicts.
- A room filled with books that features a secret door which can be unlocked using an [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]].
- Contains important correspondence from [[NPCs/Klara|Klara]], who was involved in the household.
- The secret door leads to the [[Places/Secret Room|Secret Room]] where players find letters and items.
- This area is linked to another [[Places/Secret Room|secret room]] in the manor via musical clues.

## DM Notes

- The [[Places/Library|Library]] holds crucial knowledge relating to both [[NPCs/Klara|Klara]] and possibly more historical events tied to the house.
- Accessing its secret passages could unlock parts of the narrative or lead to encounters involving cult rituals.
