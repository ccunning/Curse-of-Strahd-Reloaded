# A gloomy, fog-enshrouded village/valley located within the Mists.

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- It is the site of several notable locations like the Blood of the Vine Tavern and the local church.
- [[Places/Village of Barovia|The village of Barovia]] has been ravaged by death and despair following [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s revival.
- It is a valley surrounded by thick clouds of fog, mountains, and evergreen trees.
- The village is susceptible to attacks from Strahle's undead horde each night.

## DM Notes

- The [[Places/Mists|Mists]] prevent compasses from behaving normally near the edge of the valley (causing needles to spin wildly).
- Passing through the fog/[[Places/Mists|Mists]] can cause exhaustion, sapping life force and energy.
- The Mists surround and entrap [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], acting as a barrier that prevents escape.
