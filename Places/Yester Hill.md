# Yester Hill

> **place** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- A terraced mound in the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Woods]] where the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] have set up their ritual to summon [[NPCs/Wintersplinter|Wintersplinter]].
- Known for its maddened worship and as an area where [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] can use the Wailing Wall for ritual sacrifice.
- Home to the druids and likely where their evil ritual is currently being prepared.
- Location where the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] are based, noted to be both distasteful and useful.
- Associated with both [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]]’s corpse and the beginning of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]’s history.
- Home base for the druids and also sanctuary of [[Items/Gulthias tree|Gulthias tree]] that grows atop it.
- Previously featured in the game, now linked with another encounter scene.
- Has a [[Items/Gulthias tree|Gulthias Tree]] atop it, whose cuttings were planted in the winery.
- Its [[Places/Whispering Wall|Whispering Wall]] plays a crucial role in [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s escape strategy.
- Has a Gulthias Tree at its peak, which is central to the ritual.
- Said to be a place of ancient magic linked to the valley itself.
- Connected to the mist that later enveloped the land of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Named after its appearance and historical significance in lore.
- The site of a planned summoning involving the [[NPCs/Tree Blight|Tree Blight]].
- Home to the Forest Folk druids and their activities.
- Connected to the winery in Act J – The Stolen Gem.
- Contains a stone circle formed by black boulders.
- A hilltop where the druids conduct their ritual.
- A hill in Barovia near which the druids reside.
- Location where the players meet Strahd again.
- Location of druids planning a dark ritual.
- A hill where [[Places/Whispering Wall|the Whispering Wall]] towers.

## DM Notes

- The Whispering Wall is key to the ritual needed to break free from the [[Places/Mists|Mists]]; likely part of a climactic battle setup.
- [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] is central location for climactic confrontation between players and Forest Folk druids.
- This is where the gemstone must be retrieved from [[NPCs/Wintersplinter|Wintersplinter]]’s heart.
