# The Shroud of Mist

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Causes creatures who come into contact with it for the first time on a turn to take necrotic damage and be slowed.
- It is central to protecting [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]] from outsiders, including potential enemies.
- Connected with illusions and dreams in a hidden lore aspect involving [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- A foggy area mentioned as a possible path related to [[Places/Whispering Wall|the Whispering Wall]].
- Connected with events involving the magical environment and concealment.
- Only the elders of the [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] can conjure and maintain this mist.
- Used as a barrier during the sequence of combat involving [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]].
- Has a 60-foot radius and lasts for 10 minutes or until dismissed.
- An enchanted mist used to conceal the hidden village of [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]].
- Prevents enemies from finding it or remembering its location.
- A mysterious place or area that relates to the overall plot.
- A spell effect used by [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]] to block the players.

## DM Notes

- Maintaining or removing the shroud could have consequences in future encounters.
- The mist's protection may be a test for characters trying to enter Soldav.
