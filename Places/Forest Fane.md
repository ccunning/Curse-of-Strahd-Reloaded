# Forest Fane

> **place** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Had its bond with [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] broken during the rise of new threats, making it crucial for events following his awakening.
- Serves as a landmark in understanding how divine energy and supernatural forces operate within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Hosts connection to sacred elements like the *[[Items/Sigil of the Sun|Sigil of the Sun]]* and *[[Items/Holy Symbol of Ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]]*.
- A shrine to the [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]] of the Ladies Three located in the grove of [[Items/Gulthias tree|the Gulthias Tree]].
- Demarcated by carved megaliths symbolizing various animals, including raven.
- Site of significant battle between [[NPCs/Lugdana|Lugdana]] and the [[Factions/Cult of Chernovog|Cult of Chernovog]].
- A sacred location in the woods of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] dedicated to the [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]].
- Was central to the summoning ritual involving [[NPCs/Chernovog|Chernovog]].
- Connected directly to the wolf’s tooth and its journey.
- Players will reach it via [[Arc T - The Three Fanes]].
- Silently waiting to be restored.

## DM Notes

- Its significance suggests further role in restoring sacred connections or breaking barriers within the story.
- Represents a significant mythic site for the Hunters’ lore.
