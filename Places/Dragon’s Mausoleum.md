# Dragon’s Mausoleum

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- One of several possible areas for obtaining [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]'s remains when players journey to [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Connected to [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]’s spiritual influence and serves as a focal point where his whisperings are heard.
- A tomb beneath the mansion where Argynvost is buried, now desecrated and under magical scrutiny.
- Contains twin unlit torches resting in rusted sconces alongside the verse.
- Located within the cemetery that is part of the mansion grounds.

## DM Notes

- Will serve as the source area when trying to retrieve Argynvost’s skull for restoration; may be connected with events leading to the final confrontation.
