# Village of Barovia

> **place** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Location where [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s spies are encountered during the day as a [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] bandit or at night as swarms of bats.
- Home to key characters such as [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]] and her father [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark Kolyanovich]].
- Known for being under [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s rule but not a major focus area.
- Strahd considers the soul of [[NPCs/Tatyana Federovna|Tatyana]] residing within [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]].
- One of the locations used in Strahd's espionage tracking.

## DM Notes

- The village's spiritual and emotional core ties directly into Strahd’s character arc, involving Ireena’s destiny.
