# Old Svalich Road

> **place** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Players investigate its surroundings for connections to mysterious events such as the signet ring or paint stains.
- Also connected to the story of Van Richten's handkerchief being found near [[Places/Luna River|Luna River]] for foreshadowing.
- Players find themselves at the start of this road in a dark forest clearing outside of [[Places/Daggerford|Daggerford]].
- It connects directly to areas within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] and begins the journey deeper into the setting.
- The road leads west from the clearing where players emerge after exiting [[Factions/Death House|Death House]].
- A path leading from [[Places/Lake Baratok|Lake Baratok]] to [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]], used by players traveling back.
- Used as alternate route with significant encounters involving [[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]].
- Used as pathway for both returning and continuing exploration.

## DM Notes

- The path leads into the heart of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] and sets up exploration of Barovia’s larger world.
- Will connect to future paths leading to key areas like [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Woods]] or [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
