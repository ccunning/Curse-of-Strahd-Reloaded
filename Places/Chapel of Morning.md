# Chapel of Morning

> **place** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- A sacred space within the manor associated with morning prayers and religious significance.
- It connects to multiple areas including the [[Places/Chapel of Morning|Chapel Balcony]] which overlooks the nave.
- A location accessible via the elevator trap for access to the King’s Hall.
- Players can find themselves near the altar where [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]] hides in fog.
- Plays a key role in depicting [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]]'s anointment by [[NPCs/Ithuriel|Ithuriel]].
- Connected to the [[Places/King's Hall|King's Hall]] which is linked to the [[Places/Audience Hall|Audience Hall]].
- The [[Places/Chapel of Morning|chapel]] houses a sacred site used by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and his court.
