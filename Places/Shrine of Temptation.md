# Shrine of Temptation

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Contains a special *[[Items/Staff of Frost|staff of frost]]* which is a cursed item meant to manipulate players through temptation.
- It is one of the locations where people must go to gain access to [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]]'s secrets or tools.
- An area within [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] where players encounter dark influences and potential treasures.
- Connected specifically to Vilnius’s schemes and his desire to possess more tools in the temple.
- The shrine reflects the influence of [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]] trying to lure characters toward power.
- Plays a central part in guiding player choices that involve dark gains.
- It is the shrine in which Vilnius's master [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]]’s staff lies.
- Vilnius intends to retrieve it as a means of escape.

## DM Notes

- Access to the shrine will be key for players trying to free Vilnius or learn about the Amber Temple’s history.
- The shrine’s item and narrative suggest further involvement with [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]] and moral dilemmas for PCs.
