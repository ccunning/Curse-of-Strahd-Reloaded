# Cult Headquarters

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Has thousands of scratch marks and blood-stained carvings on walls including 'Traitor' and 'In Silence, Death'.
- A chamber with a black pentagram on the floor, large wooden chairs at each point of the pentagram.
- Carved by [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]] using his vampiric claws, indicating his imprisonment beneath [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]].
- Contains a three-foot-high carving of a dagger, representing the [[Factions/Ba’al Verzi|Ba’al Verzi]] guild.
- The location where the etherealness and restoration rituals are performed.

## DM Notes

- Serves as both a setting for the restoration ritual and the climax in a battle with Leo's ghost.
- Connected to [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]]'s background through historical carvings and the [[Places/Leyline beneath Wachterhaus|leyline]] beneath it.
