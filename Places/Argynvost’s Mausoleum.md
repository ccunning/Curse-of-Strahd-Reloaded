# Argynvost’s Mausoleum

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- It is where players can seek permission from the dead to communicate with [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]] using heartfelt speeches.
- Players gain tokens of favor based on the sincerity of their words to receive guidance.
- A burial place for [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]] located in the cemetery beneath the manor.

## DM Notes

- The mausoleum plays a pivotal role in initiating player engagement with Argynvost’s spirit, especially after the fight with Vladimir has taken place.
- It serves as a path for players to get closer to achieving victory over [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
