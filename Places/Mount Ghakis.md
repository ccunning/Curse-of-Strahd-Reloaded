# Mount Ghakis

> **place** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- A location where encounters with a dire wolf or berserker can be found during the day, or swarms of bats at night.
- Linked to other mystical forces involved in transporting individuals or events across dimensions.
- Located near [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]], where [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]]’s divine spark was sealed in amber.
- Connected to the origin of magical items related to travel through [[Places/Mists|mists]] and portals.
- A mountain featuring a hidden trail that leads from the plateau to [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Used as a portal to [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], indicating mystical connections beyond normal space.
- A mountain with ancient power that housed a sacred shrine and magical artifacts.
- Hosts [[Places/The Mountain Fane|the Mountain Fane]], resting place of the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]] in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]’s landscape.
- The site of various magical events related to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s later life and plans.
- Used as a location where the vampire’s bond with the land was established.
- Played a central role in the history of the Amber Temple and its dangers.
- Site where the *[[Items/Mist Talisman|mist talisman]]* was obtained by Rudolph van Richten.
- Home to a [[NPCs/Roc|roc]] who served the fey goddess known as the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]].
- Hosts a hidden cave that acted as an entrance to the valley.
- Used for tracking players’ movements and interactions.
- Symbolizes connection to fey powers and protection.
- Mountain along which players traverse in Act IV.
- Home to various trials including a [[NPCs/Roc|roc]].

## DM Notes

- The importance of magical items and portals points toward possible explorations involving dimensional travel in the future.
- Players will journey to its fane in [[Arc T - The Three Fanes]].
