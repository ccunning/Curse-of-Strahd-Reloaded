# Servants’ Wing

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Area of the castle including servants' entrance, quarters, elevator shaft, hall, [[Places/Wine Cellar|wine cellar]] and [[Places/Kitchen|kitchen]].
- This section requires players to navigate without alerting the [[Factions/Wights|wights]] patrolling the castle.
- It includes a servants' entrance, an elevator trap, and various storeroom-like chambers.
- Connected to other castle areas like the Garrison via secret doors.
- The servant's wing area has multiple rooms and access areas.
