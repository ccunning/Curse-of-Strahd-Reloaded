# The Vampyr’s Sarcophagus

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Part of an intricate lore involving vestiges, magic, lichdoms, and spiritual binding.
- [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina Velikovna]] used this location as a place of great significance for her plans.
- The area includes a canal of blood and souls representing [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s fear of death.
- It is linked to a secret demiplane where the hilt of the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]] was hidden.
- Located within the [[Places/Amber Vault|Amber Vault]] area near key figures like [[NPCs/Patrina Velikovna|Patrina]] and [[NPCs/Khazan|Khazan]].
- Its power may be directly tied to that of the vampire in [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Serves as a thematic location tied to darker powers from [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s history.
- Contains the remains of a powerful entity that influenced Strahd's birth.
- A sarcophagus in [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] connected to the vampiric presence.

## DM Notes

- Connected to the history of the vampire lord, especially the corruption and containment efforts throughout [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- The sarcophagus will play a significant role in the final confrontation involving the [[Items/Sunsword|Sunsword]].
- Players might encounter Patrina or other spiritual forces related to this artifact.
