# Village of Krezk

> **place** · 6 source files

## Key Facts

- The players enter here after completing [[Arc I - The Walls of Krezk]] or if they have completed [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]].
- Players can seek entry to Krezk by retrieving information about [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] through [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]]'s scrying spell.
- Symbolic representation of the increasing involvement or control exercised by the divine [[Factions/Dark Powers|dark powers]].
- Once entry is gained, players may access [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|the Abbey of Saint Markovia]] for a cure for cackle fever.
- He intends to play along temporarily with the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]'s creation of a [[Items/Flesh Golem|flesh golem]] bride.
- Affected by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s awakening and connected to early signs of supernatural influence.
- The players are invited to stay here with the family if they've aided the Martikovs.
- Symbolized early interaction with outsiders and the growing settlement of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- A location where [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] seeks to break local spirits and enforce his authority.
- The site of a monastery known for its dark experiments and mystical endeavors.
- Krezk is a suspicious village that won't allow outsiders in without a task.
- Players encounter swarms of rats during the day or swarms of bats at night.
- It has suffered from a werewolf attack that killed [[NPCs/Kala Krezkova|Kala]] and injured [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]].
- Baron Krezkov is the leader who protects and conceals the family's curse.
- A settlement near the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]] of [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]], founded by Vasha Krezkova.
- Location where the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] resides and conducts his grave-robbing research.
- It is a small quiet village in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] where the Krezkov family resides.
- Home to early developments in [[Items/Flesh Golem|flesh golem]] creation and magical projects.
- Used in the [[Items/Tarokka reading|Tarokka reading]] to reveal clues about Strahd's Enemy.
- Became part of the community surrounding Strahd's rise to power.
- Connected to both the abbey and the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]].
- Baron Krezkov has a son named Ilya Krezkov who went missing.
- Built around a trading post that grew into a small village.
- Town where the Abbot of St. [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Markovia]] is found to be mad.
- The burgomaster of Krezk is [[NPCs/Baron Dmitri Krezkov|Baron Dmitri Krezkov]].
- Known for its mad Abbot who is amused by Strahd.
- Location of recruiting [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda d'Avenir]].

## DM Notes

- The transformation of Krezk reflects broader patterns in how religious centers are shaped by supernatural agendas.
- It could involve the creation of supernatural forces that will oppose players in later arcs.
