# The Mountain Fane

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Demarcates the boundary for an area associated with the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]] of the Ladies Three shrine mentioned in Arc T - [[Places/The Three Fanes|The Three Fanes]].
- The area is heavily obscured by fog, and entering it results in a confrontation with four vampiric [[Places/Mists|mists]] that attack the party.
- Players can see the megaliths from [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]]’s hilltop after approaching to the edge of the valley.
- Symbolizes a threshold for accessing other regions tied to divine powers or ancient shrines.
- Located at the edge of the [[Places/Mists|Mists]], marked by a circle of megaliths in constant fog.

## DM Notes

- The presence of [[Places/The Mountain Fane|the Mountain Fane]] suggests links between [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]], [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s domain, and wider mythos involving the three ladies and their shrines—especially since it involves a sacred site near the hilltop windmill.
