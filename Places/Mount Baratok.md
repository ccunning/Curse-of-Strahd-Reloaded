# Mount Baratok

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- It plays a major role in [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]]'s attempt to save [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] from [[NPCs/Wolves|wolves]] and during Arc L - The Den of [[NPCs/Wolves|Wolves]].
- [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya Krezkov]]'s location was found in a cavern at this mountain’s northern shore, near the lake.
- The den of the [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]] is located here, using the cave as a base within its vicinity.
- [[Places/Mount Baratok|Mount Baratok]] is considered dangerous terrain due to its proximity to supernatural horrors.
- A mountain range near the shores of [[Places/Lake Baratok|Lake Baratok]], where werewolf activities occur.
