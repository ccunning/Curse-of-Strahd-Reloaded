# Leyline beneath Wachterhaus

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- A natural magical source located beneath [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]] which [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]] identifies for the ritual.
- Used in placing players into the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]] as part of the restoration process.
- Connected to the cultural and supernatural history of [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]].
- Provides power needed to sustain the *etherealness* ritual.
- Found by [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]] using his magical knowledge and resources.

## DM Notes

- Foreshadows connections between various [[Places/Leyline beneath Wachterhaus|leyline]] sources in the greater setting.
- Establishes magical significance of Wachterhaus beyond its immediate location.
