# Whispering Wall

> **place** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- Appears throughout [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], forming a massive wall of mist that stretches from [[Places/Mount Baratok|Mount Baratok]] to [[Places/Mount Ghakis|Mount Ghakis]].
- Is described as a living, moving entity that seeks to shelter [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]]’s spirit and advances eastward.
- A mysterious structure located west of [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] where [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] offers a reward for those who defeat him.
- An entity raised by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s power from [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]]'s vestige in the depths of [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|the Heart of Sorrow]].
- Rose into the skies by [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] and was associated with [[Places/Whispering Wall|the Whispering Wall]] near [[Places/Mount Ghakis|Mount Ghakis]].
- Consumes the land and people within its path, pulling them into an endless loop of nightmares.
- Dangerous force that threatens [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] after [[Items/Heart of Sorrow|the Heart of Sorrow]] is released.
- A [[Places/The Shroud of Mist|shroud of mist]] formed from the corpse of the Devourer after her death.
- Lent power to those who entered its [[Places/Mists|mists]]—both good and evil.
- Located beside which the druids of the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] dwell.
- Used by [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] as part of his ambition to claim greatness.
- Contains misted depths with visions of deepest desires.
- Served as a forbidden place in the valley's history.
- Located in [[Places/Lake Zarovich|Lake Zarovich]] and causes destruction.
- A mysterious wall near Yester Hill.

## DM Notes

- The mention of it being animated by Strahd’s power suggests that overcoming both the Wall and Strahd will be necessary to fully liberate [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Its movement across Barovia and consumption of settlements indicates increasing danger for all lands beyond [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]].
