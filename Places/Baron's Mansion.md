# Baron's Mansion

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]]'s bedroom is located here, from which he fights the players during rescue attempts.
- Contains a secret staircase where [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] might be hidden during [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek]]’s attacks.
- Home to various servants like [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]], Tereska, Clavdia, and others.
- The seat of power in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]], where Baron Vallakovich resides.
- Where Izek keeps [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]] after kidnapping her.

## DM Notes

- Its fire-based damage during combat foreshadows the Festival of the Blazing Sun events.
- The mansion is central to both the kidnapping and rescue arcs of the story.
