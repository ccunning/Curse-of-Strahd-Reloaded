# Swamp Fane

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Each honored one of the four goddesses: the [[Places/Swamp Fane|Swamp Fane]] for the [[NPCs/Dreamer|Dreamer]] (in part), the [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane]] for the [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]], and [[Places/The Mountain Fane|the Mountain Fane]] for the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]].
- These were central to early rituals and later used by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] in binding himself to the land.
- The sites became key to the creation of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s magical bond with [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Three sacred shrines built by [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]] to honor the Ladies Three.
- Located at places of natural power within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
