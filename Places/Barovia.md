# Barovia

> **place** · 7 source files

## Key Facts

- The heart of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s domain with historical influences tied back to early events including the Fall of [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]].
- It serves as both his domain of control and his means of escape in a ritual involving the Fanes.
- Became isolated under the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] after [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s transformation, trapping him and its inhabitants.
- The villagers were forced to flee, mostly settling in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] or remaining dead in the ruins.
- The land is deeply connected to the Ladies Three and was later corrupted by [[NPCs/The Devourer|the Devourer]].
- [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] is the land where the adventure takes place, a gothic realm governed by Strahd.
- Is a land in the Ravenloft setting, known for its dark atmosphere and tragic storyline.
- Spells cast within it are subject to cosmetic modifications and can behave differently.
- It was attacked by a mob led by [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]] in response to Dr. Rudolph van Richten’s presence.
- Many villagers blame [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark]] for inviting Strahd's wrath through the failed mob assault.
- Was originally inhabited by [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]], a nomadic group who worshipped the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]].
- It is entrapped by the [[Places/Mists|mists of Ravenloft]] and governed by mysterious [[Factions/Dark Powers|Dark Powers]].
- Once a peaceful and resilient village, it was destroyed by Strahd's undead army.
- The geography includes settlements such as [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]], Krezk, and [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]].
- Has been home to various conflicts involving supernatural and human factions.
- Named by Strahd after King Barov I, the founder of the kingdom of Zarovia.
- It is a demiplane, one of the Domains of Dread located in the Shadowfell.
- Known for its valleys, villages, and mystical atmosphere under the Mists.
- Contains notable settlements such as Barovia, Vallaki, Krezk, and [[Places/Berez|Berez]].
- The setting in which the campaign takes place, a gothic horror domain.
- Contains complex relationships and deep history of NPCs and cultures.
- A land in which the narrative is set; home of [[Places/Village of Barovia|the village of Barovia]].
- The gothic horror campaign setting for the [[Items/Curse of Strahd|Curse of Strahd]] adventure.
- A fertile valley that was the original homeland of the First Folk.
- The region has been corrupted and changed since Strahd's slumber.
- It has no sun for centuries and is surrounded by a deadly fog.
- [[NPCs/Ismark Kolyanovich|Ismark]] and [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]] took leadership for rebuilding.
- Plagued by the vampire Strahd von Zarovich.
- A cursed and dark land dominated by Strahd.

## DM Notes

- Its central location and curse drive almost all narrative events involving supernatural themes and conflict.
- It will be central to all major plot events, including his escape attempts and his reclamation strategies.
- The ruined village is symbolic of the larger tragedy of Strahd's rule and offers opportunities for quests.
- It is the primary setting of the campaign with a strong Gothic ambiance.
