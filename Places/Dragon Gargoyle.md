# Dragon Gargoyle

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- The gargoyle in the [[Places/Beacon of Argynvostholt|Beacon of Argynvostholt]] is mentioned as being capable of animating to give prophecy.
- A stone dragon figure atop the manor, often depicted with a burning flame or magical presence.

## DM Notes

- It contains crucial narrative elements when players need to communicate with [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]].
- It holds the prophecy that players must obtain for future use against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
