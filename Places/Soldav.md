# Soldav

> **place** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- Located in [[Places/Tsolenka Pass|Tsolenka Pass]], referenced by [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] as a place to seek reversal of land corruption.
- A location from which [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir]] can guide the players on a path toward [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Protected by a shroud of enchanted mist that conceals its location from outsiders.
- It is isolated and protected by natural and magical barriers from [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s spies.
- Its residents worship the Ladies Three, remembering old customs and rituals.
- A hidden settlement of the [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] located on [[Places/Tsolenka Pass|Tsolenka Pass]] slopes.
- It provides lore about ancient Barovian history and resistance to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Marked by two waterfalls running together as entrance landmark.
- A sacred site of the druids, located in the [[Places/Balinok Mountains|Balinok Mountains]].
- The elders there can provide guidance to redeem past mistakes.
- Site where [[Items/Amber Shards|amber shards]] from other entities can be recognized.
- Location visited to obtain information about [[Places/The Three Fanes|the three Fanes]].
- It serves as a refuge for those who reject Strahd’s rule.
- Center of faith for the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]] and the [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]].
- A key location in [[Arc R - Trials of the Mountain]].
- Home of [[NPCs/Elder Burebis|Elder Burebis]] and the Shrine of the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]].
- Settlement where Mountain Folk reside.

## DM Notes

- It plays a key role in revealing the history of the valley and connecting with the [[Factions/Ladies of the Fanes|Ladies of the Fanes]].
- Players visit this to encounter ancient artifacts like the [[Items/Stone Crest|stone crest]] and learn about the Rozana.
- Finding [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]] is crucial to restoring the spear and completing redemption arcs.
- The mist shroud may require specific tokens or knowledge to bypass.
