# Argynvostholt

> **place** · 7 source files

## Key Facts

- Haunted by various revenants, spirits, and hostile incorporeal warriors, including those who died defending it during the war with [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- The main setting for the events described in this arc, where players must seek out clues left by [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]’s spirit.
- The location where the spirit of [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]], a silver dragon, is said to have awakened after years of dormancy.
- A destination referred to by the [[NPCs/Revenant|revenant]] as a place for those opposing darkness from [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- A ruined mansion in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], formerly the domain of Lord Argynvost and the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]].
- The site of an ancient battle fought between the [[Factions/Order of the Silver Dragon|Order of the Silver Dragon]] and the forces of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Linked directly to the exploration of historical mysteries involving artifacts and knowledge.
- After its destruction, became a haunting site and served as grounds for future conflicts.
- Served as a source of clues or remnants left from earlier times including an ancient map.
- Home of Argynvost, an adult silver dragon and founder of the Order of the Silver Dragon.
- The location of the battle between Strahd and Argynvost that ultimately destroyed it.
- Strahd finds the order disinterested but harmless, largely ignoring them over time.
- A beacon location whose light players must relight to influence Strahd’s behavior.
- A home and headquarters built by Argynvost for the Order of the Silver Dragon.
- Empty manor visited by [[NPCs/Kasimir Velikov|Kasimir Velikov]] during his search for [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- Its beacon was thought to be relit when it actually reappeared during Arc R.
- Connected with the reward of being considered for service in Strahd's court.
- Hosted crucial discussions among knights regarding how to deal with Strahd.
- Connected to [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Sir Godfrey Gwilym]] and played a part in early investigations.
- Used as a meeting place before being attacked by Strahd’s forces.
- The seat of the Order of the Silver Dragon, ruled by revenants.
- Contains the spirit manifestations tied to the dragon scale.
- Hosted important relics, notably the location of a lost map.
- Player enters here in [[Arc M - The Dragon's Manor]].
- Located southeast of the Raven River Crossroads.
- Contains a dragon’s prophecy from Argynvost.
- Haunted mansion mentioned within Act III.

## DM Notes

- The [[Places/Beacon of Argynvostholt|beacon of Argynvostholt]] will be a direct threat in Arc R and serves as a symbolic key for Strahd's power retrieval.
- May offer opportunities for players to resolve his legacy after retrieving his skull in [[Arc Q - A Shining Beacon]].
- Serves as the central location for retrieving Argynvost's remains from [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- It will likely return in future scenes tied to clues, maps, or historical revelations.
