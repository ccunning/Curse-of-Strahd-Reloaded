# Wachterhaus

> **place** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- The manor house of the Wachter family in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]], used as a gathering point for the war council meetings and later during the siege.
- The house has a distinctive façade with thick gardens, herbs and vines, slouching roof, moss-covered walls, and red-painted door.
- Is where [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]] conducts her meetings with representatives from [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] regarding actions against [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Protected by a magical circle erected centuries ago to keep out ethereal creatures like [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]]'s gallows speaker spirit.
- Is described as being used in the dream world, particularly the dining area and [[Places/Parlor|parlor]], which are present in the future.
- The room underneath the cellar contains carved carvings including 'In Silence, Death' and images of [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]].
- Contains a complex history related to past murders and family trauma rooted in historical conflicts with [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- It has undergone changes, including the removal of Nikolai Wachter's corpse and adjustments to room layouts.
- An important hub where binding rituals involving night hags can occur after Stella is located or rescued.
- [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]] is the estate of [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]] located north of Vallaki along the northern road.
- Important rooms include Stella’s Bedroom, Storage Room, Master Bedroom, and various hallways.
- Contains protective circles around it to control passage into and out of the [[Places/Ethereal Plane|Ethereal Plane]].
- The ancestral estate of the Wachter family in Vallaki, now owned by Lady Fiona Wachter.
- A significant site for Lady Wachter’s involvement in helping [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]] restore Stella.
- Built on top of a [[Places/Leyline beneath Wachterhaus|leyline]] which is used in performing the etherealness ritual.
- The estate owned by Lady Wachter and the location of a [[Places/Leyline beneath Wachterhaus|leyline]] beneath.
- Has dining room and [[Places/Parlor|parlor]] areas for hosting visitors like the players.
- Has a cellar that serves as a [[Places/Cult Headquarters|cult headquarters]] for rituals.
- Location where [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]] is challenged by Lady Fiona.
- Ancestral home of [[Factions/House Wachter|House Wachter]].

## DM Notes

- [[Places/Wachterhaus|Wachterhaus]] contains significant items including the religious texts *Voices of the Mist* and *The Grimoire of Night's Whispers*, which connect with themes in Arc H - The Lost Soul.
- It is referenced both in the war council event and in the dream vision, showing its continuity beyond the immediate time frame.
- Foreshadows Lady Wachter's betrayal history and her family's relationship with the Van Richten family.
- Its layout reveals details about Lady Wachter’s past, particularly regarding her mentor [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]].
- Serves as a setting for both the restoration ritual and the final battle against Leo Dilisnya.
