# Abbey of Saint Markovia

> **place** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- The [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|abbey]] plays an integral role in the player's story, including access to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s castle via the Heist Arc.
- A sanctuary built by [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]], now under the care of the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] and filled with mysteries and secrets.
- It contains a madhouse housing the transformed Belviews and has various wings where different NPCs operate.
- The main hall contains the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] and his [[Items/Flesh Golem|flesh golem]] [[Items/Vasilka|Vasilka]] in their roles as guides and teachers.
- Visiting the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]] will eventually connect players with broader campaign plots involving [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- It serves as a key location for recruiting [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]] and conducting supernatural investigations.
- Was besieged and eventually destroyed by Strahd's forces during his war against its followers.
- It is connected with the Morninglord and serves as a place of spiritual danger and conflict.
- The Abbey houses a blessed pool where wolfsbane grows and is used to cure lycanthropy.
- Built atop [[Places/Mount Baratok|Mount Baratok]] by a cleric named [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]], who was killed by Strahd.
- The Abbot has a secret plot to collect hearts to strengthen his [[Items/Flesh Golem|flesh golem]] [[Items/Vasilka|Vasilka]].
- Key point in the game for unlocking quests via a hook involving [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda d'Avenir]].
- Served as a strategic location near [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] and helped grow the local hamlet.
- After decades of madness and cannibalism, it was reopened by the current Abbot.
- Contains several clues for the [[Items/Tarokka reading|Tarokka reading]]: nursery, main hall, and shrine.
- Founded as a sanctuary away from the battlefield during conflict in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- The players will meet Ezmerelda d'Avenir there after gaining access.
- It has a sordid history and strange rumors surrounding its ruler.
- The Abbot of the Abbey is the primary antagonist of this arc.
- It is the destination for the players when entering Krezk.
- Later became the home to refugees, including [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]].
- Location where a holy artifact is hidden in Act III.
- Built by Saint Markovia on a spur of [[Places/Mount Baratok|Mount Baratok]].
- Site of abbot’s madness that needs purging.

## DM Notes

- The entire Abbey structure holds significant backstory connected to Saint Markovia and contains important information for redemption of the Abbot.
- The Abbey's lore and the Abbot's true nature are central to future story arcs like Arc K - The Fallen Abbey.
- Future events tie into exploring the Abbey's deeper secrets and connections to Strahd and his curse.
