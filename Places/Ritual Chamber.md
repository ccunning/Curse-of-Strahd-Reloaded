# Ritual Chamber

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Located in Appendix B: Area 38, connected to the [[Places/Ritual Chamber|Ritual Chamber]] and leads into a cave occupied by a flesh mound.
- The [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] from this area is crucial for activating certain mechanisms in other rooms.
- A forty-foot-square room with smooth masonry walls, murky water covering most of the floor.
- Contains elements like an altar and a large door leading to the basement dungeon level.
- Has connections with cult practices involving sacrifices and supernatural influence.
- Features an octagonal dais, a stone altar stained with dry blood, and rusty chains.
- Houses significant dangers such as a flesh mound that awakens at midnight.
- It is where 'FEED HIM' is carved on the altar, surrounded by human teeth.
- A chamber where dark rituals are conducted, involving a flesh mound.
- The room contains a hidden trapdoor leading to the Family Crypts.

## DM Notes

- Its activation mechanism tied directly to an [[Items/Amber Shard|amber shard]] adds complexity to navigating the manor, making it a significant puzzle piece.
- The [[Places/Ritual Chamber|ritual chamber]] may connect the house to broader forces or events in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], especially concerning [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and his minions.
