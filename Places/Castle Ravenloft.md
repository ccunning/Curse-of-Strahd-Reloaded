> **place** · 9 source files

## Key Facts

- Has a specific layout involving multiple levels such as the first, second, third, fourth, and fifth levels which lead into nightmares.
- Players meet [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] here in multiple encounters: at [[Places/River Ivlis Crossroads|River Ivlis Crossroads]], [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]], and dine with him here in Arc O.
- Has a complex structure with towers, an organ, a drawbridge, [[Places/Servants’ Quarters|servants’ quarters]], and [[Places/Catacombs|catacombs]] beneath it.
- Holds many secrets, including the hidden chamber where the traitor [[NPCs/Sasha Ivliskova|Sasha]] meets players post-dinner.
- The interior includes multiple floors and rooms that reflect historical events and present dangers.
- Built over four centuries ago on the ruins of a much older fortress atop [[Places/Pillarstone of Ravenloft|Pillarstone of Ravenloft]].
- Connected to various arcs like Arc K - The Fallen [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]], P - Ravenloft Heist, and others.
- Features various notable levels including guest quarters, [[Places/Catacombs|catacombs]], and upper towers.
- Where [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]] joined a rebellion and where the original thefts took place in the past.
- Contains key locations like the crypts and halls where artifacts are hidden or held.
- It is the primary location for the players' encounter with [[NPCs/Varushka|Varushka]]'s nightmare.
- A central location with levels like the Garrison, [[Places/Dungeons|Dungeons]], and the North Tower.
- Served as the source of many artifacts, including the *[[Items/Sigil of the Sun|Sigil of the Sun]]*.
- Contains crypts, a magical vault, and many traps designed to protect it.
- A mystical castle shaped like a coffin located in the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Serves as the center of power in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] after [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s transformation.
- The site where many pivotal events occurred, including [[NPCs/Tatyana Federovna|Tatyana]]’s death.
- Players may explore its treasure rooms via the coin or other relics.
- Strahd’s castle built atop the ruins of [[NPCs/King Dostron|King Dostron]]’s fortress.
- Contains hidden passageways, secret doors, and dangerous traps.
