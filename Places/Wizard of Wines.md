# Wizard of Wines

> **place** · 4 source files

## Key Facts

- Constructed on ancient lands, originally a place for the production of wine from enchanted gemstones.
- Was invaded by members of the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]]; now inhabited by [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov family]] in underground caverns.
- Its destruction was linked to events involving magical forces and the betrayal of family members.
- Used as a source of wealth for the family, and later became symbolic as part of the narrative.
- Had a role in creating historical artifacts such as Champagne du Stompe and associated magic.
- Though not a significant threat, it is part of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s disciplined oversight via [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]].
- Has been under attack from druids and their allies after thefts of gems from it.
- Established trade with both [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] and [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|the Abbey of Saint Markovia]].
- Stores grapes for wines like Purple Grapemash No. 3 and Red Dragon Crush.
- Has an enchanted gemstone, stolen by the druids, used for grape growing.
- Fostered growth in local settlements during the era of development.
- Named after the belief that it was created by a powerful mage.
- Used the lifegiving gemstones of the [[NPCs/Weaver|Weaver]] to grow grapes.
- A winery located to the southwest end of Barovian valley.
- A winery stronghold for [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- Winery in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] that becomes corrupted by druids.
- Contained special enchanted gems which were stolen.
- [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] delegates discipline to his minions there.
- Key site leading to [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- An old winery located to the west in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- A winery built by wereravens in the valley.
- A family estate managed by the Martikovs.

## DM Notes

- This site is central to the plot progression in this arc—players investigate here before traveling to [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] for [[NPCs/Wintersplinter|Wintersplinter]].
- The loss of gems ties back into future occurrences where heroes must confront mysteries tied to magic in Barovia.
