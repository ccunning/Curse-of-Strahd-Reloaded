# Balinok Mountains

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Located between the valley and the land of the later kingdoms, such as Dostron’s realm.
- The mountains sheltered [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]] after they were forced from their sacred lands.
- Where [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]] entered and where [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] led a war campaign against the tribes.
- Connected to [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] near their peak.
- A range that bordered the valley of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
