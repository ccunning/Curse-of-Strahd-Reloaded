# Catacombs

> **place** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- The location where Rahadin appears during the final phase of his confrontation after players leave [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]]'s crypt.
- A significant section of the [[Places/Catacombs|catacombs]] mentioned during the layout of connections involving multiple castle areas.
- Connected to the castle through various secret passages including from the central [[Places/Catacombs|catacombs]].
- It includes several key areas such as the [[Places/North Crypts|North Crypts]], Central Crypts, and [[Places/South Crypts|South Crypts]].
- Plays an important role in introducing key combat scenarios involving death slads.
- Area located beneath the castle, part of the structure's historical elements.
- Contains hidden paths and illusions meant to misdirect characters entering.
- Contains multiple crypts across three sections: North, Central, and South.
- A complex network of underground passages beneath [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
- Part of [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]], containing multiple chambers and traps.
- Serves as a key area for both combat and roleplaying encounters.
- Home to [[NPCs/Nardag|Nardag]] and [[NPCs/Bluetspur|Bluetspur]], two slaads fighting together.
- Home to various traps, undead, and magical runes.
- Part of the castle's history and architecture.

## DM Notes

- Catacombs lead into crucial parts of [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] and are central to major events involving enemies and lore.
- Rahadin specifically makes his appearance in this location after players interact with [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Saint Markovia]]'s tomb.
- The entire area is heavily populated with undead and traps.
