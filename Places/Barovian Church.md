# Barovian Church

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Played a part in establishing a framework for divine opposition against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s tyranny.
- Connected to key figures like [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]] and [[NPCs/Escher|Escher]] during major moments of conflict.
- Used during the siege against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]], where [[NPCs/Father Donavich|Father Donavich]] was attacked.
- Served as battleground between religious faith and supernatural force.
- Symbolized faith, tradition, and resistance in the face of tyranny.

## DM Notes
