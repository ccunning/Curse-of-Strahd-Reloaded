# Creeping Hut

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- The hut's special mechanics include throwing creatures up to 60 feet, grappling them, and biting creatures who attempt to retrieve the gem from the stump.
- The hut cannot use ranged weapons if it's within 5 feet of a hostile creature due to a 'Close Quarters Fighter' trait.
- The [[Places/Creeping Hut|Creeping Hut]] is a Gargantuan construct with a high Armor Class and immunity to many conditions.
- It has a magic gem embedded in its stump that animates it, and the hut dies if the gem is removed.
- It can attack with its root, throw enemies, and attack with a rock at range.

## DM Notes

- The Stolen Gem feature suggests future adventures or questlines related to recovering or protecting the magical item that animates the hut.
