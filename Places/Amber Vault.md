# Amber Vault

> **place** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Each one leads to a unique fantasy environment and has a distinctive gift related to elements like cold, spiders, sight, strength, endurance, death invocation, invisibility, or fiendish command.
- Players are warned about losing their humanity with each use of a gift without careful consideration.
- Contain powerful dark gifts that transform players but bring increasingly severe corruption stages.
- The sarcophagi are tied directly to major lore about the Fanes and [[Factions/Dark Powers|Dark Powers]].
- Sarcophagi in [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] designed to hold vestiges of evil beings.

## DM Notes

- Different sarcophagi represent thematic sections in [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]], each leading deeper into the corrupted psyche of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
