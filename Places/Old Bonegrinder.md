# Old Bonegrinder

> **place** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- Contains hidden chambers including a compartment in the millstone believed by [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]] to be where [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] keeps secrets such as heartstones or her young daughters.
- A windmill located on a hill in the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich Wood]], serving as the lair of the three hags [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha Stormreaver]], [[NPCs/Belladonna Sunbane|Belladonna Sunbane]], and [[NPCs/Offalia Wormwiggle|Offalia Wormwiggle]].
- The windmill's hill is surrounded by a binding circle during battles with the hags, which prevents the hags from using their *etherealness* ability.
- Hostile territory for both players and cultists due to its proximity to Morgantha’s lair and active presence of hags.
- The area around the windmill is known for its proximity to [[Places/The Mountain Fane|the Mountain Fane]], marked by megaliths shrouded in fog.
- Contains a stone compartment in its millstone that holds [[Items/The Hags' Contract|the hags' contract]] and a *[[Items/Charm of Mass Heroism|charm of mass heroism]]*.
- A location where a [[NPCs/Night Hag|night hag]]'s lair is rumored to be nestled inside a windmill near the [[Places/Svalich Woods|Svalich]] Road.
- The setting for an upcoming assault planned by [[NPCs/Victor Vallakovich|Victor]] and Lady Wachter against Morgantha's coven.
- Key location in the campaign as a site where children were taken and dark rituals are performed.
- The third floor of the mill holds imprisoned children, [[NPCs/Fyodor|Fyodor]] and [[NPCs/Myrtle|Myrtle]], guarded by the hags.
- [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] is aware of the presence and activities, though shows little interest or engagement.
- Used to grind grain and was strategically located on a promontory overlooking valley.
- Participated in the economy of the valley but later became associated with conflict.
- Connected to the Dursts' story and their role in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]]’s history.
- Represented early development after the fall of [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]].
- A mill built near [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] by the Durst family.
- A location associated with [[NPCs/Night Hag|night hag]] covens.
