In this act, which begins the morning after the players’ first full moon in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]], the players receive a pair of invitations.

The first, a plea from [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin Martikov]], asks the players to travel to [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] winery to the west in order to investigate a mysterious wine shortage in [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]].

The second, a letter from [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd von Zarovich]] himself, invites the players to dine at [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] with him and his court on the night before the new moon in [[Arc O - Dinner with the Devil]].

Should the players accept the Martikovs’ mission in [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]], they gain two companions for the journey: [[NPCs/Muriel Vinshaw|Muriel Vinshaw]], a **wereraven** agent of [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]]; and [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]], who asks to join the players permanently in the fight against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]. The players must rescue the winery from the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] **druids** who have conquered it and, once they have done so, retrieve an enchanted gem from [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] to the south before [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] falls to destruction.

Meanwhile, as the players explore other leads across the western half of the [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], they can recruit the Vistana monster hunter [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]] d’Avenir in [[Arc K - The Fallen Abbey]], investigate the [[Factions/Werewolf Pack|werewolf pack]] in [[Arc L - The Den of Wolves]], and uncover the mysteries of [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] in [[Arc M - The Dragon's Manor]].

In the process, they’ll obtain multiple reasons to journey to [[Places/Castle Ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]]: to recover the *Icon of Ravenloft* to cleanse the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]’s corruption in [[Arc K - The Fallen Abbey]]; to rescue the **werewolf** [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil Toranescu]] in exchange for the *[[Items/Holy Symbol of Ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]]* in [[Arc L - The Den of Wolves]]; and to steal the skull of the dragon [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]] to bring hope to the land once more in [[Arc M - The Dragon's Manor]].

On the last night before the new moon, the players can dine with Strahd von Zarovich and the members of his court in [[Arc O - Dinner with the Devil]]. There, they’ll learn that Strahd will be gone from the castle the following night, creating the perfect opportunity to infiltrate the castle in [[Arc P - Ravenloft Heist]]. Once they have done so, the players must then return to the western valley to purify the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]]’s soul, defeat the **werewolf** [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril Stoyanovich]], and relight the [[Places/Beacon of Argynvostholt|beacon of Argynvostholt]].

This act ends at dusk on the first night after the players infiltrate Castle Ravenloft in [[Arc P - Ravenloft Heist]]. However, the players can still complete unresolved arcs from this act even after Act IV begins—assuming, of course, that those arcs are still possible to complete.
# Milestones
The players begin this act at 5th level and should end it at 8th level, gaining milestone XP when they successfully do any of the following:

* defeat the druids occupying the Wizard of Wines winery (2,000 XP)
* retrieve the stolen gem from [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] (2,500 XP)
* recruit [[NPCs/Ezmerelda d'Avenir|Ezmerelda]] d’Avenir at the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey of St. Markovia]] (1,000 XP)
* bargain with [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika Toranescu]] at the [[Places/Werewolf Den|Werewolf Den]] (1,500 XP)
* free [[NPCs/Ilya Krezkov|Ilya]] and [[NPCs/Zuleika Toranescu|Zuleika]] from the den (250 XP)
* receive [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]]’s mission at [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] (2,500 XP)
* put [[NPCs/Varushka|Varushka]]’s ghost to rest and find her hidden cache in Castle Ravenloft (500 XP)
* return from Castle Ravenloft following the dinner with Strahd (3,500 XP)
* steal Argynvost’s skull from Ravenloft’s **[[Places/Hall of Bones|Hall of Bones]]** (4000 XP)
* recover the *Icon of Ravenloft* from Ravenloft’s [[Places/Catacombs|catacombs]] (250 XP)
* obtain the *Spear of the [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]]* from [[NPCs/King Dostron|King Dostron]]'s crypt (250 XP)
* rescue [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil Toranescu]] from Ravenloft’s [[Places/Dungeons|dungeons]] (250 XP)
* rescue [[NPCs/Gertruda|Gertruda]] from the master bedroom of Castle Ravenloft (250 XP)
* raid Strahd’s hidden [[Places/Treasury|treasury]] in Castle Ravenloft (250 XP)
* defeat the Abbot (2,000 XP)
* redeem the Abbot after defeating him (500 XP)
* defeat [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril Stoyanovich]] (2,000 XP)
* light the [[Places/Beacon of Argynvostholt|beacon of Argynvostholt]] (3,000 XP)

The most common path through Act III is as follows:

* **Neyavr 9.** The players receive [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin]] [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]]'s request to complete [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]] (🔶).
* **Neyavr 10.** The players travel to the Wizard of Wines winery and defeat the druids of the [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] there in [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]].
* **Neyavr 11.** The players travel to Yester Hill at dawn and stop the ritual to summon the **[[NPCs/Tree Blight|tree blight]]** [[NPCs/Wintersplinter|Wintersplinter]] in [[Arc J - The Stolen Gem]]. The players then meet Ezmerelda d'Avenir in the [[Places/Village of Krezk|village of Krezk]] and conduct a seance at the Abbey of St. [[NPCs/Saint Markovia|Markovia]] in [[Arc K - The Fallen Abbey]], then stake out the [[Places/Werewolf Den|werewolf den]] at nightfall in [[Arc L - The Den of Wolves]].
* **Neyavr 12.** The players investigate Argynvostholt in [[Arc M - The Dragon's Manor]].
* **Neyavr 13.** The players take a day of downtime.
* **Neyavr 14.** The players dine with Strahd at dusk in [[Arc O - Dinner with the Devil]] (🔶).
* **Neyavr 15 (New Moon).** The players infiltrate Castle Ravenloft at dusk, then escape through the teleportation brazier in [[Arc P - Ravenloft Heist]] (🔶).
* **Neyavr 16.** The players light the beacon of Argynvostholt and defeat the werewolf Kiril Stoyanovich in [[Arc Q - A Shining Beacon]] (🔶).
