# Witches of Mother Night

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] leads a coven, teaching followers to regard themselves as siblings of [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- They have members in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] and serve [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] indirectly through [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla Vilisevic]].
- Their members are often outcasts, exiles, or those seeking vengeance.
- They worship [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]] instead of Strahd or the Morninglord.
- A dark cult seeking the power and favor of [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]].

## DM Notes

- Their dark influence may be involved in various plot threads involving lycanthropy or corruption.
