# Ezra

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- She is revealed not to exist, but is instead a simulacrum produced by [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]].
- Players' understanding of her nature changes in the latter parts of the campaign.
- The god worshipped by [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]]’s cult; believed to be the Mistwalker.
- Shepherd of the [[Places/Mists|Mists]], representing power as illusion and suffering as reality.
- Many Barovians turn to this faith in times of despair.

## DM Notes

- The truth about '[[Factions/Ezra|Ezra]]' will unfold during character arcs and climax involving [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]].
