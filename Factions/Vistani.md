# Vistani

> **faction** · 5 source files

## Key Facts

- They were once saved by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] when he was still a living soldier, and later allowed to leave the [[Places/Mists|mists]] after becoming undead.
- A member of the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] is working as a spy for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]], remaining under that guise, and has joined [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|the Keepers of the Feather]].
- They play a role in the campaign's lore as a bridge between the mortal world and the supernatural realm of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Represent knowledge about local legends, magic, and possible resistance networks within the land.
- Play roles that connect lore from different perspectives and may aid players subtly or overtly.
- The [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] are loyal to Strahd in the [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] area, while [[Places/Tser Pool Encampment|Tser Pool]] tends to be more neutral.
- A group of people with deep ties to the region of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], particularly the [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] camp.
- A nomadic people connected to the mystical and often secretive practices in Barovia.
- They may be encountered during the campaign and contribute to its social dynamics.
- A nomadic group dwelling in the valley around the time of Strahd's transformation.
- Known for their connection to fate and prophecy, especially in relation to Strahd.
- A cultural group in Barovia whose views are described as racist by the campaign.
- [[NPCs/Arrigal|Arrigal]] is a notable member of their group who interacts with Strahd’s court.
- Played an important role in aiding and protecting those fleeing from Strahd.
- The faction has cultural ties to certain supernatural events in Barovia.
- Provided sanctuary to many refugees, including the [[Factions/Dusk Elves|dusk elves]].
- [[NPCs/Katarina|Katarina]] was a Vistani woman and later became [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]].
- Cultural group involved in foretellings and prophecies.
- Encounter [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]] to receive future predictions.

## DM Notes

- Their unique ability to travel through the [[Places/Mists|mists]] suggests they may be key to uncovering the mysteries of Barovia.
- Could provide insight into future threats or resources, especially as related to information gathering.
- The Vistani are linked to future events involving Strahd and his fixation on specific women.
- May be instrumental in the fight against Strahd due to their unique abilities or access.
