# Vallakians

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- They are described as being afraid of the unknowns in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], especially [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s presence.
- The majority of citizens have given up freedom for safety, though they remain silent.
- [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]] and her followers form an opposition faction within them.
- They are the people of the town [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]] and largely support the Baron.
- Most fear [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]], who enforces his will harshly.

## DM Notes

- Their loyalty is fragile and may shift depending on events and choices in the campaign.
