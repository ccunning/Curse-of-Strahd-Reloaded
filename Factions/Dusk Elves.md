# Dusk Elves

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Received refugees from other nations during [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s expansion, especially the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]].
- An elven population conquered by King Barov and subjugated to his rule.
- Their royal line was destroyed by Barov, leading to their subjugation.
- Staged a rebellion against the governor after [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] took over.
- Found sanctuary in the valley with support from the [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]].
