# Death House

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Contains multiple floors with various rooms including a main hall, [[Places/Library|library]], children's room, [[Places/Master Suite|master suite]], and basement levels with cult quarters.
- The house holds a dark history related to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s early followers, as noted in the lore section about [[Factions/Strahd's First Followers|Strahd's First Followers]].
- Players must either appease or defeat a monster within the house before it begins to collapse and transports them to [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- The central setting of the first area, which collapses following the defeat of the flesh mound and Elisabeth Durst’s spirit.
- Has an informal agreement between its cult and [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] allowing it to venture outside [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] for supplies.
- Players enter it in their own world and emerge into Barovia after defeating or pacifying the cult within.
- It is the focal point of the first arc, where players are trapped and must navigate its dark corridors.
- [[Factions/Death House|Death House]] is a cursed estate that serves as the starting location for the players in the adventure.
- This house is connected to the Durst family history and contains clues about their tragic past.
- Their methods and obsessions are distasteful to Strahd, but their efforts are appreciated.
- Is the origin point for both adventure hooks ('Lost in the [[Places/Mists|Mists]]' and '[[Items/Barovian Relics|Barovian Relics]]').
- Serves as the primary setting of the initial escape from [[Factions/Death House|Death House]] adventure hook.
- The house collapses after players escape, leaving behind a dark and bottomless pit.
- They are known for bringing worthy consorts and adversaries from beyond the [[Places/Mists|Mists]].
- Death House is the main location where players are trapped during the adventure.
- It is located outside of [[Places/Daggerford|Daggerford]] at the beginning of the [[Places/Old Svalich Road|Old Svalich Road]].
- A grand, old manor known for disappearances and mysterious occurrences.
- Contains secret stairs and leads players into the land of Barovia.
- Haunted location that serves as a gateway into Barovia.
- A cult that Strahd views with respectful disdain.
- Used as a portal to Strahd's domain, Barovia.
- Located in Barovia, near [[Places/Daggerford|Daggerford]].

## DM Notes

- The social encounters in Death House foreshadow future characters like Strahd, Sergi von Zarovich, and [[NPCs/Lady Fiona Wachter|Lady Fiona Wachter]].
- Death House collapsing, implying that this house is not just a location for exploration but something malevolent itself.
- The house's collapse occurs if players kill the monster within it.
- The house’s history ties into broader lore about Strahd’s origins.
- Arc A. Escape From Death House.
