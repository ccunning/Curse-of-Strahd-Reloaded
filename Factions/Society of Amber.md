# Society of Amber

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- It played a significant role in the downfall of the temple due to internal conflicts.
- The members were ultimately corrupted by dark energies seeping through the amber.
- Its goal was to contain and study vestiges using amber sarcophagi.
- A secret society of mages who founded [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
