# Ba’al Verzi

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- The motto of the guild 'In Silence, Death', appears etched in the walls of the [[Places/Cult Headquarters|cult headquarters]].
- Leo betrayed his order’s vows when attempting to kill [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]], betraying its core values.
- Has dagger weapons that are central in Leo's avenger form during combat.
- Connected to [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]]'s backstory and motives to avenge his family.
- A secret assassins' guild belonging to [[NPCs/Leo Dilisnya|Leo Dilisnya]].

## DM Notes

- References to this organization connect back to the deeper political intrigue of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Its dagger is key to the plot involving the sealing mechanisms of fane corruption.
