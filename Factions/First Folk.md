# First Folk

> **faction** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Split into two groups due to conflict with [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and [[NPCs/King Dostron|King Dostron]]: [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] and [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]].
- The people built shrines (fanes) to honor the Ladies Three, which later became places of power.
- The druids among them were banished by [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] for hubris but later moved to [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]].
- A nomadic society of hunter-gatherers who first inhabited the valley of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Were unified under warlord [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]], leading to the fall of their peaceful society.
- Worshipped a quartet of minor fey goddesses collectively known as the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]].
- Their remnants scattered and hid from the increasing corruption in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Their homeland 'Girvisa' (meaning fertile land) was corrupted by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- An ancient people of the valley who arrived long before Kavan's birth.
- Worshipped the triad of [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]].

## DM Notes

- Conflict with the [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] over betrayal and belief in Strahd sets up future interactions.
- Their religious practices and history may become key in accessing the secret of [[Places/Soldav|Soldav]].
