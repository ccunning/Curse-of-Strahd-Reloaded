# The Bonegrinder Coven

> **faction** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- When reduced to 0 HP, the coven transitions into a second form called *[[NPCs/The Nightmare Three|The Nightmare Three]]*, where they assume terrifying new forms—Bella into a crocodile, [[NPCs/Offalia Wormwiggle|Offalia]] into an elephant, and [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]] into shadowy enshrouding form.
- A trio of night hags: [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha Stormreaver]], [[NPCs/Belladonna Sunbane|Belladonna Sunbane]], and [[NPCs/Offalia Wormwiggle|Offalia Wormwiggle]] who share a collective set of abilities and statistics that are more robust than individual hags.
- They possess *Innate Spellcasting*, *Claws*, *Change Shape*, *Etherealness*, and *Nightmare Haunting* features, and do not benefit from shared spellcasting normally.
- Coven members are particularly dangerous in their lair actions: Warding Wind, Gust of Wind, Amphibious Cacophony (to lure targets), and Dispel Magic (once per day).
- They have special abilities such as magic resistance, hag coven traits, and the ability to fight in unison.
- Their shared hit points mean damage taken by one hag is subtracted from the coven's total.
- Each member has unique spellcasting powers that complement the coven’s overall effect.
- If reduced to 0 hit points, they transform into [[NPCs/The Nightmare Three|The Nightmare Three]].
- They function as both a combat unit and an integrated magical team.
- A trio of night hags, including Morgantha, Bella, and Offalia.

## DM Notes

- The coven forms an integral part of the larger threat landscape in [[Items/Curse of Strahd|Curse of Strahd]], particularly in regards to Morgantha’s plans involving souls, the soul bag, and the overall narrative arc concerning [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
