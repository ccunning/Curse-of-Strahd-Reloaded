# Martikov family

> **faction** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Includes members like Adrian, Elvir, Stefania, Dag, [[NPCs/Claudiu|Claudiu]], [[NPCs/Martin|Martin]], Viggo, and [[NPCs/Davian Martikov|Davian]] who each have distinct personalities and stances on matters concerning [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] and the rebellion.
- Several family members were involved in a feud that includes questions over whether [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin]] stole their third enchanted gem.
- The family's relationships are complicated due to past tragedies such as [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]]’s death fighting in [[NPCs/Doru|Doru]]’s rebellion.
- A noble family in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] with complex internal dynamics and differing views on the death of [[NPCs/Elric Martikov|Elric]] [[Factions/Martikov family|Martikov]].
- They live in [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] area of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] and are connected to the events around [[NPCs/Urwin Martikov|Urwin]]'s theft.
- Symbolized continuity and reclamation within the valley’s changing social structure.
- Descendants of the druids who originally owned [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] winery.
- Regained ownership of the winery through marriage and inheritance schemes.
- Maintained relations with Krezkova family despite their rivalry.
- Assimilated among the Barovian people after centuries.
