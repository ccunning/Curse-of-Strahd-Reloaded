# Ladies of the Fanes

> **faction** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- The [[Factions/Mountain Folk|Mountain Folk]] still hold faith to these goddesses but remember only their symbols and roles.
- Only the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]] lives on in Avatar form through a pact with [[NPCs/Katarina|Katarina]], manifesting as [[NPCs/Madam Eva|Madam Eva]].
- Their holy places—the [[Places/Swamp Fane|Swamp Fane]], [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane]], and Mountain Fane—have been desecrated.
- Once three powerful goddesses who ruled over the valley, now mostly spirits.
- They were associated with craftsmanship, foresight, and beasts respectively.
- Recognized by members of the [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|Keepers]] or [[NPCs/Kavan|Kavan]] for deeper lore connection.
- Players encounter them spiritually in [[Arc T - The Three Fanes]].
- Refers to three fey goddesses: [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]], [[NPCs/Weaver|Weaver]], and [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]].
- The [[Items/Stone Crest|stone crest]] symbolizes these goddesses.

## DM Notes

- The spiritual conflicts around them may influence events involving prophecy or divine intervention.
- They guide the players through mythic lore and divine harmony.
