# Cult of Chernovog

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Assembled rituals at [[Places/Forest Fane|Forest Fane at Yester Hill]], breaking [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s bond with the fane.
- Sought to summon him into [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] as means of overthrowing [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Led to the temporary loss of the *[[Items/Holy Symbol of Ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]]*.
- Influenced major turning points in the valley's history.
- Fiendish cult venerated the demon prince [[NPCs/Chernovog|Chernovog]].
