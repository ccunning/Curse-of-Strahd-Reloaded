# Brides of Strahd

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Each bride is known for different abilities: [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]] is the arcane researcher, [[NPCs/Anastrasya Karelova|Anastrasya]] is skilled in espionage, [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]] is a master of traps and torture.
- They gather intelligence through beasts of the valley: [[NPCs/Wolves|wolves]] to [[NPCs/Volenta|Volenta]], bats to [[NPCs/Anastrasya Karelova|Anastrasya]], rats to [[NPCs/Ludmilla|Ludmilla]].
- They all compete for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s favor, vying to prove their worth and remain close to him.
- Post-Sunrise Awakening, each bride has been pursuing her own agenda within [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- They are three vampiric consorts who serve [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] loyally.

## DM Notes

- Their ambitions and power may be central during Strahd’s rise or during climactic confrontations.
