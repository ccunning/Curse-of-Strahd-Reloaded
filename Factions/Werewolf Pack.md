# Werewolf Pack

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- They originally came into conflict with [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] after his reawakening by demanding loyalty as previously agreed.
- [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]] leads the pack as [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s servant, leading attacks in the wilds of [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- [[NPCs/Emil Toranescu|Emil]] was the original leader, but was betrayed and replaced by [[NPCs/Kiril Stoyanovich|Kiril]].
- A pack of [[NPCs/Werewolves|werewolves]] loyal to [[NPCs/Mother Night|Mother Night]] until Strahd's awakening.
- They are tied into lycanthropy mechanics specific to the setting.

## DM Notes

- The pack may play roles in encounters involving lycanthropy or serve as targets of betrayal.
