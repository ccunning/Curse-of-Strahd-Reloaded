# Radanavich Clan

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Eventually became enemies following the loss of their son due to Rudolph van Richten’s quest for vengeance.
- Played key roles in shaping future interactions between [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] and others, establishing conflict zones.
- Formed alliance with Baron Metus to kidnap victims for his personal needs.
- Killed [[NPCs/Erasmus van Richten|Erasmus van Richten]] as part of their plot against Rudolph.
- A [[Factions/Vistani|Vistani]] clan led by [[NPCs/Madame Irene Radanavich|Madame Irene Radanavich]].

## DM Notes

- The radicalization of this clan may lead to more conflicts or alliances with other tribes in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
