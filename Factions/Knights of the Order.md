# Knights of the Order

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- The order was destroyed in a war against [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd von Zarovich]], who slew [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]] and desecrated the Order's legacy.
- The order swore an oath to uphold virtue, with honor as their shield and truth as their guide.
- Its members include notable figures like Commander [[NPCs/Vladimir Horngaard|Vladimir Horngaard]] and [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Sir Godfrey Gwilym]].
- A noble order dedicated to protecting [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] from evil, founded by [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]].
- Their symbols include the silver dragon and the oath which they swore.
