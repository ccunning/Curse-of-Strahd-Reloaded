# Rozana

> **faction** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- Each governed a different aspect of life, with symbols including the spider, wolf, raven, and butterfly.
- Comprised four sisters: the [[NPCs/Weaver|Weaver]] (spring), [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]] (summer), [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]] (autumn), and [[NPCs/Dreamer|Dreamer]] (winter).
- Their worship was practiced by [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]] and their priests were known as druids.
- The [[NPCs/Dreamer|Dreamer]] turned to darkness after being denied a shrine by [[Factions/First Folk|the First Folk]].
- The quartet of minor fey goddesses who ruled the valley in ancient times.
- The Weavers' gems gave life and vitality to the wilderness around them.
- Each goddess has a corresponding holy site known as a Fane.
- Consists of the [[NPCs/Seeker|Seeker]], the [[NPCs/Weaver|Weaver]], and the [[NPCs/Huntress|Huntress]].
- The druids paid homage to the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]] at their fanes.
- A trio of goddesses worshiped by the First Folk.

## DM Notes

- The gemstones associated with the [[Factions/Rozana|Rozana]] are central to both historical conflict and future quests.
