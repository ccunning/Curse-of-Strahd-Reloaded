# Forest Folk

> **faction** · 3 source files

## Key Facts

- The [[Factions/Forest Folk|Forest Folk]] recently formed a conflict with [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] Winery due to the [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|Keepers]]' presence there.
- They serve as useful tools but must be used carefully since they're not fully loyal.
- Seem to be preparing an evil ritual involving a [[NPCs/Tree Blight|tree blight]] called [[NPCs/Wintersplinter|Wintersplinter]].
- A group of druids from [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]] who are enemies of the [[Factions/Keepers of the Feather|Keepers]] and Martikovs.
- A faction of druids who betrayed the old ways to protect themselves from [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- They were previously allied with the druidic elders during [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s invasion.
- Aligned with [[NPCs/Baba Lysaga|Baba Lysaga]] and involved in various attacks on the winery.
- They are led by [[NPCs/Svarog|Svarog]] and are known to worship Strahd as a deity.
- Their leader is [[NPCs/Svarog|Svarog]], a mad druid bonded to [[Items/Gulthias tree|the Gulthias Tree]].
- A group of druids who have invaded [[Places/Wizard of Wines|the Wizard of Wines]] winery.
- They nurture a cutting of [[Items/Gulthias tree|the Gulthias tree]] within the winery.
- They accept Strahd as their deity, becoming obsessed with him.
- Plan to summon a being called [[NPCs/Wintersplinter|Wintersplinter]] at [[Places/Yester Hill|Yester Hill]].
- Modern generation lacks the nobility of older generations.
- They are despised by Strahd and found amusing.

## DM Notes

- Their actions and influence may lead players into direct confrontation with other factions or plots.
- The druids’ lair is located on Yester Hill, a key location for upcoming encounters.
- Their leader Svarog will be met and challenged during the climax of the arc.
