# Cult Fanatics

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Though not explicitly characterized further, they likely operate under orders aligned with Lady Wachter’s agenda and possibly part of a broader organization.
- Are seen participating in the raising of the binding circle around [[Places/Old Bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]] in cooperation with Lady Wachter.
- They act as guardians of the circle, essential to protecting against magical counterattacks from [[NPCs/Morgantha|Morgantha]]’s coven.
- If any one is knocked unconscious, it will cause the entire ritual to fail and the hags to escape.
- Four followers supporting Lady Wachter in performing ritual binding circles.

## DM Notes

- The [[Factions/Cult Fanatics|cult fanatics]] hint at a hidden underground resistance or opposition to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] in [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]], whose actions directly connect to the local conflict narrative.
