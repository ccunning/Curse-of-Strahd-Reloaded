# The Belviews

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- They were transformed via surgeries performed by the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]], replacing body parts with animal limbs and features in attempts to 'perfect' them.
- They believe their transformation will eventually result in a complete 'perfection', even though the [[NPCs/Abbot of the Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbot]] claims he will find a way.
- A family descended from early members of the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]] suffering from a congenital disease causing premature aging and physical frailty.
- The descendants of [[Factions/The Belviews|the Belviews]] interbred within the [[Places/Abbey of Saint Markovia|Abbey]] over generations, becoming twisted chimeras with deep madness.
- The process resulted in madness and uncontrollable mental states; they are now isolated due to this.

## DM Notes

- Their condition is part of the overarching lore connected to the Abbot's fall from grace and his corrupted state.
