# Wights

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Used as guards and sentries to protect the castle and its grounds.
- Symbolized how [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] maintained power through fear and death.
- Undead creatures raised by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] after his transformation.
- Revealed the extent of his mastery over life and death.
- Part of the army of undead that guarded the castle.
