# Dark Powers

## Key Facts

- They present [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]]'s soul as a means to end [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]'s reign, implying [[Factions/Dark Powers|the Dark Powers]]' ultimate goal involves shaping the cycle of reincarnation and power dynamics.
- They use the vision of [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena]]’s sacrifice as a powerful motivator for peace or conflict, offering a path to salvation or destruction based on player choice.
- An ancient force that manipulates events in the Barovian narrative and presents moral dilemmas to the players through visions and illusions.
- The mask of one of its members, named [[Factions/Ezra|Ezra]], appears as a grey-cloaked woman resembling Stella in [[Places/Lake Zarovich|Lake Zarovich]] during the dream.
- Entities that manifest in whispers and attempts to influence the characters within [[NPCs/Varushka|Varushka]]'s nightmare.
- A group or force that emerged as a result of Vilnius's betrayal and the subsequent death of [[NPCs/Exethanter|Exethanter]].
- They seek to manipulate players into staying in the nightmare or prevent them from escaping entirely.
- This group is implied to represent significant threats beyond normal gameplay obstacles.
- They attempt to prevent her from being taken by [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] through temporary oblivion.
- They feed off terror and despair, particularly from those trapped within.
- Created during the downfall of the original wizard society in the temple.
- They reside within [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] and trap individuals like Vilnius.
- They are nameless entities that govern [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] and jail the Darklords.
- They have offered Ireena Kolyana choices between light and darkness.
- Supernatural entities born from the corruption in [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]].
- [[Factions/Dark Powers|The Dark Powers]] feed off fear and madness of trapped individuals.
- They are associated with Strahd's pact and influence on [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Their purpose is to ensure Strahd von Zarovich remains punished.
- They are not malevolent themselves but are bound by cosmic laws.
