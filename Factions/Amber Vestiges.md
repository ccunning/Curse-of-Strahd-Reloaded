# Amber Vestiges

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Their ultimate goal is to free themselves from the amber [[Places/Prison|prison]] by claiming human souls.
- Shards of these vestiges are scattered across [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]], serving as gates for corruption.
- They dream of renewed power and aim to corrupt those who touch them.
- Non-sapient remnants of dead gods, imprisoned in amber sarcophagi.
- Elisabeth Durst once bore one, and now [[NPCs/Izek Strazni|Izek Strazni]] holds one.

## DM Notes

- The corruption of those touched by shards may become part of player-character arcs.
