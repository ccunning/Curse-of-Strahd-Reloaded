# Order of the Silver Dragon

> **faction** · 2 source files

## Key Facts

- An ancient order dedicated to justice and defending the weak, originating before the [[Places/Mists|Mists]] fell over [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- The order was driven back to [[Places/Argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] after [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s invasion and later banned from there by Vladimir.
- Has a secret purpose related to stopping corruption from [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] and safeguarding its power.
- Founded by [[NPCs/Argynvost|Lord Argynvost]], a silver dragon in disguise, who later fell during the war with [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Revenants of the Order, including Sir [[NPCs/Sir Godfrey Gwilym|Godfrey]], are bound by oaths to Commander [[NPCs/Vladimir Horngaard|Vladimir Horngaard]].
- Its existence marked resistance against Strahd's tyranny in [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Founded by [[NPCs/Argynvost|Argynvost]] to defend innocent people from evil forces.
- Used to protect [[Places/Amber Temple|the Amber Temple]] and ensure it remained secure.
- Fought against Strahd in a desperate effort to save the valley.
- Lost in battle at the [[Places/Luna River|Luna River]] when their leader was slain.

## DM Notes

- Will play a role in the final events involving the retrieval of Argynvost's remains; players' actions may restore the order.
