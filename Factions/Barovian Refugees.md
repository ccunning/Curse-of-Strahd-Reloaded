# Barovian Refugees

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- [[NPCs/Ireena Kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]] may be motivated by empathy and duty to help these people.
- They camp outside [[Places/Vallaki|Vallaki]]'s walls, seeking sanctuary but denied entry.
- Their hope diminishes constantly with lack of supplies and support.
- A group of displaced villagers from [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]] after [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s siege.
- Few have made the journey; most are lost to [[NPCs/Wolves|wolves]] or undead.

## DM Notes

- Players might encounter them, aiding them in the story or being challenged by their fate.
