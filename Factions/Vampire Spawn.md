# Vampire Spawn

> **faction** · 1 source file

## Key Facts

- Born through the consumption of life forces and turned into undead servants.
- Served purposes both as companions and tools for [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]’s rule over [[Places/Barovia|Barovia]].
- Creatures created through vampirism, often bound to [[NPCs/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]].
- Used as followers in Strahd's campaigns and as sacrifices.
- Symbolized a key aspect of Strahd's dark nature.
