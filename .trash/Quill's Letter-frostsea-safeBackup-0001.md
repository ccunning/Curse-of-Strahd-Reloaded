Felicitations and Salutations my friends,

If these words have met your eyes, then the secret I have carried in silence has finally been laid bare. You see me now not as the man who walked beside you, but as I truly am.

Long ago, the Archmage Mordenkainen saw fit to grant a simple quill the gift of flesh and sentience as a helper and apprentice to his work. When he departed, he left me behind to watch his tower and I have not seen him since... a living spell with no master. For years, in order to try to protect myself and prevent me from turning back and you now see, I have used every ounce of my abjuration capabilities to shield my own existence, terrified that a single stray pulse of magic would unravel the weave of my life. It seems that shield has finally failed.

I ask you now, not as a collection of wood and feather, but as the comrade who fought at your backs: **Please, do not let this be my end.**

Within my belongings is a vessel of **Leaded Amber**. I beg you to place my feather and my spellbook inside it immediately. The amber will insulate my essence, preserving the "memory" of my humanity and protecting the ink of my soul from the suffocating rot of Barovia. In that box, I am safe; outside of it, I am merely debris.

Please, carry me with you. When your journey leads you beyond these mists, seek out a wizard of true and peerless power—one capable of the highest arts of Transmutation. Tell them who I was. Tell them that Quill is not merely a tool to be used, but a soul that wishes to return.

I have spent my life protecting you with my wards; I must now ask you to protect me in the only way I have left.

Until the ink flows once more,

**Quill**
